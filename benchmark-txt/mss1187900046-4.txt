Jan[uary] 22 1874
Washington, D.C.

MY DEAR FATHER.

I have received your kind letter and was very glad to hear from you. but I am so sorry that you feal so. a bout going to Rochester. I hope it will be all Right thair. When you go thair. I shill take good care of your letters and not think of distroying them in anny way, we have had a few very cold days but we have very pleasant weather.
