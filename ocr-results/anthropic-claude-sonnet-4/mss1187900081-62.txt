Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Douglas Esq.
Peards.
This is Clipping
Copied from the Kansas City Times
of the 25th I enclose to you for
your verification or denial. Hundreds
of your Cold addresses, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow.
We have been induced to believe that
both the New York, and Indiana
Courts be fully relied upporn to
Cast their Electoral vote for Our
beloved Standard bearer. Harrison
And Morton. If reply, either
Confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige
Yours &c
A.L. Rosenberg
