Jany 29
                1874.
        Washington D.C

My Dear Father.
    I have received your
kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you. but I am so
sorry that you feel
so. a hurt going to
Rochester. I hope it will
be all Right thair.
when you go thair. I
shall take good care of your
letters and not think of
destroying them in any way.
we have had a fair every
Color days but we have
very pleasant weather.
                   2940
