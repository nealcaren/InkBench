THE EAGLE MOUNTAIN HOUSE
WHITE MOUNTAINS - JACKSON, NEW HAMPSHIRE

Aug 9th 1949

Dear Cousin Alice :
    When Ethel went
to Huntington I went out to
spend the day. Now it
was about two weeks after she
had gone. She had failed
steadily. I spoke to Horace
& said that perhaps I had
better write you for I then
realized that she had
cancer & that she could
not get well. Horace
said that he had told
