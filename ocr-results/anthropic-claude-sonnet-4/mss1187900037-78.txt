Westfield May 2d 1868

My dear Letitia
                The kind favor came
in due time - for which all thanks -
Its suggestions will doubtless of service
and its kind sayings mark grateful
to both of us - Tom will stay where
he is - at school, with an increase
in his Salary of $200. and has also
engaged to teach 6 or ten at the
Methodist College, when this loss of
compensation will, he more liberal
than he has had before - and in
addition to this, he is determined
to try at Public Reading, the next
winter - and can furnish them
with a book of the different Scenes
& - where he might be able to
make engagements I & can back
