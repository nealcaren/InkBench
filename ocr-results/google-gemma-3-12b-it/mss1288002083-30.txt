The Smith sisters of Glastonbury have lately parted with a share of bank stock and two of their pet Attorneys, rather than pay their taxes in the regular way, in our present depreciated currency. But the Worters, Foster, Stephen & and Abby Kelly, who have been & suffered… The Worters often shown, rests upon the entirely mistaken notion that taxation is connected with the right of suffrage or any way dependent on it. The world was taxed six thousand years before suffrage was invented, and the citizens of countries where suffrage does not exist are taxed to day without a thought of protest. Ministers are taxed, non-residents are taxed, and universally, in the theory and practice of civilized and uncivilized governments, taxation is recognized as the fair contribution of the citizen to the expenses of the government whose protection and conveniences he enjoys. Not to pay taxes is a fraudulent evasion of a fair debt.
