1601 Mt. Vernon Street,
Philadelphia. Oct. 23rd '95

Dear Success
My Dear Sa. I saw by the “Intelligencer”
you were way in Macedon. Thought I
had once mistaken it. I saw it as Handit
last night. I need it very heartily.  He
was much pleased with his visit with me
in pronouncing it Ollie, Jan Howard
do not good. -

Yours truly, M.S. Sherwood
