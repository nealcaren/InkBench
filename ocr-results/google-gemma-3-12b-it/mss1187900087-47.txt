“We shall have on the 19th a cable on “Northern forces” via Havana, a cable on “20th via Cuba and a maid again on the 25th “and”- I continue to believe that the “things will continue for 24 months.

“If the Government of Cleveland and Hasselt-“down had acted in good faith in their relations “with Douglas (I think), this war would not have “lasted….” I ask myself, if it would work “be well for me to go to Port an Prince with “Mr. Bougler to see if the two parties contending “ing consent to arrive at an honorable understanding”.

Now, my dear Douglas, this letter is already so “long that I must cut it short right here. 

My prayerly and very cordially yours, 
Mrs. Bassett, as totty. E.B. Basset.
