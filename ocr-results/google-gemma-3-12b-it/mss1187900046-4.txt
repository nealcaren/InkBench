Jan. 22 ‘74.
Washington D.C.

My Dear Father.

I have received your
kind letter and ours.
Every place to hear
from you. but I am so
sorry that you feel
so a bout going to
Rochester, I hope it will
be all right there.
When you go there. I
shall take good care of your
color. “Boys” but we have
more pleasant weather.

[*Stamp*] 20
