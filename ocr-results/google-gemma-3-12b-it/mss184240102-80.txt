Friday Mar 8
My dear friend,
I sent W.K. because I
suspected you were to
be there at first, but thine
list I could not ask news
from as. I was sick
for two weeks & I was very
much disappointed to
with so glad you & Mother
enjoyed yourselves so well.
& did take all care first-
self for what future
hopes. But now, hereafter be
sure if it was one long summer
