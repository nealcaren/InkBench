Rochester Oct 14-94

My Dear Brother Frederick.

This is a prime crisp
cool morning, once while
the girls are taking their
last nap I will pen a line
to you. An hour or two
really quiet time & then
the rest of the day is so
full of work and duties.
We are all settled down to
our every day life. Each day
has its own you know,
and what with getting
things going, to move and keep
things going here I find

1741
