No. 61. Monkey Temple. One hundred monkeys live in the trees surrounding the temple. Only one got in the picture. The other monkeys snatch away their babies when they think they have had enough, and run up the trees with them.



No. 62. The burning ghat of the Hindus on shore of Ganges. The body on the frame is lying foot in the holy water of the river, exposed briefly. About six or seven men are bathing, washing, and rubbing fingers, legs or arms into the river.
