13. Private DOMINIQUE HILBERT, Company B, 4th Missouri Cavalry.
CHARGE — “Conduct to the prejudice to Good Order and Military Discipline."

SPECIFICATION — In this: that Dominique Hilbert, Private of Company B, 4th Missouri Cavalry Volunteers, did visit the house of Michael Long, citizen, residing near Nashville Station, on the New Orleans and Carrollton Railroad, and did abuse and threaten Mrs. Long, the wife of said Michael Long, citizen, in the following manner: "pointing a revolver at her, saying at the same time, 'I will murder you, Station, in the Parish of Jefferson, Louisiana, on or about the 7th day of August, 1865."

PLEA - Not Guilty.

FINDING - Not Guilty.

And the Court does therefore acquit the accused.
