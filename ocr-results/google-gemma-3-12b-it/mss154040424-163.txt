Sir, years; six years to observe the infirmities of American politics, you have had, women of America. Will you allow any power whatsoever to steal from you the significance of the vote you struggled so long to win? My it is the proposal of rebellion. 

No wrong was ever righted by obedience to those who have sponsored that wrong. Wrong was ever righted by straining against those who stand for that wrong. Think it over, pray, think again. Keep on thinking. You will find a way. When you have it, arise, put aside your ease, your soft indulgences. There is work for you. The primaries need reforming, the parties need renovating and all politics need disinfecting. Is it true that you women have no influence as standardbearers? Blaze a way the path of rebellion and blaze a new trail to decency and honor for your party and your nation. You can have influence if you will. Yes, a hundred fold more influence than the Pennsylvania statesman has now.

But you can only win through rebellion. The times call for the fighting spirit of the Magna Charts and the Declaration of Independence. You have influence whenever you are ready to fight for it. Then that time comes, you women will not fight alone. There will be plenty of splendor, big-souled American men to fight by your side. Do not forget that there are many men who bore the humiliation of being so-called rubber stamps before you.
