-OFFICE OF-

❖C. B. BARNES❖
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Cuthbert, Ga.
1890

Printer Given Burrows
5th inst. Answer, I wish you
would send me one of your
hand bills, Burrows, this Jower
& wish you to ole me a great deal
of money this year for the
Republican Cause, and is now
friendly in debt for the same.
And the Republican County
Committee have sent a resolution
to Mrs. Barnamaler P. P. Gen
(Cuthbert Ga) pointing at
taking Thaddeus Canton to the
Postoffice at this place
[illegible] & Ga) this will help
us to secure my nome for
the money spent for the party
