Bailey, Sena. Nov. 4th, 1891
Hon. Frederick Douglass

We have a secret movement foot here
to move three thousand families out
West. We are trying making every
effort to assist the money. In the
name of God we call upon you assist
us + set your friends to assist. How
this wave comes directly from the
cabins of the poor oppressed downtrodden
northern people request to help us we are
dead men without a hope. All we want
is to set away from our oppressors +
than we can Educate our children.
We have several writers addressing
friends + we ask you to do the same in
behalf of poor suffering humanity
for reference of writers addresses Hon.
C.B. Eaton Minneapolis, + Miss Bailey Ln
Indly Yours.
C.H.N Coombs Sec.
