-OFFICE OF-

❖C. B. BARNES,❖
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.
Catholt, Ga. 189
Printer Gibson, Gun, Bimes
5th the Cover, is wish you
would hand over one of your
Bond men, Cousins, this Joun
is sick you to see a great amount
of money this year for the
Republican cause, and is now
busily in debt for the same.
And this Republican County
Committee has sent a resolution
to Hon. Barnamore Petty, Gen.
(Catholt & Ga.) who will help
me to secure my money for
the Republican Party.
