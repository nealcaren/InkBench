June 21, 1858.

My dear daughter. - Miss Mack came yesterday and brought the box and she said with me awhile. Trip up and working at weeds in the back and to talk we were very glad night. It helps Parker, feel well as we are doing very well at present. Brother well & getting are quite often now & he can’t come so busy out is still work and won’t hope you are well for you come home. Be suppose some of the children will be over to stay, much love from Mother.
