My dear Steady:
I cannot think I could possibly convey to you the very great pleasure and satisfaction your graphic letter gave and that you tried and you told it clearly that it was like a conversation with you.

The Christmases card came and was loving up on the post. My bed fellows were there. They tell us that travel in the tropics must nearly immobilise us, but I thought myself quite immune as I had not had such a thing for thirty years, but I got one nevertheless for three weeks.
