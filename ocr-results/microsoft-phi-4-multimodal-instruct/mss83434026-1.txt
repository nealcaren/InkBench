[ORIGINAL RECEIPT.]

CLAIM No. 38. 1864

COLUMBIA, S. C.,

Received of the State of South Carolina Eighteen hundreds 
(1800) dollars. 

being the amount admitted and advanced by the General Assembly of said State, upon the 
claim of  Jesse Briggs , for compensation for his slave, 
named Charles , lost by reason of the employment of said slave by 
the authorities of the Confederate Government upon the military fortifications in this State.
