Secret Service has at last been able to show that the war of our “Rebellion” was mitigated by this T. & that again they had so slandered us in the great Nations of Europe that one nearly blind another the doors of the old would were nearly closed to us - as a market. Hence our long years of stringency, a strike which victimised Christian Capitalists dare not explain to the people - England & Germany the two great Protestant nations had been made to believe in a great American conspiracy - to abduce them & we have come very near - a 2 Com not come it. It’s to terrible in view of the blood of ever nation in our veins - & the spirit of bitterness and strife in our own minds - with this 7. damnable issue almost waiting to occupy again, a million dead men’s bones, on the land, as they did, both North & South after they had brought a family war - so to speak, which brother to brother, met in mortal to the extent of almost a nations death. Suffice it the truth had been reached when about 3 years ago - Sir Edwin-
- Arnold
