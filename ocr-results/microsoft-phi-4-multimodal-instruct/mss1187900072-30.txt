Washington April 10th 1887
Dear Father

Your letter dated March 24th I received yesterday; it found us all well; and I pleased to hear from you.

Mr. Trotter, the new Recorder of Deeds is nervously sick with pneumonia. Gerrit S. Bogan has been appointed Deputy Recorder.

The Lawyers are much put about it. Senator Sherman is making a trip South. in Georgia, a colored delegation called on Bishop Walker from Kansas has been appointed to the Siberian Frissim.
