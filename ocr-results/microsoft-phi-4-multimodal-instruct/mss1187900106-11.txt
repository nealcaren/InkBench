Franklin, Wash., Aug 9, 1892.

J. S. BLAKENY
1610, 5th Ave, Spokane, Washington,

Dear Douglas: Here I am, away up in the
North West, on my first visit to my great
daughter, Adelaide, the wife of R. B. Scott, who
happens to be released from discharge
of his official duties, at "Sooyous Lake," in the
"Kanagan Country," as they Call it here. For
2 years and a half, until two months ago,
almost a perfect blank in my life's journey.
To be blind, is a terrible Calamity to any human
being. Since the surgical operation, two months
ago, which extracted the Cataract from my
left eye, and then waiting for the senses and
Sensitiveness to light to subside two weeks ago
for Sooyous and for Science. Thanks to Providence, his
mean discovery and skill for my holding out so long. Nature has
