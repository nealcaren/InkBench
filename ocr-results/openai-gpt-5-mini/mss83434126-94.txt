1828 May 13th Tariff Bill imposing heavy duties on British goods

1829 Feby 27th Battle of Tarqui in which the Peruvians are defeated by the Colombians

1829 March 4th Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun Vice President.

1829 Sept 11th A Spanish expedition for the recovery of Mexico sailed from Havana, July 5th - it surrendered to the Mexican Genl. Santa Anna.

1830 May 7th A treaty was effected between the United States and the Ottoman Porte - the ports of the United States were again opened to British commerce.

Dec 17th General Bolivar of South America and the liberator of Bolivia from the rule of Spain died aged 47 years.

1831 April 6th Abdication of Pedro I. emperor of Brazil in favor of his infant son Don Pedro.

Nov. 17th Venezuela, New Granada, and Colombia, So. America again became separate states.
