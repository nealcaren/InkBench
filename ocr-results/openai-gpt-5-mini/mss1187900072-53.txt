Charlotte writes that she received an invitation from Mrs. James (Maria Mitchell's niece), to attend an Alumnae gathering in Washington, before which Stephen Foster's daughter Allie, had been invited to read a paper.  Charlotte listened to her address with absorbing interest for her parents' sake as well as for her own — and said it was an uphandive description of the government of all the Colleges that are worthy of the name — forty eight in twenty one States.  Miss Foster is a teacher in the Girls High School in Boston (where my Cousin, (sister of the one who died, spoken of above) — also teacher.)
