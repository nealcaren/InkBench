On or before the 15th day of January
1866 we promise to pay Jane E Stearing
her heirs or assigns the just and full
sum of four hundred dollars in
current funds.- it being for the hire
of a negro slave named Sumner which
negro is to be clothed in the usual man-
ner of hirelings with the exception of
a blanket (It is understood by the
parties that should the said negro servant
be taken either by the enemy or the
Confederate authorities, the amt of the
time lost shall be deducted from the
hire.) For the true payment of which
sum we bind ourselves jointly & severally
our joint & several heirs, witness
our hands & seals this 4th day of Jany
1865 - H.H.P. Williams  Seal
