Hd Qrs 8th Regt. U.S.C.T.
Near Chaplins Farm Va
January 17th 1865

Respectfully forwarded.
I certify that the within ap-
plication will not make the privates
on leave or furlough from
this Regt in excess of one tenth
(10) of its present strength.
I do not belong to a one years Regt
that I have not returned from
a leave of absence since the 1st of
April 1864; that I have been in
the service more than one year;
that my term of service does
not expire before the 1st of May
1865; that I have never been
tried & convicted of any offence
by a court Martial; that I am
a member of a Regt raised
in the State of Pennsylvania.
The number of Officers present
in my Regt is 20
Number absent is 12
The number of enlisted men
present in my command is 538
Number absent is 256

R. P. Bailey
Capt. 8th U.S.C.T.
Comd'g Regt.
