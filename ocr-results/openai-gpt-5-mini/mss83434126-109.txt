1859 Jan 28th William Hicklin Prescott, American Historian died aged 65.

1859 Feb 2nd Genl Miguel Miramon, elected President of Mexico, by the revolutionary junta Jan 6th abdication of Zuloaga.

1859 April 11th Entrance of Genl Miramon into the City of Mexico - he assumes the presidency and governs without respect to law, life or property.

1859 July 15th Juarez, constitutional President of Mexico, confiscates the ecclesiastical property of his country.

1859 Oct 23d James G. [?] United States Minister to France died at Paris.

1859 Oct 17th John Brown with twenty one companions crossed the Potomac River at Harper's Ferry and entered Virginia, where he and his followers incited the slaves to rebel, and to take up arms against their masters; after a short time Brown was captured, tried and found guilty of Treason, and conspiring and advising with slaves and others to rebel, and murder in the first degree - for which he was executed on Friday 2nd day of Dec, at Charlestown.
