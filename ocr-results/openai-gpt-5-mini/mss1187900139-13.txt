Honorable
James G Blaine

Sir: I have the honor to ask the favor of a leave of absence from Port au Prince, beginning on the 20th of June, excluding the time required to reach Washington and the return to again reach Port au Prince, if the latter shall be deemed my duty.

I am required to state in this request for leave of absence, the the number of days I was absent from my post, during the preceding year.
I respectfully comply with this instruction or rule, by referring you to my Dispatch, No. 95 dated Dec: 18th 1890 in which it is stated: "I left Port au Prince, on the 19th July and on the 26th of the same month, arrived at my home in the District of Columbia. On the 3d Octr
