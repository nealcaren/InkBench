March 30th 1866 - Issued to Bvt. Major A. H. Buffington
then Commanding U. S. Arsenal at Baton Rouge, La.
the following Ordnance Stores, as per Inventory and
Inspection Report, of Bvt. Major Genl. T. W. Sherman
dated March 28th 1866.

8. Eight Springfield Rifled Muskets Cal 58.
7. Seven Cartridge Boxes Cal. 58.
7. Seven Bayonet Scabbards.
8. Eight Gun Slings
9. Nine Screw drivers and some wrenches
10. Ten Uplins [?]
3. Three Tumblers and hand spring punches
5. Five Waist Belts (Pots)

                          Unserviceable

Sent the invoices through the Post-Office
Dept. and received my receipts April 12th bearing date
April 4th 1866.

                               Stephen S. Newman
                               1st Lieut. Co. F. 81st U. S. Col. Inf.
