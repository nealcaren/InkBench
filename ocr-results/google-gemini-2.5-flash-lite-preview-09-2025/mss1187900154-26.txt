Chicago, Ill. U.S.A. June 1st.

My dear Mr. Damplass:
I leave for the East on E
tomorrow morning & now I have just offered
my calling on you & Mrs. Damplass that
I shall be unable to do so. Do make my
excuses to your first wife for me. I remember
her with so much pleasure in Paris. I hope
to turn to Chicago later in the summer &
Then I will not fail to see you both. &
the meanwhile believe me,

Very truly Jny,
[Indecipherable writing that looks like a signature] Stanton,
26 West 61st St.
New York.
