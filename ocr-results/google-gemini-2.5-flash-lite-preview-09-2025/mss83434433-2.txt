Dear Sir

I have thought of an expedient to bring
in Caroline which I submit to you. It is this Genl
Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mississippie
Now, if you could disperse with Janeet & see Gene all
& let her go to stay, his negro’s & get it out that he has bought
her. it would I am sure bring her in & let it be out
that it is an arrangement of mere will which you have
no control: What do you think of it? Let me know

Yr truly
Allan O'Neile
John H. Lewis.
