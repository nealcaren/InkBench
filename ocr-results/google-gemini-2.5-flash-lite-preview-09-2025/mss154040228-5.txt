Dictated 11-20-'31

My Dear Mrs. Catt: For six in
the General Hospital, Osteopathic
Unit #780 - at Los Angeles, after been
present with a fractured leg.
I have been here 8 weeks today.
Hope soon to be released from
this terrible home of torture, a
cast, so that I can return
to my little apartment at
609 Fairview Ave., So. Pasadena
Calif. Many friends, wonderful
to me in many ways.
Address my close
friend Mrs! Lois Jenkin, 1918
Oak St., So Pasadena - who has
all of me in charge -
Cordially
Henrietta G Moore
& 7g
