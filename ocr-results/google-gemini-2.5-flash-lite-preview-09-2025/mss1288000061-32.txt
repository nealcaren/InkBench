Georgeville, P. Q. S.B. Aug. 20th 189/
Papa & Mamma:
Yesterday we went on a pleasant picnic to
Fitch Bay. It was an all-day trip. We had an early breakfast, & then the Holbrooks came over, & we all piled into a little steam-launch, chartered for the occasion. It carries 25, & there were 25 of us. The engine divides it in the middle, with wood heaped up on each side (fancy a steam engine run by wood!) & at the back

too. Bessie is his favorite, but she is more than Sadie. I am well.
Sorry this is not to. It is a pretty, as a romp.
