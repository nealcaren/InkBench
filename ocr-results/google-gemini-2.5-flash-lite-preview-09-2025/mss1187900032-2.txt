Supper we all went to the South
to skate and skated until half past
eight when I came in and
Sam sitting down by [?] after
his horse drive all here content.
me for not bringing my ska
with me and if it is convenient
I wish that you would have them
sent with my other things. I must
close this letter short as it is almost
bedtime and we must be up
at five o'clock. I should like to
hear from home once in a while
and you shall hear from me
I know this is not as good
writing as you would like
but I am in haste to go to
bed.

From your affectionate
Son Charles R. Douglass
