New York, Feb 21st 1879

My Dear Mrs Dickinson
I am afraid your business
affairs are again suffering. You have
not the party call as it seems to come
much with. [3] as soon as invited
but "Can't Keep a Hotel" I am sorry, I
think has inspired your "boss"
with [?] it might have
been more in a different position -

You lotd me you would see me about
your Theatrical business the next I hear
you have "a Boy" (as it were) dotting
with your affairs & "mixing a[?] Children
up" I thought you had a dear experience
before with Analem Manages & had
learned by experience the value of management.
But thate I am scolding you only that I
feel slighted. I can do more for you than any
man in this business - or out of it - for that
matter I owed (or view) him done it cheerfully
