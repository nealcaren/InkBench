was sought by the Director of Howe Institute
mentioned in annexed paper. - To tell me he had
proof that "Philanthropic money" as he called it could
be found in the North (in fact had been offered)
at low interest for such a nick work as I had hinted
to which he added the suggestion mentioned
- and attracted my attention to Part 4, Page 4 of the
Chicago
Daily Inter-Ocean of 25 March giving an account

your [&] - It is a number of 62 pages printed at

Fair. When upon this I heard of your work
