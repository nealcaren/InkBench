Mch 26. 1923.

My dear Mollie:- I gave you this peace for a cable and none is here. She remains another day but I suspect that progress in the direction of recovery and that you have not cabled at all.
Mch 27 - I was interrupted here and now send all things are different and time has passed your cable has been received, and it was such a relief to get it. I imagine you passing through that discouraging stage when you think you never will be well again and when life has lost all its fervor. Well, keep up your courage and you'll pull through. I am so glad you are better. This would make be a black old place without you dear Mollie.
