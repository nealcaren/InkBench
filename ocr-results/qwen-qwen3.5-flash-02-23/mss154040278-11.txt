Directions for Chicken
Scrape off the jelly or juice.
Take a table spoon full of flour, add salt, pepper to taste.
Stir well and drop chicken in this mix and def chicken in
a nother iron pan. Have a skillet hot; melt butter or
bacon fat in it and quickly
fry the chicken. Remove
the chicken, pour the juice
or jelly into the pan. If you
like to add a bit of cream and
thicken it with a little flour.
