DISCHARGE.

This is to certify, That No. 503. Spenced Barr a Landsman
has this day been discharged from the U. S. Ship "Bilwille"
and from the naval service.

Dated this 14th May, 1865.

APPROVED: Jno. P. Coghlan
Captain Command.

Wm Wallace Gordon
Paymaster.
