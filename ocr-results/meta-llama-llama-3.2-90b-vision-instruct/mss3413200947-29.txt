THE EAGLE MOUNTAIN HOUSE
WHITE MOUNTAINS - JACKSON, NEW HAMPSHIRE

June 9th 1947

Dear Cousin Alice:

When Ettie went to Harts Location I went out to spend the day - now it was about two weeks after she had gone.  She had failed steadily - I spoke to Morse & told that perhaps I had better write you for I?  Then Morse realized that she had cancer & that she could not get well - I said that he had told me that he would
