Georgville, Pa.

Dear Papa & Mama:
Yesterday we went on a pleasant picnic 
to Title Bay.  It was an all-day trip.  We had 
an early breakfast, & then 
Holbrooks came over, & 
we all piled into a little
steam-launch chartered 
for the occasion.  It 
carries 25, & there were 25 
of us.  The engine divides 
it in the middle, with 
wood heaped up on each 
side (fancy a steam engine 
run by wood!)  At the back 
Jessie is his favorite, but she is more 
sociable.  So we all 
enjoy it well.
[*scratched out text*] 
And Andy has lots of 
good things to say 
about you & your 
pleasant letter - 
You didn't have to 
bother about the 
other note, - it was 
not worth it.
[*scratched out text*] 
You will 
have a nice long 
visit when we 
come home - 
that is a comfort 
to think of.
