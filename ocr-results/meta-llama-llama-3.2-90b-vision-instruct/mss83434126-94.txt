1828 May 16th Tariff Bill imposing heavy duties on British goods

1829 Feb 24th Battle of Gurgui in which the Persians are defeated by the Russians

1829 March 4th Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun this President

1829 Sept 16th A Spanish expedition for the recovery of Mexico raised from Havana, July 5th. A summons to the American General Santa Anna.

1830 Dec 17th Twenty six articles exhibited between the United States and the Ottoman Porte at the ports of the United States are again opened to British commerce

1830 Dec 17th Union of Bolivia, South America and the liberation of Bolivia from the rule of Spain which had existed 13 years

1831 April 6th Abdication of Agustin emperor of Brazil in favor of his infant son Don Pedro

1831 Nov 17th Venezuela, New Granada and Columbia So America again became separate States
