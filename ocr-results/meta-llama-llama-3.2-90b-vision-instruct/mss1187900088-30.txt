FIRST ANNUAL ASSEMBLY, LAKE JACKSON, LEON COUNTY, FLORIDA.

Columbia, Tenn.
Nov 5th 1879.

Hon. Frederick Douglass,
My honored Sir—

We respectfully call your attention to our letter here and also take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful prospects this move is making, both for its immediate and lasting well of those of our race have been conferred with, and up to date, out of the many proposals received, not one has withheld endorsement and active cooperation.

The plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in Florida that being near the center of the “Gathtaginian Belt.”

At first the Chautauqua is to be literary extra place of meeting.  The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the former New York Chautauqua.
