I came to Boston on Nov. 8th
to make a short visit before winter, & 
I am still here now waiting for winter
to be gone.  I have lived very quietly, only
been to the opera a few times.  I have
seen none of the celebrities, they were
too costly.  My heart is not broken, else
it would have burst long since.  I thought I should
see you here this spring, because the Knapps
said so, It has been a trying winter,
but Spring & violets are near the door. 
Agnes worries me often, & is happy if
her bit of house-work will do instead.
But if she is shut in, she shuts herself 
in "closet duties".  She frolics so 
she has a good-practical mean, & 
kept head from amidst unheard things.  I ain't
easy.  Love blots you dear- always.

Lovingly- 
Mama Lucy.
