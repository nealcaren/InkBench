11-20-'36

Dictated

My Dear Mrs. Catt:

I'm in the General Hospital, Osteopathic
Unit #780 - at Los Angeles, after being
present with a fractured leg.
I have been here 8 weeks today.
I hope soon to be released from
this terrible home of torture, a
cast, so that I can return
to my little apartment at
609 Florence Ave., Pasadena
Calif.  Many friends are wonderful
to me in my ways.

I address my close
friend Mrs. Lois Fiske, 1918
Oak St., So Pasadena - who has
been of great help to me in charge.

Cordially
Henrietta G. Moore

L.G.
