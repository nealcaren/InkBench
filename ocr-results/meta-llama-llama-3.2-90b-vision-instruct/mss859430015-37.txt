June 21, 1958.

my dear daughter. - miss mack came yesterday and brought the box and visited with me awhile. she said she had a nice trip up and working at home and clearing away the likes in the back and the like. we were very glad to talk with you Sunday - better. It helps Parke, feel well are doing very well at present. Brother & family are quite well. he doesn't often now. is still work - ing out at Jackson, and they are getting work help. I hope you are well and won't over work before you comes home. now that school is out I suppose some of the children will be over to stay, much love from mother.
