1862  June 17th evacuation of Cumberland Gap, Tenn.  The rebels
and the Genl Morgan, advanced in force for the defence
of expelling the rebel Genl. Stevenson 2d. Smith who was camped
this gap with 13000 men and were fortified the same,  This
gap is a chasm in the Cumberland mountains, and is so high
and narrow that there is through the gap towards
way of escape, It was expected that the rebels would defend
its possession with the utmost tenacity.  So much was
foreboded,  When the rebels were informed of the approach
of the federals, they precipitately evacuated the place,
