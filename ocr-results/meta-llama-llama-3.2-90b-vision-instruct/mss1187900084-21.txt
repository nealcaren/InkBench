Mary Department
Bureau of Engraving & Printing
April 22, 1889.

New Bedford, Mass. U.S.

I have the honor to forward to
you the published action of the Ebenezer
Lyceum as deserved, and ask you
acceptance of the same as token of their
and respect for your great leaders.

[Newspaper clipping titled "For the Good of the Whole People." Details a meeting of the Ebenezer Lyceum where a series of resolutions are adopted in appreciation of Frederick Douglass, including a tender of thanks for his timely address and pledge of support.]

Respectfully,
C.S. Atkinson, Sec.

[The number "712" is written below the signature.]
