No. 955.

Kansas City, Fort Scott & Memphis Railroad Co.
Warrion Arkansas
September 21st 1885

Hon Frederick Douglass
Washington D.C.
Dear Sir

I am an Ex Union Soldier and have endeavored to be a cool - radical Republican ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the civil war and in my humble sphere in life have been a friend to the Colored race. It certainly seems to me that the current of absolute Oliver is still not upon this Country and hears me rely on the colored race. I reside at present in '86
