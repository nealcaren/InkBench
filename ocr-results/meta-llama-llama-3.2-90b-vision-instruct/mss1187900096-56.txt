Nantucket, July 18, 1891.

My dear friends;—

We, Charlotte and I, congratulate you upon your safe arrival back to Nantucket. Charlotte is sojourning in Nantucket for a few weeks. Mr. Mann Re-mains at New Haven. Mrs. Mo-comes frequently to pass the day with me and I return the visit. She suggested my writing to you. Writing has become quite burdensome. It is so feeble - seemingly dwindling down!
