dear Sir

I have thought of an expediant to bring
in Cavalry which I submit to you. It is this Gen.
Mobley is mustering his force to leave for Manassas.
Runley you could dispense with Janes & see Gene de
Lattre go & slay his negro & get it out that he has bought
her & now I am sure bring him in so let be out
that it is an arrangement of mene over which you have
no control.  What do you think of it?  Let me know

Yours truly
John A. Lewis
All. 8 Reille
