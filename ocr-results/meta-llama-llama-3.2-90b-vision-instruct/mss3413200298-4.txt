MRS. MARY WARE DENNETT
51 SINGER ST.
ASTORIA, LONG ISLAND
NEW YORK CITY

May 21
1935

Dear Mrs. Brown;

I can't let your note about
Mrs. Blatchford for unmeasured, even
for Check I mailed it to send
must be omitted.

I have always admired Mrs. Blatchford,
and, in late years, have loved her
especially. Her always beautiful Chris-
tian character and Barrett messages, and her
inimitable spiritual grace are such
lovely additions to the great back-
ground of her life work,

It wrings my heart to know that-
she, too, is facing a financial vacuum.
There are so many who'd be
of them myself, or appointe I would
share in making the fund which you
are raising. Being a stammering artist
is a most painful handicap,
in every way, but in in particularly
hard to endure, when appears like
that comes.

Thanks for including me
as one of her friends, - which
I most heartily am.

Sincerely,
Mary Ware Dennett.
