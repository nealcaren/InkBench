Jan 22 1874.

Washington D.C.

My Dear father.

I had received your kind letter and was glad to hear from you, but I am so sorry that you feel so. a bout going to Rochester, I hope it will be all right their. When you go their. I shall take good care of your letters and not think of destroying them in any way, one have had a few very cold days but here has been very pleasant weather.

Geo 40
