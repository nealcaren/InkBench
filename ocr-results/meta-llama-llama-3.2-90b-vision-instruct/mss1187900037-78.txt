Pencatille, Pa./ Aug 12. 1866

My dear friend

Your favor came in due time for which all thanks - 
Its suggestions were directly of service, and its kind sympathy most valuable 
to both of us.  You will stay where 
he is at school, with an increase 
in his salary of $200.  and has also 
engaged to teach Elocution at the 
Lutheran College, where his late of 
compensation will, he is liberally 
than he has had before - and in 
addition to this, he is determined 
to try at Public Reading the next 
winter - and can push him 
with a hitch of the Agricultural Lyceum 
- where he might be able to 
make engagements & I came back 
1937
