To the Speakers Bureau:
Oct 18 in office
Three roses more sweet, beautiful, tempers disheveling, but
Pansies and Verbenas.
Keep your money; I'd just as
Chorister.  Please don't dishonor roses again
I have a vile laryngitis, growing rife every day which
shows what follies and so for a woman so 
is a daily comfort to me; The Speakers Bureau!
New York City, October
1915
The Terror
