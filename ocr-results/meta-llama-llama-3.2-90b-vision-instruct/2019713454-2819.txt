BOUND VOLUMES OF OUR YOUNG FOLKS.
Volumes One and Two of Our Young Folks, comprising the Numbers for 1865 and 1866, have been bound uniformly in handsome cloth. Price of each volume, with plain edges, $2.50; with gilt edges, $3.00. Each contains about 800 pages, and nearly 200 illustrations. These elegant and admirable volumes will be sent, free of expense, to any part of the United States except Nevada and the Territories, on receipt of the price. Cloth covers will be furnished for 50 cents.

SPECIAL NOTICE.
Every letter on business relating to Our Young Folks should bear the name of the State as well as the Post-Office from which it comes.
Letters requesting a change in address of magazines should always state both the former and the desired address.  No change can be made after the 10th of any month in the address of magazines for the following month.
