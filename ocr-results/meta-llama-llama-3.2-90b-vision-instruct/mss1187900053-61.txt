Conegar Villa,
Bridport.
Jorset

May 16th 1849

Dear Mr Douglas

It is a very long time since we have written to you or heard from you, and it is now in hope of provoking a reply.  We really are very much in the dark about you, & do not even know whether you still hold office under the Government, as we have not been to the Contrary & conclude that you have continued holding & holding over the Admiralty as of old.  I hope that you could report will of yourself & of your family

2H08
