Boston, July 21, 1889

Dear Frederick Douglass

When we first saw your appointment to Hayti - and also has many kind and beautiful things the newspapers were saying about you began to clip and stick them with the intention of sending them all on to you - I cut "lots" of them. But alas! In the huge piles of clippings & scraps they here all disappeared but three

I enclose

It was pleasant to see the appreciation in which you are held while you are abroad to see it. 
I am not sure we are glad to have you so - We needed you here.
