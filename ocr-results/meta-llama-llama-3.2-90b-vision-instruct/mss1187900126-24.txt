Rochester Oct 14-94

My Dear Brother Frederick,

This is a fine crisp cold morning, and while
the girls are taking their
lust nap I will pen a line
to you.  An hour in the
morning is about the only
real quiet time I have,
since the rest of the day is
full of work and activities.
We are all settled down to
our every day life.  Each day
has its own you know,
and what with getting and keeping
things going here is Friday
1741
