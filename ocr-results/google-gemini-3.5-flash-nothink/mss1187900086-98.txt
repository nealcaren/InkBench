Massachusetts Woman Suffrage Association, 

3 PARK STREET. 

Boston, July 21 1889. 

Dear Fredrick Douglass
When we first saw your
appointment to Hayti - and also hear
many kind and beautiful things
the newspapers were saying about you
I began to clip and stick them with
the intention of sending them all on
to you - I cut "lots" of them - but alas!
in the huge piles of Editorial scraps
they have all disappeared but three
I Enclose -
It was pleasant to see the
appreciation in which you are held -
while you are alive to see it.
I am not sure that we are glad
to have you go - we need you
here.
