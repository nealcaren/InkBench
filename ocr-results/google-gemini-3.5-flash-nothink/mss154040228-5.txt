Dictated               11-20-'31
My dear Mrs. Catt:
Am in
the General Hospital, Osteopathic 
Unit #780 - at Los Angeles, at the 
present with a fractured leg.
Have been here 8 weeks today.
Hope soon to be released from
this terrible [home of torture] cast, so that I can return 
to my little apartment at
609 Fairview Ave., So. Pasadena
Calif. Many friends are wonderful 
to me in [ways] Address my close
friend Mrs. Lois Jenkins, 1918 
Oak St., So Pasadena - who has 
all [---] of me in charge.
           Cordialy
           Henrietta G Moore
