Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

F. Douglass Esq.
Dear Sir
The enclosed
clipees from the Kansas City Times
of the 25th I enclose to you for
your verification, or denyal, Hundreds 
of your Cold adnyrers, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow,
We have been induced to believe that
booth New York, and Indianna
Could be fully relied uppon to
Cast their electoral vote for Our
Gallant Standard bearer, Harrison
And Morton, A reply, either
Confirming or dennying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige
Yours &c
P.F. Seisenring [-]
