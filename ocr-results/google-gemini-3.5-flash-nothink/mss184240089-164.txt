if you will but keep
the eye of your own
spirit turned within - 
for it is [from] within
(not without) that [that] 
the precious jewel of 
truth & right is to 
be sought - & found - 
And darling - you
will find it, & speak
it, and live it - and
all men & women shall 
call you blessed - 

hastily but oh so dearly
