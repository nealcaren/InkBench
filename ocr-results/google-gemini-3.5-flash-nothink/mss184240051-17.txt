[* The Light in the Tunnel. *]
[* From the Virginia City Chronicle. *]
[* A new device for the bedevilment of lovers was introduced successfully by a heartless young man on the train south yesterday. A couple sat in the seat before him. The lady was young and modest, and the swain wore very large cuffs. One arm was thrown carelessly along the back of the seat, and upon this the evil-minded person behind rubbed some phosphorus. The affection-ate performances of that arm, when the train was rush-ing through the kindly darkness of the tunnels, were be-held by all the tittering and snickering occupants of the car, whose cruel enjoyment was not a little increased by the demure and unconcerned appearance of the lovers each time the train bounded again into light. *]
