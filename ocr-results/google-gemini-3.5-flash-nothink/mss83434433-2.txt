Dear Sir
I have thought of an expedient to bring
in Caroline which I submit to you. It is this, Genl
Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mississippi
Now if you could dispense with Jane & see Genl M
& let her go & stay, his negro's & get it out that he has bought

no control: What do you think of it.? Let me know.

Yrs truly
