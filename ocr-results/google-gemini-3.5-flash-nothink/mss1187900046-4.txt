Jan, 22
1874.
Washington D.C.
my Dear father.
I have received your
kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you, but I am so
sorry that you feel
so. about going to
Rochester, I hope it will
be all Right thair,
when you go thair. I
shill take good care of your
letters and not think of
distroying them in anny way,
we have had a few very
Cold days but we have
[crossed-out/illegible] pleasant weather.
