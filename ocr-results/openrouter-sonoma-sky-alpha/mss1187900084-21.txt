Mrs. Mary Vincent Lynch

Nov. 2d 1871

Mrs. Mary Lynch

I have the honor to forward to
you the publishers notice of the Phenixian
by Rev. N.[?] dictate, with your
request for the use of your White
meeting, after the reading of it. S. D.

Having read it through, I can
testify to every statement
it contains.  The Rev. Dr. Douglass has
written a very able & interesting
history of the Phenixian, in which he
has embodied the opinions of
many persons who have known
him from his boyhood up.  I
think it will be read with
interest by all who knew him
or the Phenixian.

Regretfully,
C. Ellsworth
