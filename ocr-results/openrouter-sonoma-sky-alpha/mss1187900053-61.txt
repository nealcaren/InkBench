Congersville Pa.
March 18th 1869

Mr Engle

Dear Mr Engle we have written to
you but have not heard from you
We are waiting a reply. We really are
much afraid we shall not get our
little office with the
furniture as we would like
to have it done. The Harness that your husband
over the quality plant would
go of. I hope that you report
well yourself your family
