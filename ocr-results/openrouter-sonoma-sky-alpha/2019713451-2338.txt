Principles of Seal Letters

Sail of Lenghts

Give special heed to these directions.

The No. is from the first page &c. The last
page shows the address, &c. The
Eng. in Ch. &c. has the key.

The Eng. in Ch. has the key to the cipher.

The first page of the letter is the subject of the letter in each case. The subject is always to be given in plain language.

The second page is to be written in cipher.

The cipher is to be written with a pen and ink, and is to be as legible as possible.

The third page is to be the signature and date.

The fourth page is to be left blank for the seal.
