The California Federation of Women's Clubs one
and seventy three thousand strong is the largest
voluntary non sectarian non political organization
of women in the state.  It was founded in 1896
and has since grown to its present size.  The
Federation has taken an active part in all civic
and educational movements in the state and has
been instrumental in the establishment of many
public libraries, playgrounds, and other community
facilities.  The four women shown above have been
presidents of the California Federation of Women's
Clubs.  Mrs. Los Angeles was president from 1914
to 1916, Mrs. San Francisco from 1920 to 1922,
Mrs. Sacramento from 1926 to 1928, and Mrs. San
Diego from 1932 to 1934.  These women have all
been active in the work of the Federation and
have contributed much to its success.

The four women shown above have been
