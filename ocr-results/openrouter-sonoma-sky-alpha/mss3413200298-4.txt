Dear Mrs Cornell

I came home last evening, and found
thy letter awaiting me.  I was grieved to
learn that thy daughter was not so well
as when I left her.  I have been anxious
about her ever since.  My heart is
with her in sympathy, & I trust she
will soon be restored to health again.
This course, from which I have just
returned, has been a source of much
instruction to me, & I am particularly
grateful for the opportunity of becoming
acquainted with thy son, & his wife.

I must tenderly say goodbye
Mary Jane Cornell
