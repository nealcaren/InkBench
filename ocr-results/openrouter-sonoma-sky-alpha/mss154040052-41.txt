Carrie Chapman Catt
Miss Alice Stone Blackwell
Roxbury Mass
Nov 9th 1904

Dear Miss Alice,

In this morning's paper I see that Mrs. [?] & her husband & her sister were both in the hospital.

Every time I see such an item I think of you & wish you were here to whom I could express my sympathy as I am doing to you now.

My sympathy is sincere, if there ever was one.

I am sorry she has gone to Boston when you

send the enclosed which I hope will interest you.

Lovingly,
Carrie Chapman Catt
