elf Augusta  
Oct 19'92  

Dear Grandfar-  
I have learned with  
much regret That you are still  
in the city much against your intention  
I am very glad  
That such a friend  
has granted [that you were granted]  
you and Enid that you were far  
happier no leas and then only works  
That matter could to advanced  
is all in you as remain in this  

92
