I was truly sorry to hear of the death of your dear sister  
Truly sorry my dear friend & wish it were in my power to  
offer you some consolation in your affliction. I have been thinking of you  
a great deal since I heard of your loss & have felt very much for you.  
Your sister was a very dear friend of mine & I loved her very much.  
She was always so kind & gentle & I shall miss her very much.  
I know how you must feel & my heart aches for you. May God  
comfort you in your sorrow & give you strength to bear it.  
Please give my love to your family & believe me  
Your affectionate friend
