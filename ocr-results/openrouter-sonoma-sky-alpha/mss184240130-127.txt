My dear Lizzie

I have just received your
letter & under the circumstances
many better things would
have been said but the
task is too much for me.
I can only say that I
am sorry for the death
of your Uncle who had
a heart of gold. His
death was not worthy
of such a good man.
I have no words good
with which to express
it. I would that I could
see you now & tell you
all I feel. You have
my deepest sympathy in
your great trouble & I
hope you will write me
soon.
