COOK COUNTY HOSPITAL

Dietary Management

Dear Mrs. Closkey:

December 8, 1940

I wish to thank you for your appreciation of the commemorative
gift of the book "History Lemons '19".

Therefore, I was glad to receive a copy on Tuesday.
Personally, our club has made efforts to serve in every way the
young girls who choose nursing as their vocation.  We are sorry
you could not be with us at the last meeting.

Very truly yours,

Millie B. Kalen

Chief Executive Dietitian
