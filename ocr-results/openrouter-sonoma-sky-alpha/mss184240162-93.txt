Camp near Falmouth Va
Dec 18th 1862

My dear Sal I take my pen in hand to let you know that I am well at this time hoping these few lines may find you the same. I have nothing of importance to write. The regiment is in good health at this time. The 1st N C Regt is guarding the last ford. They are doing nothing but standing picket.

I should like to inquire if you have received the letter I sent you last. If not I will send it again. It contained the money I got from the paymaster $50.00 which I hope you have received by this time.

The regiment is still in the same place. We have fine weather for the time of year.

Your affectionate husband until death
