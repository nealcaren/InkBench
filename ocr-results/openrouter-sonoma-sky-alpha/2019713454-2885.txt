Our Mother's Library

We have long desired to devote a department of our pages to the best books for mothers and their little ones; and now propose to do so. We shall select from time to time such books as we think will be of interest to our readers, and give a brief notice of them, with extracts. We shall be glad to receive suggestions from our friends.

The Nursery; and a Young Lady's Diary. Edited by Mrs. S. C. Hall. Harper & Brothers. We have long been acquainted with the writings of Mrs. Hall, and have always admired her pure and elevated style. This little volume is a reprint from the English, and is just the book for a mother to read to her children. It is full of good lessons, taught in the most attractive manner.

For the above mentioned works we shall be glad to receive orders, and will forward them to purchasers at the publishers' prices.
