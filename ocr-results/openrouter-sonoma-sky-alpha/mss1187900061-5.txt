Washington D.C.

July 18th 1883

Dear Grandchildren,

Your affectionate letter received

12th & we were very glad

to get it from you & know

that you are getting along

quietly passing along

doubtless having at this

most glorious of all

summer months a most

delightful time & I

Charles has not left yet:

for the cavalry service will

or don't think the father

Don't tear Sunday
