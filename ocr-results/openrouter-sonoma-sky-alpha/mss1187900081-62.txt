My dear Byrnes

I send you the little book you asked for. I hope you will like it, & that it will be of use to you. This little book said something about the subject you were speaking of, & I thought it might interest you.

I have been to see Dr. Holbrook. He thinks the matter will be settled before long. I hope so.

With much love,  
Jno. W.

[*Postmark: Ellis Oct 26 1908*]  
[*Stamp: 2¢*]  

Mrs Jno W Ellis  
1601 Spruce St  
Phila Pa  

Miss Byrnes  
[*Postmark: N.Y. Oct 26 10*]
