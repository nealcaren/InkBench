World's Columbian Commission
Department of Agriculture
Chicago Ill. May 24, 1893.

Hon. Frederick Douglass,
316 A St.,
Washington, D.C.

Dear Sir:

For a series of three honor talks limited to thirty minutes each, on Forestry, Agriculture, Live Stock, lectures, or subjects, to be given at the Exposition June 20th, in the Assembly Hall, built for this purpose in connection with the Agricultural Building.

It is our utmost desire for the best results from these talks that you will do the honors to be secured, and send to me the names of such members, and associates, or prominent men for this purpose.
