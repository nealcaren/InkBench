State of Pennsylvania, ss.

County of Schuylkill

An Inquest taken and held at the house of John Boyer in the
Township of North Manheim in and for the County of Schuylkill
on the 24th day of July A.D. 1848 before Anthony Miller Esqr.
one of the Coroners of said County upon view of the body of
John Boyer then and there lying dead upon the Oaths of John
Schappell, John Reed, Jacob Reed, Daniel Boyer, Benjamin
Schappell, and Jacob Fenstermacher good and lawful men of
the said County who being duly sworn and charged to inquire
on the part of the Commonwealth how and by what means
the said John Boyer came to his death do say that from the
testimony of divers witnesses and from a view of the body
that the said John Boyer came to his death by the hand of
God in a natural way and not otherwise.

In Testimony whereof the Jurors hereunto subscribe their names.

John Schappell
John Reed
Jacob Reed
Daniel Boyer
Benjamin Schappell
Jacob Fenstermacher

State of Pennsylvania, ss.

County of Schuylkill

I Anthony Miller Coroner as aforesaid do certify the above
Inquisition by me taken to be true and correct.

Witness my hand this 24th day of July A.D. 1848

Anthony Miller Coroner
