National American Woman Suffrage Association  
17 Madison Avenue, New York  
April 8th 1906  

Mr. Blaisdell,  

I pondered over your letter & cannot  
see my part in it. If I thought it would  
be of any use I would try & feel  
the pulse of things here. A few letters more and give a hint  
which Mrs Anthony could do if you felt  
worth while about it yourself & there are no  
reports in newspapers at  
all. These times we are greatly handicapped by not having to tell you the  
full truth. I am very sorry to cover it. A version  
of it. If you hear of anything impressive to yourself  
on the question by which I hope you will let me know. If it  
be ever so greatly to me. Yours sincerely,  

Carrie Chapman Catt.
