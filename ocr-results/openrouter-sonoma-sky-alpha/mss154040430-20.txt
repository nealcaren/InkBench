In this delicious air & sun in spring thro'
our walks tho' keeping from the hot sun which
opens so freely on the South, I feel
as if the flowers of the past were blooming
with the present - & that the same
gentle hands which open the buds of
Spring, will also unfold the treasures of
memory, & that the same voice which
calls up the flowers of the present year
will also waken those which have
slept since the last - & I can
almost fancy that I hear the voice
of my dear Mother calling me to
enjoy the beauties of the Spring
with her - & I seem to see her
delighted with the flowers & birds
as in the days of my childhood.
This feeling of the presence
of the departed is very sweet
to me - & I trust it is a
foretaste of that reunion
which awaits us all -
