Marble Granite Statuary

Mrs. Frederick Robeson. Nyack N.Y. Sept 10 18--

Dear Madam

Our Mr. Illi Annapolis, for the order you so kindly gave us.

Aug. 31st The contract mentioned in the letters, we can have printed

in our office. The invoices are a form of contract, and if selected

decide upon designs or we will send them to you, and will

send them and return one marked ok. with two heavy leaves

used in frames, and this should be cut better than the other so

that

The rest can be if this meets with your approval, and return

then all we can, and will then place this order in your hands,

and we shall take pride in executing it in the finest way, and

hoping to hear from you, respectfully,

J. Weston
