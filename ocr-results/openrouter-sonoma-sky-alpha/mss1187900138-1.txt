To Mrs. Francis McGivens of
Weymouth Mass. Greetings in the Joy
of this Glorious Day. This Morning's Newspaper
brought down to me says In Plainest
Words that today In which the Bells will ring out
for Joy throughout the land and in the
White House his guests will be full of Joy & Peace
from the great event. Its review there
Our grateful hearts are made to bound
with Joy. Our own and constant friend
The World is gazing on this sight. With this note
with care Send my congratulations then.
