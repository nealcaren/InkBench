and many thanks for thy
kind letter which came
yesterday.  I was glad to
hear from thee, & that
thou hadst so much enjoyed
thy visit to Phila.  I
had come from there myself
just before thee, & had
left my letter with Cousin
Mary for thee.  I hope
thee has got it by this
time.  I am glad to hear
that the children are
well, & that thee is
improving in health.
I remain, thy affectionate
sister,
M. E. Cope
