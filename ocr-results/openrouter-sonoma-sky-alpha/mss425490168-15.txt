PULPIT COMMITTEE Feb. Thursday, March 1st, 1962

I met with Dr. Court on Friday morning, Feb. 2nd, 1962

In the office of Dr. Thomas, 1701 16th St. N.W., at 10:30 A.M.

Present: Dr. Thomas, Rev. Dr. Arthur L. Smith, Rev. Dr. E. L. Harrison, Rev. W. L. Carter, Mrs. Mary L. Smith, Mrs. E. L. Harrison.

The matter under discussion was the assignment of Rev. Dr. Arthur L. Smith to the Metropolitan Baptist Church.

Dr. Smith was advised to make application to the Pulpit Committee of the Metropolitan Baptist Church for the pastorate.

It was pointed out to Dr. Smith that the Metropolitan Baptist Church is in the E. L. Harrison District, and that the recommendation of the District Leader must be obtained before the Pulpit Committee of the Church can act.

Dr. Smith was advised to secure the recommendation of Dr. E. L. Harrison, the District Leader, and to file this recommendation with the Pulpit Committee of the Church.

The Pulpit Committee of the Church will then file the application with the General Board for approval.

Dr. Thomas stated that the Board has approved the recommendation of Dr. E. L. Harrison for the assignment of Dr. Arthur L. Smith to the Metropolitan Baptist Church.

Rev. Dr. E. L. Harrison stated that he had recommended Dr. Smith to the Board, and that the Board had approved his recommendation.

Mrs. Mary L. Smith, Secretary of the Pulpit Committee, stated that the application of Dr. Smith had been received, and that it was in order.

Rev. W. L. Carter, Chairman of the Pulpit Committee, stated that the matter would be presented to the Board at its next meeting.

The meeting adjourned at 11:30 A.M.

Respectfully submitted,

Mrs. Mary L. Smith, Secretary
