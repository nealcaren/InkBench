Mrs. Jane Irwin  
10 W. 130th St.,  
New York  

May 5, 1907  

Dear Mrs. Irwin,  

Your letter of the 3rd inst. is received and I am obliged  
to you for the information it contains.  I am doing nothing  
at present, but shall be glad to hear from you whenever  
you think there is a vacancy which might interest me.  

It is possible that I may not feel that the archives are open to me  
until after June 1st, but in any event I shall be troubled  
with the problem of how to secure the position.  

Very sincerely yours,  

Gussie Otts  

GOO.
