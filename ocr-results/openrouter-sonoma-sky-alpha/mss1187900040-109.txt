I was really sorry to hear  
that I would not see you  
more.  Thou seem'st of late  
much depressed about the  
Water Power question but  
I trust the letter to Pres't  
will effect all the good  
it is intended to do.  Thy  
delay in sending it has  
been by a deficiency  
in State funds which  
prevent our getting  
the forms with  
which to print it
