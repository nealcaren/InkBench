Mr. Frederick Douglass

My Dear Sir:-

Your attention is respectfully called to the following
facts which have been heretofore published but which we
deem of sufficient importance to repeat at this time.

In the case of the late conference leading up to the
organization of the National Executive Committee of the
Republican Party, your name was presented as one of the
members of that committee.

This oversight has caused much embarrassment and regret
amongst those who know you and admire you as a leader.
