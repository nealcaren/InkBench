West Hartford Conn. 8 mo. 18th 1854

My dear friend

The application which thee made to me
to intercede for the extension of thy
absence from the colored school, has
been denied by the committee.  There are
reasons assigned, which, in my view, are
not very satisfying.  The colored people
themselves now feel that the battle is
fought, & that children should
return to school.  I can not but
approve of this, & hope thee will
