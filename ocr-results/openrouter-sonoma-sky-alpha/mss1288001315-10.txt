WOMEN AS POOR LAW GUARDIANS.

Because the larger number of Paupers are Women and Children.

Because the care of the getting up of Schools only required the domestic work which has been done by the Ladies in the Union where

Because to many cases come before them that should be brought before the Board & can escape attention

Because they being the women are best able to understand home management

To secure the election of a fair proportion of women as

Poor Law Guardians is earnestly requested by the Society.

An annual subscription of £1 is requested by the Society.

Miss L. Corcoran, Hon. Sec., 29, South Kensington.

Published by the Society for Promoting the return of Women as
Poor Law Guardians.
