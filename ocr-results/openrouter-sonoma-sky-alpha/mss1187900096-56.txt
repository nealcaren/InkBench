Middletown, July 15, 1894

My dear friends:

Congratulations to you on your safe
arrival in Washington. I am so glad
for your sake & our own. I think
we shall return the day with you
suggested - the 23rd - if you will
have us. Miss Charlotte has been
here. She is down as usual. I
think she has improved since she
came. She does quite a little
walking, the first since her illness.
I am walking more than I did not.

994
