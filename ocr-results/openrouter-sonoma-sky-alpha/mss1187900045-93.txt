My dear friend,

I have been thinking of thee
much of late, & wishing to hear
from thee.  I suppose thee
hast heard of our troubles
with the nurse.  She has
been very ill, & we have
had to send her away.
We are now looking for
another.  I hope thee'll
write soon.  Affectionately
thy friend,

S. G. Lyte
3 mo. 18th 1873
