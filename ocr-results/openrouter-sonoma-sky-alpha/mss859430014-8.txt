My Dear daughter
Oct 8 1857

We are still getting along
well but still we are on the job Small
respite heat, debate to summer
bright Weather Brother we are having
such nice weather since cotton harvest
she was Daisy was not feeling
well, during the past days, the Dr came
her ladyship and she ate it.
I called better now well she at all as
that came I am sending a letter
last week, and so from that day on
write whenever you can Do
to you much love from us
mother
Sallie.
