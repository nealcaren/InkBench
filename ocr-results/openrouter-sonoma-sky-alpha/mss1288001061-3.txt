My dear friend,

I am glad that thee has been able to attend meeting. I have seen my dear Sally who has been absent from meeting.

I am so glad to hear from thee and so sorry to hear of thy illness. I hope thee will soon be better. I have been very busy with my work and have not had time to write sooner. I am glad to hear that thee hast been able to attend to thy business. I hope thee will soon be quite well again.

Thy friend,

M. Shober
