Mrs. Douglas: May 6/94

Dear Madam -

I am glad to hear from you
that you are better. I was
glad to get your letter, & I
hope you will soon be
quite well again. I am
much pleased with your
daughter's letter, & thank
you for letting me see it.
While they were with me
I was quite well, & in
knowing they would
be with you I felt
better myself, as I had
the assurance of your
being with them.
I have been much
better myself & trust I shall
continue so. I have been to
the country to see
my friends & am
now quite well.
About the book I
have not yet heard
from the publisher,
but hope to soon.
I remain yours truly,
