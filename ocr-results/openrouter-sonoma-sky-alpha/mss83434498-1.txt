Augt 15 1861

My dear Cousin

I have just received your letter of the 10th inst enclosing check for $50 - many thanks for the same. I am glad to learn that you are all well & that you are enjoying your visit. We are all well here - the children are all at school & doing well.

The war news is not encouraging - we have had a severe defeat at Bull Run & the army is in a bad way. The President has called for 500,000 more men & I fear we shall have a long war.

I am busy with my work & get along very well. The girls send their love to you all.

Your affectionate Cousin
