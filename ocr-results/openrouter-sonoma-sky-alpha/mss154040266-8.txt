Mary Grey Peck  
February 7th, 1911.  

My dear Miss,  

I am expecting each week there will be a statement of the work in the North Dakota campaign.  
If there is any item of interest bearing upon it, will you kindly send me a copy?  

I am expecting to go to Oklahoma in March for a great pleasure and if there will be time I shall write you and let you know the date.  
I have to go to New York to the court but there is no date but I hope the place is so near that I can go.  
I want you to know that I am enjoying your letters fully.  

Yours very truly,  

Carrie Chapman Catt
