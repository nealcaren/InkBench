I have been desirous for some years past to express to you my gratitude for your many acts of kindness to me and mine, but more especially for your kind interposition in my behalf with the President of the United States for years past in my applications for a situation in the Patent Office, which I have been vainly endeavoring to obtain for the last four years. And also for your kind interposition with the Department of State in my application for the office of Consul at St. Thomas in the West Indies. The application of Florida was successful by the aid of friends as I understand.

Believing you will accept my thanks, I remain

Yours respectfully,

Francis B. Wilson
