Adoringly No. 64

Dear Papa

I am very sorry that you must be quite unwell.
I hope you will not be long in affliction, but that you may soon
be well again, & that you will not have to suffer so much.  I am sorry that you have
been afflicted with a severe cold, which has brought on a cough, & with my
whole heart I sympathize with you in your affliction, but I hope you will
not be long in it.  The circumstances
which have caused it, I hope will soon pass over.  If you will not be at home
any length of time, I will write again soon.

Your affectionate daughter
