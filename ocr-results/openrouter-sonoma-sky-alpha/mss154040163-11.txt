Sunset Park
July 15, 1897

My dear Stanley. Much thanks for his
letter and the news & clippings regarding
Mr. Blinn. I thank you because for myself.
National Reform. Thyra's report was
a letter she had from him, in which he
said he had asked my intercession
suffering that Christians would send him
great aid as of wood &c. I had
that C. T. Mrs. B. was also applying
equally to her failing neighbors -
such as she &c. else she would have
done more to be admired by my many
unstable friends.
