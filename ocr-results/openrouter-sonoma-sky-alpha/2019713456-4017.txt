Septr 7th 1859

The Career

of the

God-Idea in History.

BY Hudson Tuttle,

Author of "Arcana of Nature," "Origin and Antiquity
of Man," etc.

"Through the long night that covers me,
Dark as the grave within its gloom,
Awaiting the great Deliverer,
When shall he come?"

"But this the fire of that dark place,
Somewhat of all that it hath been
To me who am its former self,
Knows more of all the world's affairs
Than thou or I."

Boston:
Published by Adams & Co.
