Proctor, [Ms.] Aug 2. 1865

My dear Friend
Your kind favor came in due time for which all thanks - the suggestions were sensible of service, and its kind sayings much grateful to both of us - Tom will stay where he is at school, with an increase in his salary of $200. and has also engaged to teach Elocution at the Catholic college, where his rate of compensation will be more liberal than he has had before - and in addition to this, he is determined to try at Public Reading the next winter - and can you furnish him with a list of the different Literary S- where he might be able to make engagements?
