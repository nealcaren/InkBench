But as there are no complete libraries in Romania and not
a single feminist library, it is impossible for us ever to know
the title of the book, necessary for preparation.  We therefore
beg you, as to the other sections of I.W.S.C. to ask your help.
Feeling so much the lips of a poor library we will
try, at one time with our studies, to found one at B. and
perhaps then we'll be able to assemble round it the
Women in a club a, we are dreaming of since a long time.
Could your association, be able, for the generous act of yours,
help with titles and to send us one or the other standard work
on women movement in your country and specially
books, where we could find biography of your famous women.
