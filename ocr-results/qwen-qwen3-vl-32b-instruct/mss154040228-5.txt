Dictated 11-20-'31

My Dear Mrs. Catt:

I'm in the General Hospital, Ostopathic Unit #780 - at Los Angeles, at the present with a fractured leg. I have been here 8 weeks today. I hope soon to be released from this terrible house of torture, a cast, so that I can return to my little apartment at 609 Fairview Ave., S. Pasadena, Calif. Many friends are wonderful to me in many ways. Address my close friend Mrs. Lois Franklin, 1718 Oak St., S. Pasadena - who has all - of me in charge.

Cordially

Henrietta C. Moore
S78
