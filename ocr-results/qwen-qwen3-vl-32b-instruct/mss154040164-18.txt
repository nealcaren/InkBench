My dear Steady:
I cannot think I could possibly convey to you the very great pleasure and satisfaction your biographic letter gave me.  I wanted to know all that you told me, and you told it clearly that it was like a conversation with you.
The Christmas card came and was hung up on the foot of my bed for a week.  They tell us that travellers in the tropics must beware of diarrhea, but I thought myself quite immune as I had not had such a thing for thirty years, but I got one nevertheless and was in bed a week.
