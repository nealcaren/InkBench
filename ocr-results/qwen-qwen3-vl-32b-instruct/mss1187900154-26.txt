Chicago, Ill. U.S.A. June 12.

My dear Mr. Douglass:
I lean for the last call
tomorrow morning & how I have put off so
long my calling on you & Mrs. Douglass that
I shall be unable to do so. Do make my
excuses to your good wife for me. I remember
her with so much pleasure in Paris. I hope
to return to Chicago later in the summer &
Then I will not fail to see you both. In
the meanwhile believe me,

Very truly yours,

Theodore Stanton,
26 West 61st St.,
New York.
