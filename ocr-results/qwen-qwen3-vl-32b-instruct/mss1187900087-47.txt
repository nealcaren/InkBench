"and. -- I continue to believe that the
"Northern forces will raise the siege and
"retreat to the North, proclaiming perhaps
"secession, and that the unfortunate state
"of things will continue yet for months.
"If the governments of Cleveland and Harrison had acted in good faith in their relations with Hayti, this war would not have
"lasted. -- I ask myself if it would not
"be well for me to go to Port au Prince with
"Mr. Douglas to see if the two parties contending cannot arrive at an honorable understanding."
Now, my dear Douglas, this letter is already so
long that I must cut it short right here.
Very sincerely and very cordially yours,
E.S. Bassett.
