Washington D. C.
July 15. 1883.

Dear Grandpa,
I received
your affectionate letter on the
12th. and we were very glad
to hear from you, and I know
that you are getting along
nicely.
Everything is passing along
smoothly here, excepting the
weather which is at times al-
most intolerable.  It is now
about nine o'clock and the
thermometer stands about 84½°.
Charlie has not left us yet-
for the Eastern Shores, and
I doubt whether he will or
not; I think the latter as
