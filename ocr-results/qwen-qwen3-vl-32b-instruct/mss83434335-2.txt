Hartford, Octob. 14th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen pounds and seven pence —
Exeter Cyms
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1781
as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army. — In behalf of Exeter Cyms
£. 13. 0s. 7d.
Chas. Fanning Trust.

Voucher in Char. Lambit's. This of this date.
