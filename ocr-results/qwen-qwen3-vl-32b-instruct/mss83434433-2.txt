Dear Sir

I have thought of an expedient to bring in Caroline which I submit to you. It is this, Genl. Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mopopeppi now, if you could dispense with Janet & let Genl. M. & let her go & stay his negro & get it out that he has bought her, it would I am sure bring her in & get it to out that it is an arrangement of mine over which you have no control: What do you think of it? Let me know.

Yours truly
Jno. H. Seay
Mr. O'Neill
