Georgeville, P. O. S. C.
Friday, Aug. 20th, 1880.

Dearest Papa & Mamma:
Yesterday we went on a pleasant picnic to Fitch Bay. It was an all-day trip. We had an early breakfast, & then the Holbrooks came over, & we all piled into a little steam-launch, chartered for the occasion. It carries 25, & there were 25 of us. The engine divides it in the middle, with wood heaped up on each side (fancy a steam engine run by wood!) & the back

was pretty, too. Jessie is in his favorite, but she is more of a rough & less of a young lady than Sadie. Sorry, I am well.

You ought to have been there to see the pretty place & feel the breeze.
