DISCHARGE.

This is to certify, That No. 503, Spencer Darr, a Landsman, has this day been discharged from the U. S. S. "Belville" and from the naval service.

Dated this 14th May 1865.

APPROVED:
J. R. Connolly
CAPTAIN. [signed]

H. MacGowan
PAYMASTER. [signed]
