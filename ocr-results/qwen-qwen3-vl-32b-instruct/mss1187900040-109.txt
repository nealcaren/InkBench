But it really seems that I am never - never to have what I wish for. - Everything seems against me - I wonder if some of us are really destined to suffer - Perhaps it is the heavy means of softening the heart. - and of teaching us true - sympathy - if so - oh! it is better to be afflicted - for it is terrible! to be indifferent to the sorrow of any delicate creature; I know by sad experience - what it is to be surrounded by those - upon whomfortune ever smiles - Draw hard hearted they are! Their coldness is worse to bear than the sorrow which tears the heart -
