Just this moment comes the
letter by with you letter
about me — Mary dear — I
sympathise with the pang
you feel at my
dinner's decline.  But all
things work together for good
to do good souls as you say.  So
Mary dear never mind,
there must be something good
for you somewhere, because
you deserve it.  I am glad
the children are as good as
nice.  I hope they will
always be.  I'll Mary I want
to have brie in the butter—
Please look and see if there
is any — love to Mother & all — but mostly you.
Mrs. Fen.
