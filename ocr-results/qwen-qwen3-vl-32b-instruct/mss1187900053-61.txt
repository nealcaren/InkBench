Conegar Villa,
Bridport.
Dorset
May 16th 1849.

Dear Mr Douglas

It is a very long time since we have written to you or heard from you, and therefore now in hopes of provoking a reply.  We really are very much in the dark about you, & do not even know whether you still hold office under the Government, as we have not heard to the contrary I conclude that you do - & therefore that you have not been lecturing, holding meeting over the country that last winter as of old.  Hope that your health is good - & that you can report well of yourself & of your family.
