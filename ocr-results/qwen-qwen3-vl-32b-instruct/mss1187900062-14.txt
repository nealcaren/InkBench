ALBERT H. WALKER,
COUNSELOR AT LAW,
(Including Patent Causes.)

Hartford, Connecticut,

If the colored leaders take to denouncing the Supreme Court; they will alienate the sympathy of their best friends in the North. The abolition of slavery; the prohibition of discriminating State laws; the extension of the ballot to the colored man: these are secure. If the colored leaders denounce any body for not also furnishing by law, the unauthorised and absurd exclusion of colored people from particular private property and of hotels & theaters it will look rather childish. It will tend to show that the colored people care more for the show than for the substance of citizenship.
