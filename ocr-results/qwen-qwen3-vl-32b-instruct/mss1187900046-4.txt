Jan 22 1874.
Washington D.C.

My Dear Father,
I have received your kind letter and was very glad to hear from you, but I am so sorry that you feel so.  A heart going to Rochester, I hope it will be all right their, when you go their.  I shall take good care of your letters and not think of disobeying them in any way, we have had a few very Cold days but we have very pleasant weather.

Yours
