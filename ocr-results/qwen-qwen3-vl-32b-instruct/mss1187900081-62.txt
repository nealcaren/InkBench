Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Douglas Esq.
Dear Sir:

The enclosed
Clipper from the Kansas City Times
of the 23rd. I enclose to you for
your verification, or denial, &c., 
&c., of your Col. lawyers, and friends
in that state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow.
We have been induced to believe that
both New York, and Indiana
Creed be fully relied upon to
Cash their electoral vote for our
Galveston Standard bearer. Harrison
and Morton. A reply, either
Confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige

Yours truly
J. S. Severtson
