Bedridden. I began this epistle at 9 A.M. and it is now 7 P.M. It may not be much of a letter, but it represents a day’s worth of labor. I have had a number of experiences with folks who had to treat me and they gave me a headache and made me sicker all the twice. I'm not going to take a stroll about the house while my bed is being repaired and after taking seven doses of the bottles and snifters, I should try to get some sleep for tomorrow must be well whether I can or not.
