**Everett House**  
J.G. WEAVER JR. & CO.  
PROPRS.  
**Ocean House, Bellevue Av.**  
**Newport, R. I.**  
**Sunday, August 19, 1883**  

My dear Mr. Doylard,

I have just found out this morning that the export of salt barrels from Newport has been suspended. I will inquire further into this matter and report to you.

Yours truly,
