1400. On or before the 1st day of January 1866 we promise to pay Jane E. Stealing or her heirs or assigns the just and full sum of four hundred dollars in current funds, it being for the hire of a negro slave named James, which negro is to be clothed in the usual manner of hirelings, with the exception of a blanket. It is understood by the parties should the said negro's hire be taken either by the enemy or the Confederate authorities, the amt. of the time lost shall be deducted from the hire. For the true payment of which sum, we bind ourselves jointly severally our hand & seals this 4th day of January 1865. H. H. & T. Williams  [Seal]
