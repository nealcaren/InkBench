Friday Mar 8

My dear friend.

Yours fromclosed last night.  I am not at [?] that time & I must write you more to be cheerful your mother herself. While I could just have met you.  Yesterday I was ever so much disappointed not to see her.  I was very glad you & mother enjoyed yourselves so well.  I did the same for I had a very pleasant time.  But Dr. & Mrs. Reeder look like if it has one long summer
