Directions for chicken

Scrape off the yellow outside
take a tablespoonful of
flour, add salt, pepper, bake,
stir milk and dip chicken in,
to make it all wet. Have a
skillet half full, melt, butter &
bacon fat in it and put the
fowl in. Turn the chicken, remove
from the fire, pour the nice
hot milk into the pan, mix
quickly until the p. cream, and
add a bit of flour. Run in a little
at a time.
