OFFICE OF
**C. B. BARNES,**
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Chester, Ill., - 1882

Printer Sivorn [Sivorn?] you being
to the Cause. I wish you
would send me one of those
Rand County Banners.
I wish you to do me this favor
as I have spent a great deal
of money this year for the
Republican Cause. And is now
greatly in debt for it. I am sorry.
And the republican committee
have sent a resolution
to Hon. Banamanius PostMaster Gen.
asking the Appointment of
Hen. Charles Paunton, to the
Postoffice at this place
Chester Ill This will help
me to screw my nose for
the money spent for the Party
