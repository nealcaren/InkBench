Nantucket, July 18 - 1891.

My dear friends,

We (Charlotte and I) congratulate you upon your safe arrival back to Washington. Charlotte is sojourning inexplicably for a few weeks. Dr. [Name] is placed at New Haven, he used to come frequently to pass the day with me and I return the visit. He suggested and wishes to stop. Weakley has become quite burdensome to her she is as feeble seemingly the whole town but that she has improved since her arrival on the island is clearly evident.
