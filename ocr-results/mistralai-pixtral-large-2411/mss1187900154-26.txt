### WORLD'S COLUMBIAN COMMISSION. 
#### OFFICE OF THE
##### Director-General of the Exposition.
**Chicago, Ill. June 1st.**

**My dear Mr. Douglas:**

A line for the tail end of summer morning to show I have not forgotten your plea & that Mrs. Douglas's card of recall re automobile problems. Do not be too excused to pass my size for me. I remember her with so much pleasure in Paris, where you took us in the darkness to the memorable Alice was.

Yrs trucktive I'm

Sidney L Stratton,
26 west 61st St.
New York
