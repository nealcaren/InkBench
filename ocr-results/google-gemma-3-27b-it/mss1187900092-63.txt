1601 Mt. Vernon Street,
Philadelphia. Oct 28th ‘95

My Dear Suppose,

My own dear, I saw by some floating 
on my way News papers Speech, thought I 
had more interest Micawber dwell’d it & 
last night I need it very flattering to 
see such pleasant little notions 
in pronouncing it Ollie, for News & 
delins. It throughout the country, still 
do just good.

Yours truly,
M. G. Howard
