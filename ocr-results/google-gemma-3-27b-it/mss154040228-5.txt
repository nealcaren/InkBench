Dictated 11-20-36

My Dear Mrs. Catt:

The General Hospital, Osteopathic
Unit #780 at Los Angeles, affirms
present with a fractured leg.
I have been here 8 weeks today.
Hope soon to be released from
this terrible home of torture, a 
eased, so that I can return
to my little apartment at
609 Fairview Ave., S. Pasadena
Calif.  Many friends, wonderful
to me in every way. 

Friend Mrs. Lois Jenkins @ 1718 
Oak St., So Pasadena is who has
all – 

Cordially,
Henrietta C. Moore
£78
