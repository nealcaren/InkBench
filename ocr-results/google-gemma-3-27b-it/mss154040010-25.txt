**No. 61.** Monkey Temple. One hundred monkeys live in the trees surrounding the temple. Only one got in the picture. They steal fruit from the hands of strangers and the mother monkeys snatch away their babies when they think they have had enough, and run up the trees with them.

**No. 62.** The burning ghat of the Hindus on shore of Ganges. The body on the frame is lying both in the holy water, and on the bank before burning.  About six may be burning at one time.  When complete, the attendants sweep ashes, and unburned fingers, legs or arms into the river.
