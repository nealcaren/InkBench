Washington, D. C., January 7, 1866.

      Ct. 

Sir:

Your Return of Quartermaster’s Stores as of [illegible - looks like Oct?] , for the 
month of Oct., 1865, has been received, and, when examined, will be sent to the Third

Very respectfully,
Your obedient servant,
