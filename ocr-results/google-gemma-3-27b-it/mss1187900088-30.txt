*FIRST ANNUAL ASSEMBLY,* *LAKE JACKSON,* *LEON COUNTY,* *FLORIDA*

Columbia, Tenn.
Nov-5th-1889.

Hon. Frederick Douglass,
My Honored Sir:-

The respectfully call your attention to our letter here with and ask take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful progress this move is making. As far as it is practicable the leading men of letters of our race have been conferred with, and up to date, out of the many responses receive, not one has withheld endorsement and active cooperation.

The plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in Fla. that being near the center of the “Chautauquan Belt.” It first the Chautauqua is to be itinerary extending the scope of meeting. The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the famous New York Chautauqua.
