Chicago, Ill., U.S.A. June 1st.

My dear Mrs. Daylass:

I can for the fact in & 
knowing my coming & how I have put off so 
long my calling on you & Mrs. Daylass that 
I shall be unable to see you. Do make my
excuses to your just wife for me. I remarks
her with much pleasure in Paris. I hope 
to return to Chicago later in the summer & 
Then I will not fail to see you both. &
the meanwhile believe me, 

Very Sincerely

Milmore Stanton,
26 West 61st St,
New York
