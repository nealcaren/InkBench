REPUBLICAN NATIONAL COMMITTEE,
• No. 241 FIFTH AVENUE,

New York, Aug 17th 1880. 

Dear Sir:

I send you by mail today the
advance sheets of an article by Mr. Jay
which is to be published in the Internation-
al Review on the 23rd. Thinking it
may interest you I take the liberty of
sending you this copy, but not if you
please to be made public before that
date.

Yours Truly,
[signature] 
Marshall Jewell
