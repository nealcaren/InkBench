Ro. Chester Aug 1/59

Amelius Caulkin Esq.

Dear Sir

Herewith I 
hand you one dollar which I 
am Hotel is the Amt required
to secure me a Copy Right
on my new Game, the
title of which Dinclaire 
& I might have the produces
to return me Copy right 
for damas they Much oblige

Yours very truly
G. W. Deekes
