Princeton, N.J. Aug. 29. 1868

My dear Anna,

Your kind form came in due time for which all thanks - the suggestions were sensible & serviceable and its kind Sayings most grateful to both of us.  And I will stay where
he is at School, with an increase in his salary of $200. and has also engaged to teach elocution at the Institute tooley, when his fate of compensation will be more liberal than he has had before - and in addition to this, he is determines to try at Public speaking the next term - and can pursue it more with a lack of the differences of them 
& where he might be able to make engagements & I can look
