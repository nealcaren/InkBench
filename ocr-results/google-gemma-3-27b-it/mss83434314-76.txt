**REMARKS:**
1.  All officers and soldiers are to take the oath prescribed by law, to support the Constitution of the United States; and all are to certify, on this roll, whether they have so done, or not; 
2.   Changes of any commissioned officer, muster into service or otherwise, are to be noted here; also, the date of such change, and the cause thereof;
3.  All applications for furlough, for any cause, are to be noted here, specifying the length of time, and cause, and the disposition thereof; and upon the return of any soldier from furlough, the fact is to be noted here, with the date.  Likewise, all applications for discharges, and all discharges, are to be noted here, and the cause set forth. 
4.  The monthly muster rolls and reports, with all their accompaniments, are to be forwarded, according to law, to the proper office the 1st day of each month. 
5.   Each officer and soldier is required to examine carefully this roll, and state, in writing, if any inaccuracies are perceived; and if any mistake is discovered, it is to be corrected immediately.  
6.   Particular attention is to be given to the like or want of uniformity in the persons of soldiers, and to the correctness of any remarks; also, to the dates in the various items.
7.   The proper entries are to be made in this roll, relating to the wants of the soldier and the United States.
