June 21, 1958.

My dear daughter.- Miss
Snack came yesterday
and brought the box and
visited with me awhile.
Trip up and had a nice
home and working at
weeds in the back and 
the like. 

We were very glad
to talk with you Sunday- 
better. It help Parkes, feel
well at present. Brother & 
family are doing very 
well! he doesn’t come as
often now is still work-
ing out at Jackson. And
he [?] getting welfare
and won’t you are well
for you comes home be-
fore you. School is out &
ren will be over to Child-
much love from Mother’s.
