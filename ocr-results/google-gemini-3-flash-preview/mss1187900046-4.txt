Jan, 22
1874.
Washington D.C.
My Dear father.
I have received your
kind letter and was
very glade to hear
from you. but I am so
sorry that you feal.
so. a bout going do
Rochester. i hope it will
be all Right thair.
when you go thair. I
shall take good care of your
letters and not think of
distroying them in anny way,
we have had a few very
cold days but we have 
[we] very pleasant weather.
