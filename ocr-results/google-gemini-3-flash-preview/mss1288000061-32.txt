Georgeville, P. Q. Canada.
Aug. 20, 1897.
Dear Papa & Mamma:
Yesterday we went
on a pleasant picnic to
Fitch Bay. It was an
all-day trip. We had an
early breakfast, & then the
Holbrooks came over, &
we all piled into a little
steam-launch, chartered
for the occasion. It
carries 25, & there were 25
of us. The engine divides
it in the middle, with
wood heaped up on each
side (fancy a steam-engine
run by wood!) & the back

[*was pretty, too. Jessie is his favorite, but she is more
of a romp & less of a young lady than Sadie. I am well.
Sorry to think it is getting near the end. Lots of love. S.S.D.
Sophia got her hat - on Miss Barrows, & you ought to
have seen her.*]
