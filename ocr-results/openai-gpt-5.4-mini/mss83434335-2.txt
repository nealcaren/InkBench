Hartford, Oct. 19th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen pounds, and seventeen shillings —
Extra Cyen
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1782
as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army... In behalf of the Cyen
Chs. Fanning Test.
