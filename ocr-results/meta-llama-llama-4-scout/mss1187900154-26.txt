
WORLD'S COLUMBIAN COMMISSION.
OFFICE O' THE
Director-General of the Exposition
Chicago.  W.W.S.A. June 1st.

My dear Mr. Douglas:

I can for the last two 
months or so how I have not offered
to pay my calling on you & Mrs. Douglas That
I shall not make excuses for my not doing 
her with so much the more in Paris. I hope 
to turn to Chicago later in the summer
Then I will not fail to see you to Th. By.

The meanwhile I have,

Yos. Cent T.Jeney

26 West 61st. St.,
New York
