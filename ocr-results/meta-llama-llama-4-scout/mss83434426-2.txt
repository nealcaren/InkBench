$400 On or before the 15th day of January 1866 we promise to pay Jane E. Stearing her heirs or assigns the sum of four hundred dollars in current funds.  It being for the hire of a negro slave named Turner which is to be clothed in the usual manner of hirings with the exception of a blanket.
