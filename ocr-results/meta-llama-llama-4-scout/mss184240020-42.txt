1790 Locust St. Philadelphia
March 21. 1871

My dear sister,

I write, first of all, about Edwinus,-because after a long talk we held about him last night, that takes the first place.  I read thy little note to him from Friday eve in which, while thee hopes to find him better, thee say "I fear me -."  There has been nothing startling new with him, only there seems to be less of him from week to week, and his energy and ability to go or do anything grow less continually.
