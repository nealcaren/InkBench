Jan 23 1894  
Washington D.C.

My Dear Father. 

I have received your kind letter and was glad to hear from you, but I am so sorry that you feel. I had to leave you.  I do.  a bout going to Rochester . I hope it will be all Right there.  When you go there 
shill take good care of your Letters and not think of 
disregarding them in any way,  we have had a poor 
very 
Color days here but have 

Mary pleasant weather. 
$0 40
