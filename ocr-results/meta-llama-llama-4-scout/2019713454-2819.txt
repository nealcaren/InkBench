BOUND VOLUMES OF OUR YOUNG FOLKS.

Volumes One and Two of OUR YOUNG FOLKS,
comprising the Numbers for 1865 and 1866, 
have been bound uniformly in handsome cloth.
Price of each volume, with plain edges, $2.50; 
with gilt edges, $3.00.
Each contains about 800 pages, 
and nearly 200 illustrations. 
These elegant and admirable volumes 
will be sent, free of expense, 
to any part of the United States 
except Nevada and the Territories, 
on receipt of the price. 
Cloth covers will be furnished for 50 cents.
