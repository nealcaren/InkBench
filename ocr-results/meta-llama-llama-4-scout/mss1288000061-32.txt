Georgville, P.Q.

Papa & Mamama:

Yesterday we went on a pleasant picnic to Little Bay.  It was an all-day trip. We had an early breakfast, & then it  Holbrooks came over, & we all piled into a little steam-launch, chartered for the occasion. It carries 25, or there were 25 of us. The engines divides it in the middle, with wood heaped up on each side (fancy a steam engine run by wood!) At the back
