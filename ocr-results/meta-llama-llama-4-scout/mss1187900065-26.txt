House o Representatives, Washington, D.C.
Dear Sir

Yesterday, deposed with the Surrogate of Hudson Co, N.Y. the Will of the late Ottilie Assump, along with a better one from me declning to serve as Executor, Copy of which I forward to you with this.

You will please take such action in and beleave me 

Your's with great respect
Henry Bergh
