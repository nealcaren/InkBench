FIRST ANNUAL ASSEMBLY, LAKE JACKSON, LEON COUNTY, FLORIDA.

Columbia, Tenn.
Nov. 5th 1889.

Hon. Frederick Douglass,

My honored Sir -

The respectfully call to your attention to our letter had and also take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful progress this move is making. So far as practicable the leading well of leaders of our race have been consulted with, and up to date, out of the many responses received, not one has withheld endorsement and active cooperation.

The plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in Fla. That being near the center of the "Bathgapian Belt."

At first the Chautauqua is to be itinerant and go to place of meeting. The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the former New York Chautauqua.
