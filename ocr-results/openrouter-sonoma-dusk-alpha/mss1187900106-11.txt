State of Washington,
1610, Blk. 6, Spokane, Washington,

Dear Daughter:

North Yakima, Aug 7, 1892.

On my first visit up in my new
daughter, she tells me the wife of H. P. Scott who
holds the office just from Spokane in the
K. of P. & C., as they call it here.
has been absent about half the time for
two years or more.  She was in perfect health in my last campaign
doing clerical work in my office, two months
ago, which exhausted her for my
left eye, and then the cataract for the front of my
eye.  I went to Spokane to sublet with special coach so
to my left eye direct steps, with the aid of Dr.
Fores, I can see at least as well as before.  Thanks to Providence.  I
can discern thy skill for my being.  May it bring thee
happiness with love, from thy Father.
