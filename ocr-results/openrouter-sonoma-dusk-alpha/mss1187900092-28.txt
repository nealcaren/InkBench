Philadelphia
Oct 14th 90

Dear Grandpapa,

I have learned with
much surprise that you are still
in the city, much as you are unwell
& intended leaving for Atlantic City
your sudden ducky, I am very glad
that each is better than you were granted
you & Edwin that you were far
happier no doubt & then only wants
that matters could be advanced
as all in you as remain in this
city

E.W.
