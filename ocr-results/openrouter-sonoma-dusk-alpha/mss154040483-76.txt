Whose Government Is This?

How to Enlist Help
From Our Readers

There are two questions in the mind of every woman reader of The Crisis today.  The first is, "What is the Woman's Party doing?" and the second, "What can I do?"  To the first, the League of Women Voters and the Woman's Party can give a detailed answer.  To the second, the Woman's Party must look to its readers for an answer.

If every woman in the Woman's Party would get one new member, the membership would be doubled.  If every member would get two new members, the Party would be quadrupled.  The need for a strong organization is greater now than ever before, for the fight for equal rights for women is on in every State.  The Woman's Party has branches in every State, but it needs more members in every State.  It needs more money.  It needs more workers.  The women of the Nation need the support of every woman in the Party.  The Party needs the support of every woman in the Nation.

The Woman's Party is fighting for equal rights for women in all the States where women have the vote.  It is fighting for the submission of the Federal Amendment to the States.  It is fighting for the enforcement of the laws which have already been passed.  It is fighting for the appointment of women on all governmental boards and commissions.  It is fighting for the recognition of women in the diplomatic service.  It is fighting for the equal pay for equal work for women in the Government service.  It is fighting for the right of women to serve on juries.  It is fighting for the right of women to hold office.  It is fighting for the right of women to practice law.  It is fighting for the right of women to enter the professions.  It is fighting for the right of women to vote in all elections.  It is fighting for the right of women to be represented in Congress.  It is fighting for the right of women to be represented in the Cabinet.  It is fighting for the right of women to be represented on the Supreme Court.  It is fighting for the right of women to be represented in every branch of the Government.
