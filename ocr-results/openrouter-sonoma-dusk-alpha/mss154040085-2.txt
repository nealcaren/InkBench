COOK COUNTY HOSPITAL  
MANOR, ILLINOIS  

December 6, 1940  

Mrs. David Clemens  
120 La Salle Avenue  
Chicago  

Dear Mrs. Clemens:  

I wish to thank you for your appreciation of the commemorative  
gift of the book "History Lemons Low."  

Personally, I was sorry about the address, a copy of Tuesday's  
International.  Our club has made efforts to carry on the every  
letter young girl who is choosing nursing as her vocation  
to your excellent adjustment.  

You see, therefore, I was glad as we are this membership  
meeting.  

Very truly yours,  

Millie B. Ralston  
Chief Executive Secretary
