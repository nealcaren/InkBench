Dear Mrs Warren

I can not leave town without
telling you how much I love
you.  I have always admired your
intellect, your frankness & your
unfailing sympathy with all
spiritual work.  The work
of the past year has been
my greatest joy & from
there in my heart I thank
you for your sympathy in
the founder's work.  There
is no work I love more
especially as it is so
necessary & in particular
this course.  Thank you
for your friendship, & know
that I must leave, Sincerely
Mary Jane Bennett
