December 6, 1863

Dear Father,

I have not heard from you since I last wrote you.  I am well & hope you will be the same.  I think that you will not expect to hear from me very often, not having much to write about.  I am attending to my business as usual & have no news to tell.  I think of the family often & wish that I could be with you.  I have been to see Mother twice & found her as well as could be expected.  I am anxious about her & hope she will soon be better.  If you will let me know if you need any help I will send it.

I remain,
Your Affectionate Son
