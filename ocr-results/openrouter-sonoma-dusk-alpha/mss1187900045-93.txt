We have thee's letter of 26th
ult. & are truly glad to hear
that thou art so much better
than when we last heard from
thee.  We have been quite
anxious about thee, & are
rejoiced to learn that all
things are going on so well.
We have been very busy
with our own affairs, but
have not been so absorbed
but that thy condition has
been much in our thoughts.
We are glad to hear that
thy mind is so quiet.  We
hope that thou wilt soon be
able to travel.  We shall be
very glad to see thee, & hope
that thou wilt come as soon
as thou canst.  We are all
well, & join in love to thee.
Thy affectionate sister
S. G. Lyttle
3 mo. 1st 1873
