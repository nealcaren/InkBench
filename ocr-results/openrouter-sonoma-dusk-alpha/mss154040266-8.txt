Mary Grey
February 7th, 1911

Dear Miss Shaw,

I have been so busy of late that I have
not written to thee for some time.  I have
been expecting to hear from thee
what the plans are for the meeting
of the Board in Washington.  I am
exceedingly anxious to have
a statement as to what the North Dakota
campaign will cost.  I am staying in Oklahoma
so long with great pleasure
that I have written to Mrs. [?] and asked her
if she could not send me the list of
what the campaign cost.  I have not
heard from her yet.  I think it would be
well to have it printed and sent out to
the places where we are to go.  I have
not heard from the Secretary but I suppose
she is ready to go.  I think the best plan would be
to have her go with me to
the places where I am to speak.

Yours for liberty
Carrie Chapman Catt
