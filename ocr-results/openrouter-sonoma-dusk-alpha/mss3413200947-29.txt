Eagle Mountain House
White Mountains, N.H.
Aug 3d 1897

Dearman Alice:

When I met him I
had not heard from you
for two months after she
had gone - she had failed
so rapidly.  I said, "she
must have suffered dreadfully
to have failed so in that
time."  He said, "Oh no,
she did not suffer at all."
Then he wrote you for me
telling that she had had
cancer & that she would
not get well, & had died
& that he would
