February 2d, 1870.

Mrs. Mary J. Douglass.

I have the honor to forward to
you the publisher's notice of the "Pioneer"
pamphlet, entitled "In Memoriam"
For the White side of the great
controversy between the late Senator
Sumner and your distinguished husband.
It was prepared after a reading of all the
speeches and every other
word that has been printed
in the controversy.  The pamphlet is
in the form of a letter to
the "New York Times" and was
written by one who has known
your husband from boyhood
and admires his character
and public services.  The
pamphlet is published by
the "American News Co."
and will be sent to all
the leading papers and
reviewed in the "New York Times."
The writer will be glad to
have your opinion of it.

Respectfully
Geo. Alfred Townsend.

Mrs. Mary J. Douglass,
Washington, D.C.
