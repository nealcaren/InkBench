Aug 24, 1907  

Dear Miss Dickinson  

I thank you for your kind letter of the 22nd inst.  
and for the reason that I am not in a position to  
offer you an engagement at the present time.  

Mrs. Lindsay Morris is thoroughly posted in the matter of this kind  
of theatre, and her judgement is to be relied upon.  

Very truly your kind wishes,  
Henry C. Tupper
