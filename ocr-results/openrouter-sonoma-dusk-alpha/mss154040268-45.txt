To the Evening Post.
Oct 18th

Dear Sir,—

Pray send my verses, "Graceful, lucid, &c." to
my address, as I am anxious to read them again.
Through your kindness, please don't dishearten me
any more, by promising for every one of
those vile policies, not of the majority, but
what count's nothing, is a daily nuisance.
The Syracuse
The Terror

New York, Oct.
1915

Th. Devore
