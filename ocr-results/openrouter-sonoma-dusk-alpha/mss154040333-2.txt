2d of Peace 1947

Dear Mr. Chaput,

I just finished the superb book
on your Scout work which you
have accomplished during your
wonderfully active life.

This book is a grand revelation
for all of us in every country
and a deep reward by the
four international groups
through which you have
accomplished such great work.

Sincerely yours,
[signature]
