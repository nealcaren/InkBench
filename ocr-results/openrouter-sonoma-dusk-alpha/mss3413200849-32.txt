Thou mayst have already
seen - This morn
just told me shortly of
the death of Dr. Set Levi
I know...  From 1871
the Prof. of the Law
school of the Univ.
The death was presented
with much feeling in
the papers & the
eulogies said of him
were of the highest
character.  He was
at the head of his
profession & was
universally esteemed
& loved.  He was
about 70 yrs old.
I shall miss him
greatly.  He was
my friend.
