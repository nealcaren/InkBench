Ellis Oct 26 1858

A. King? 

Dear Friend

I received thy letter from
Richmond with much pleasure
& was glad to hear from thee
so soon.  I have been thinking
of thee often since I left
thee & was very much afraid
thee would be sick, but I
am truly thankful thee
are so much better.  I
hope thee will soon be
entirely well.  Give my love
to thy mother & tell her
I was very sorry to leave
her.  Please write me soon
& tell me how thee are
getting along.  

Thy friend

J. W.
