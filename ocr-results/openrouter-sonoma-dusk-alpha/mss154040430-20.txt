In this delicious air and sun in spring
one can almost forget hunger & thirst, but opening
our mail tray brings us back to the world
of gardens that feel this spring with the most
delightful of all the flowers.  The first
fruits of the post season with apples of such
deliciousness that one could eat the whole
bushel.  The girls have been busy with
the usual tasks, & all things seem to
be going on well.  The 2d & 3d class
have been quite busy with their lessons
& the 1st class one would suppose
would be glad of the recess!  but
such is not the case.  They would
rather have their studies.  I have
been thinking much of the pleasure
it would be to have thee with us
