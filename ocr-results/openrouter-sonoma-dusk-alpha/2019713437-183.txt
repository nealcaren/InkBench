July 3, 1832

Sea Garden

To the Hon. John Sergeant

Sir, 

I enclose you a copy of the inclosed, which is the form of a notice in the case of the Commonwealth vs. Blaylock.

Respectfully your obdt. Servt.

J. R. Slack

Philadelphia, July 2, 1832.

To J. R. Slack, Esq.

Sir, 

You will please to give the notice in the case of the Commonwealth vs. Blaylock, in the District Court, in the usual manner.

Respectfully, 

Thos. I. Wharton, Dist. Atty.

In the District Court for the City & County of Philadelphia.

Commonwealth

vs.

Blaylock

Notice

To the Defendant.

Take notice that unless you appear at the next District Court for the City and County of Philadelphia, on the first Monday of August next, and defend the above action, judgment will be entered against you by default, &c.

Thos. I. Wharton, 

Attorney for the Commonwealth.
