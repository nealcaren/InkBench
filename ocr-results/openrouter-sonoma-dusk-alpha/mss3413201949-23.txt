(1) The Durham News and Herald
will receive subscriptions for the Herald
from persons in the county who have not
already subscribed.  This will be done
until the first of the year, after which
time no new subscribers will be received
until the first of the following year.
We hope every one will subscribe before
the first of the year.  We want every
one in the county to read the Herald
every week.  We believe that the Herald
can and will be a great factor in the
upbuilding of Durham and Durham
county.  We shall strive to make it
interesting to every one.  We shall
give the news of the town and county
and state and nation and the world.
We shall endeavor to give the best
thought of the leading thinkers on all
vital questions.  We shall try to be
a good paper and we hope to receive
the support of every one in Durham
county.
