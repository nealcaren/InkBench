Dear Sir,

However, we have searched the archives thoroughly, including ledgers from 1910 to 1925, and there is no mention of the sum you refer to, nor any correspondence relating to an estate or claim of this nature.

If you can provide additional details, such as specific dates, account numbers, or the names of any officers involved, we would be pleased to reopen the inquiry. In the meantime, I regret that we are unable to assist further based on the information supplied.

Please accept our assurances of continued interest in resolving this matter to your satisfaction.
