Worlds Columbian Commission  
Director General of the Exposition.  
Department of Music,  
Chicago Ills June 18  

Dear Sir  

In assuming an official  
position in my calling, I trust you will  
excuse the liberty I take in asking  
your aid in a matter of great  
importance to me.  Should you  
be willing to assist me in Paris, where  
I am to conduct labors in the same  
line as in Chicago, the obligation will  
be great.  If not, I trust you will  
excuse me.  

Very truly  
Hiram Watson  

26 West 61st St.  
New York.
