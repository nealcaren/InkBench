Washington D.C.  
April 15th 1869  

Dear Sutton  

Your reply to my letter was  
received yesterday.  You said in your letter  
that you would let me know what you did  
with my last of which neither  
I nor my friend was not satisfied  
but which was not first rate  
I thought I'd not wait until I  
went home.  I would communicate  
to me immediately that had  
been a member of the Law Class  
here: relieved.  The following is it  

Mr. Langston has taken charge of  
our branch of the Howard University  
and has admitted 43 colored students
