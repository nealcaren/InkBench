Dope's Wells on the Pecos northeast of Carlsbad

Beginning in the spring of 1858 a townsite was surveyed under the direction of the U.S. Army near the mouth of the Delaware Creek on the Pecos River.  New Mexico to supply water for a proposed townsite and military post.  The well was drilled to a depth of 1,000 feet but ultimately failed to produce water and the project was abandoned.
