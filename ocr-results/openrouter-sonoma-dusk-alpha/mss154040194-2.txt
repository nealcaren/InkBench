An Aprile 12, 1872

Amis in progres of the Lliga
I have the honor to inform you that
I have received the sum of $18.50 from
our friend in Richmond because of his
interest in the cause.  I am glad to
say that this money has been added to
our fund in a large measure
through the efforts of our
husband and with your
united and generous
support.  If you could
have been present you
would have enjoyed the
pleasure of seeing the
joy and satisfaction
expressed by these poor
women and the hope
it has given them
for their future.  But
this is only the beginning
of what we must do
to bring to them the
help they so much
need.  We must
have more money
to carry on our work.
If each one of you
would send in your
contribution no
matter how small
it would help us
greatly.  I trust you
will all respond to
this appeal.  I remain
yours very truly

[signature]
