3. There are two instances in the back—one of the, the other directed to self, in the recent book. For either, every will be filed in, until the recent book is filled, then transferred to the present one, & so on. Every letter so presented will be endorsed & a card made for it, which will be filed. The method indicated, provided for the letter book, will be followed. The index, provided for the letter book, will be made every day, & transferred, at the end of a month, to a general index, in which all the letters of the month will be included. 

General Orders
Adjutant General's Office
Washington, Feb. 10, 186_

By command of Maj. Genl. Halleck

E.D. Townsend
Asst. Adjutant General.
