WOMEN AS POOR LAW GUARDIANS.

WOMEN AS POOR LAW GUARDIANS.

Because the largest number of Paupers are Women and Children.

Because more than two-thirds of the work done by the Poor Law Board requires the presence of Women.

Because the election of Women as Poor Law Guardians in the Unions where they have been brought forward has been successful.

Because it is most necessary to visit among the Poor, and

Because the Women Guardians can give careful attention to matters which will engage the Board of Guardians.

Because many of the Women who are now brought before the Public as fit candidates

secure the election of a fair proportion of Women as

Poor Law Guardians is earnestly requested by the Society.

An Annual Subscription of £1 1s. is requested by the Society.

For further information apply to Miss L. F. Cohen, 33, Bedford Place, South Kensington.

Published by the Society for Promoting the Return of Women as Poor Law Guardians.
