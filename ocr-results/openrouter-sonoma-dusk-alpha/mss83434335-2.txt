Hartford, Octr. 9th 1828.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order

on the Treasurer, of this State, for the Payment
of the Balance due me on the first Day of January last,
as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army, in lieu of Subsistence Trust.

L.B. Grant
Pay-Table-Committee Order

To Miss Lambeth Mrs. of Theodore.
