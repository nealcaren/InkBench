After we all broke up the party
we went from place to place
to find thee down at Glover's
but without success.  We
went to my room & spent the
evening there.  We had a very
pleasant time.  I thought thee would
have been there.  But tell me
what thee did with thy evening.
Come over here Saturday evening
if thee can.  I expect to go
to the lecture at the Institute
from which we can return
to my room.  I shall be
very glad to have thee
do so.

From your friend
Anna B. McCleary
