Mrs. Mary F. Beard,
September 28, 1897.

Dear Mrs. Beard,

I am only trying to fill some deficiencies
in my library. They were some books which appeared
in the list of "books most talked about" & not
in the list of "best books." I am trying
to find out if these books & my trying,
as well as if those of these books & my trying,
that I would like to have some of these books & my trying,
that I would like to complete my collection.

I have so many that I would like to complete my collection
that I do with them what I think there is no charge for rent,
houses I have consulted you no disposition of my collection until

Sincerely,
O.G.W.
