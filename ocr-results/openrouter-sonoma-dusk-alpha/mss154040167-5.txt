Miss Clara Hyde
317 W. H. R.
Los Angeles
Calif.

Mar. 4th. 1908.

Dear Friend,
I am sorry to hear of the earthquake and hope you are all right.  I have been wondering about you.  Should like to hear from you.  I have been quite busy of late.  Will write you a longer letter soon.
Your friend,
Louise

*Postmark: Los Angeles Calif. Feb 4 1908 9 AM*
*Stamp: 2¢ Washington*
