AFFIDAVIT TO CLAIM

In the Circuit Court of the City of Richmond,
Va. -- County of Henrico -- to wit:

In the matter of the claim of
Julius Meier

[[printed]]
To the Confederate States
of America, for the use of the
United States, for property
taken or destroyed by the
Army of the United States
during the late war, and
for which the United States
is held responsible by law.

[[printed]]
In pursuance of the Act of Congress approved
March 3rd, 1871, I, Julius Meier, do make oath
that I am the person named in the claim presented
by me to the Southern Claims Commission, and
that the statements contained in the proof of
claim filed by me are true to the best of my
knowledge and belief.

[[printed]]
Sworn to and subscribed before me
this 25th day of February, 1871.

J. B. Minor
Notary Public
