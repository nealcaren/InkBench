Carrie Chapman

Miss Alice Cassatt
80 East 10th St.
New York, N.Y.

November 9, 1906

Dear Miss Cassatt,

This morning's paper tells me how your husband and your sister were both in the hospital and both died yesterday.

Every time I've seen only sure of getting an article you would be willing to have printed if there was ever one, and now should there be one, I'm sorry I have none to send.

My sympathy, as I have gone to you both when I would express it in person, I send in this.

Lovingly yours,
Carrie Chapman Catt
