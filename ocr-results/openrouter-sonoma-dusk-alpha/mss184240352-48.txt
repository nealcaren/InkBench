State of Pennsylvania 5
County of Schuylkill

Before me the subscriber a Justice of the Peace in and for said County personally appeared John P. Runkle who being duly sworn according to law doth depose and say that he is the father of Sarah P. Runkle and that she was born on the 14th day of January in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty two and that she is now in the twenty first year of her age

Sworn and subscribed before me this 14th day of April A.D. 1853

John P. Runkle
his mark

State of Pennsylvania 5
County of Schuylkill

I John B. Deitrich Justice of the Peace in and for said County do hereby certify that John P. Runkle before whom the above deposition was made was at the time of taking the same a Justice of the Peace in and for said County duly elected qualified and acting and that his signature thereto is genuine and that the facts set forth in said deposition are entitled to full faith and credit

In testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this 14th day of April A.D. 1853

John B. Deitrich J.P.
