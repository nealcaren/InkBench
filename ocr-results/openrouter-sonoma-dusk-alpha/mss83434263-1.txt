DISCHARGE.

No. 505.

John William Gordon

Hon. Discharged from the U. S. Navy

and from the service

of the U. S. Ship Kearsarge

at Philadelphia

Oct 1st 1865

after 4 yrs from the Naval Service

of the U. S.

G. Welles

Secy. of Navy

J. W. A. Nicholson

Captain

Commanding
