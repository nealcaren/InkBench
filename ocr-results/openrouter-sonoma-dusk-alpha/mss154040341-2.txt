DRAFT

Washington, D.C.
January 19, 1957

My dear Mr. Talmadge:

I am writing to the Governor of North Carolina to urge that he sign the bill which has passed the legislature providing for the sale of the lands in the Great Dismal Swamp to the Federal Government for inclusion in the Great Dismal Swamp National Wildlife Refuge.  I trust that he will do so.

This is one of several states in which similar legislation would be pending, and in each case I hope the Governor and the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House will recognize the national importance of this form of legislation, without regard to political considerations, and that the Governors will sign the bills of which we spoke.

Yours sincerely,

(signed) Carl T. Rowan

Mr. Joseph R. Talmadge
Secretary to the President,
White House,
Washington, D.C.
