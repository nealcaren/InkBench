Lectures

by

Mrs. Ida Porter Boyer

on

Mexico

Mrs. Ida Porter Boyer, noted lecturer, author and traveler, will speak on Mexico under the auspices of the Women's Department of Columbia Bank & Trust Company.

The first lecture will be held Tuesday, February 9th at 2:30 P.M. in the main banking room.  This is the first of a series of four lectures, the others to be held on February 16th, 23rd and March 2nd.

Mrs. Boyer's lectures are given under the auspices of the Pan-American Society and are most interesting and instructive.  The public is cordially invited to attend these lectures.

There will be no admission charge.

Your Investment

Account

with Evelyn King

You can open a special investment account with Evelyn King, our new Investment Officer, at Columbia Bank & Trust Company.

Mrs. King, who has been with the bank for some time, is thoroughly familiar with the various types of investments and will be glad to assist you in the purchase or sale of securities.

Mrs. King will be pleased to discuss with you the advisability of investing in the higher grade bonds and preferred stocks now available.

She will also be glad to discuss with you the advisability of placing your securities in a safe deposit box.

Mrs. King will be glad to discuss with you the various types of trust accounts.

If you have any securities which you wish to sell, Mrs. King will be glad to assist you.

If you have any securities which you wish to purchase, Mrs. King will be glad to assist you.

If you wish to discuss any phase of investments, Mrs. King will be glad to discuss it with you.

Columbia Bank & Trust Company is equipped to handle all types of investment accounts.

We invite you to discuss your investment problems with Mrs. King.
