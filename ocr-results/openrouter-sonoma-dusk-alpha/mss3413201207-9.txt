Mrs. Howard Lynn
Mod. Dec. 1st/79

Dear Madam,

Enclosed you will find $25.00 which I send to pay for the Howard Journal for one year. I have my address changed to one that I thought of through a friend, but I find that I can not have it. So I have sent one of my own.

I have received your letter & thank you for your kindness, & I wish to thank you for your friendly criticism & for your work. I feel more encouraged by your commendation
