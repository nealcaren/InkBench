Regina

When thee has a moment
in a letter—write to
me a brief account of
thy journey & arrival
at R.Shober's

I enclose what I think
thee'll like—a receipt
for a cake which I have
just made

E.M. Wistar

I enclose some raisins
which were sent to me
by a friend & which
I think thee'll like. 

Philadelphia 2 mo 21. 1870
