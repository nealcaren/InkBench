First Annual Session Lake Jackson Columbia, S.C.

Mr. Frederick Douglass
Washington, D.C.

My Dear Sir: -

We unfortunately call
your attention to our desire to have you as
guest speaker at our next
annual meeting which
is to be held with the
leading colored people
of this section.  We are
aware that this is a great
request but we feel that
with the aid of your
many friends here
the plan is not an
impossibility.  We think
the "Montague Hall" would
suit the Center of
the meeting. The aim is to largely
increase our membership
at the beginning of our
fourth year. The program
would be arranged to suit
your convenience.  

Mrs. F. Douglass  
Secy.  

1879
