Washington D.C.
July 18th 1883

Dear Grandmama,

Your affectionate letter received
12th inst. and was very glad
to hear from you and learn
you were getting along
well.  We are having along
droughty spell at this time
and everything is suffering.
I have not left my bed much about ¾
of the time since I came home.
For the last two days I have
been able to walk and
don't think the matter as
serious as I first thought.  
Don't worry about me.
