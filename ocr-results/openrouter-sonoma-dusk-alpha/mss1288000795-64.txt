Elizabeth Johnson
Richmond Va.
Oct 24th 1862

Dear Cousin

I write you a few lines to let you know that I am well and hope these few lines may find you and family enjoying the same blessing.  Cousin I have been expecting to hear from you long before this, but I suppose you have forgotten us.  I want you to write to me and let me know if you have heard from your husband since the battle of Sharpesburg, and if you have not heard from him, I want you to come to this place and stay until you do hear from him.  I think you had better do this.  

Your affectionate cousin  
until death
