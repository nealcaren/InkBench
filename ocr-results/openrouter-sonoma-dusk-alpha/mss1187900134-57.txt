Mrs. Frederick Robeson, N.Y. Sept. 10, 18[?].

Dear Madam,

We are in receipt of yours of the 8th inst., and are very glad you can supply us with the sketches for the order.

Aug. 31st, in our contract, the letters are to have your choice of design, and if you select
the one in our office, and send us a regular form of contract, and if selected
on this line with either of the two, and return them to you, and will
send us and return one marked "O.K." with two heavy leaves.

Use in front, and this should be cut in the other way, so
than the letters. If this suits with claim for 90 days, and return
them all to us, and with such plans or returns on your hands,

and we shall take pride in executing it in the finest way, and

return it to you as speedily as possible.

Yours respectfully,
J. Weston
