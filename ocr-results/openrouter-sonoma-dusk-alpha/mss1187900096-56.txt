Manchester, July 17, 1894

My dear friends:

Congratulations to you both on your safe arrival at Manchester for a few weeks.  Mrs. Charlotte and I both remember with pleasure our visit to Manchester.  We hope you will remain the day with us.  With this I enclose a suggestion regarding the public speaking she has been doing.  As quite a number have enquired for her services I thought it would be well to have a circular printed.  Down quite a number of engagements already.  I think by advertising she will get more.  I am doing well.  The doctor says not to walk more than a mile a day which I am doing.

94
