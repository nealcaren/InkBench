Mrs. Sarah Osborn

To Francis McTeague of
Weymouth near Boston in the family
of his Father Abraham McTeague
in Philadelphia who has been down to
visit us & who is 
in his twentieth year of 
his age 
In his own hand writing
In his heart will ever be
In act & in his 
In a soul his genius loves.
And with memory's day of joys
Has great & his sisters have
Our chief gratitude is made to
him for his kind & constant friendship
The World is gazing on this.
Slight with thy precious love
Feel my affection. Sarah.
