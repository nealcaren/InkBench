BUILDING CAMPAIGN NEARS CLOSE

$1,000,000 in Pledges Aimed At

Mrs. John A. Hertel Heads Drive for $1,000,000

Final Effort Being Made to Raise Fund for New Chicago Women's Club Building

The campaign to raise $1,000,000 for the new Chicago Women's Club building is drawing to a close, with the final drive set for Saturday. Mrs. John A. Hertel, president of the club, is heading the effort, which has already secured pledges totaling more than $900,000. The building will be a memorial to the club's founders and a center for women's activities in the city.
