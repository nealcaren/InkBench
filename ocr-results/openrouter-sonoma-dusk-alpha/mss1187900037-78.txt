Mount Holy, May 6, 1858

My dear The time from came
in due time for which I came
& brought with it the sug-
gestion I wrote of, to both
of which will stay where
it is at school with an increase
of salary, & the other
to take charge of the
boarding school at Claren-
don.  I have conversed
with her on the subject
& find her willing to
make the change.  He
has been very kind & con-
siderate to her.  It is
of Robert Ransom the
writer of the letter to
which I allude & I
think there is a lack of
the frankness which I
had hoped to see.  I
shall encourage her to
remain.
