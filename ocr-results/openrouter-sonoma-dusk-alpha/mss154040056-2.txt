I pondered over your letter & cannot
see my way clear to do what you wish.  I would try & feel
the pulse of things here.  If I thought
there were a chance of success, I would
write Mrs. Anthony to do the work.  I did not
think Mrs. Anthony would do it.  I tried
to write you upon the worry about
the question there are.  All that I can do
is to say that you are greatly handicapped by not having told me what
the reports in the newspapers are
about times.  We are greatly handicapped by not having to tell you what is the
