My dear Brandy; I do think thee
possibly convey thro' the very great
pleasure & satisfaction I'm feeling thro'
graphic letter ye wrote me.  Ye told it
so clearly that I had little conversation
with thee.  The Christian's first & best
reward - They tell me that during
the two months just past myself
in the highest must go through
as I have not felt such a thing
for thirty years & had work one whole
& more in at it.  I'm feeling
that laudanum fashion.  I daresay
get very good by eating is Southport
& utterly air which is so much better.
