J Weaver's House, Bellevue
N.P. Willis's Summer Residence

My Dear Friend,

I have just returned
to the city, to the hotel,
after a fortnight's absence,
visiting friends in the
country.  I have been
thinking of thee, & 
undertaking to write
thee a line of friendship -
since our last interview -
Time has been passing
but I trust not unprofitably
to thee, & I hope to
hear from thee soon.
