The petition is as follows:

To the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States in Congress assembled:

The undersigned women of the United States, above twenty-one years of age, respectfully but earnestly petition that your Honorable Bodies will pass without delay an act prohibiting slavery in the Territories of the United States.

Whereas, The agitation of the subject of slavery has so far succeeded in diverting the public mind from the consideration of the rights of women, that they are now scarcely remembered or thought of in the political discussions of the day; and

Whereas, Women are heavily taxed without representation, are tried in all courts of justice without a jury of their peers, are not even allowed to make their wills or to sue or be sued in their own names, are held in all respects as helpless minors, and are allowed no voice whatever in the government under which they live; and

Whereas, In view of these facts, the women of the United States demand equality before the law, without distinction of sex or color; therefore,

Resolved, That the women of this nation, in common with all other loyal citizens, desire to see the rebellion suppressed, slavery abolished, the national boundaries enlarged, and the general welfare promoted.

Resolved, That while we recognize the heroism of our soldiers, and the sacrifices of our people, we also claim a share in the glory of the hour, and demand that our rights be respected, our voices heard in the councils of the nation, and our influence felt in the legislation of the country.

Resolved, That we earnestly request the Congress of the United States to pass forthwith an act prohibiting the further extension of slavery in the Territories, as a measure of justice to the colored race, and a step towards the ultimate abolition of slavery throughout the land.

Resolved, That copies of these resolutions be forwarded to the Senate and House of Representatives, with a request that they be entered upon the journals of both Houses.

Mary F. Davis, Secretary.

The meeting adjourned.
