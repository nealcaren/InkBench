WRECKS

AND

RESCUES.

By a Member of the Board of Managers A.F.S.

Published by the Publishing Committee A.F.S.

Let us hear both sides that seek,
The good and the evil, the false and the true;
Such is the world of the weak and the strong,
Of wreck and of rescue, of right and of wrong.

In wrecks that occur and in deaths that ensue,
In the trials that come and the sorrows we rue,
To the tempted and tempted, the fallen and tried,
Let us lend our best aid, and our help not deny.

From the American Female Guardian Society,
No. 29 East Twentieth Street
1859.
