2

Hartford, Conn.

To the Colchester County Tax
Collector for the support
of the applicant who is poor
& infirm & has no means
of support.  Her daughter in
extremis & her son (a
soldier) is absent from this
country not having finished his
enlistment.  She is not able
to furnish her own support
& her daughter is now
confined in the Hospital
suffering from childbirth
which has left her childish.  She
can do no work for the support
of herself & her mother.
