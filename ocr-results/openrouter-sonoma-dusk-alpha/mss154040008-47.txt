New York

The two who have the most. In the
struggle of life the weak in purse would be
like the weak in body, - crushed out by
the strong. Let the best survive, since
only the fittest can be trusted with
the great trust. But there is one
great difference between the two struggles, a
difference so vast that it would make
all the difference between the
strong and the weak in wealth. Yellowly
and inexorably the weak in body
must perish, but the weak in
purse need not. The fittest
would survive, but the unfit
would be cared for with
true Christian charity.

The two parts of it were in a state of
suspense. In the "struggle for life" there was a
truer sense of the word, - a struggle
between the original two which had
been.
