Jean fed us Oolos attended the
League W. Voters and I dodged among
them and along toward
night bought a blue dress and
secured a caller.

I'm hoping the war will end
in the South East and that the Jews
in their white petticoats chase the
Nazis home where at least one
will commit suicide.

Then we meet again and
will be as clean as you are.
Like Mary Wolstonecraft you'll
be "A shining Woman

Looking Forward
Carrie C. Coats

Tuesday 1941 April 8,
