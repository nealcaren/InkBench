But it really seems that
I am never-never to have
what I will, for.-
Everything seems against
me- I wonder if some
of us are really destined
to suffer- Perhaps it is
the only means of
softening the heart- & of
teaching us true
sympathy- & so- Oh
it is better to be afflicted-
for it is terrible! to be
indifferent to the sorrow
of other delicate creatures!
I know by sad experience
what it is to be surrounded
by those - when ice has
frozen over smiles-
Draw hard hearted by
one- Their coldness is worse
to bear than the forgave which
breaks the heart-
