To the Speakers Bureau:

Three roses were sweet, beautiful, temper dispelling, but
Roses sind Verboten.
Keep your money to defend the suit to vindicate my
character. Please don't disobey orders again.
I have a vile temper, growing vile every day which
shows what politics and as for a woman & is a daily comfort to witness the pleasantry of
the Speakers Bureau!
The Terror

New York City, October
1915

Oct 18 in office
