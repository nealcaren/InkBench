you 600 or 700 pages of
my book in a fragmentary
form, not having sufficient
proofs enough to
give you what I wanted
like, just yet. I shall
soon have advance sheets
of the whole 650 pages
and will send you an
early copy ahead of
delivery.  As I get
back proofs from other
chapters I have sent

more.  To-day I enclose
