Directions
for chicken
Scrape off the jelly or juice
Take a table spoon full of
flour, add salt, pepper to taste
stir well and dip chicken in
it.  Have a
skillet hot, melt butter or
bacon fat in it and quickly
fry the chicken.  Remove
the chicken, pour the juice
or jelly into the pan.  If you
like add a bit of cream and
thicken with a little flour.

Brown Bread.  Heat work
in a pot big
enough to contain the ten
of brown bread.  A half
hour's cooking will not hurt
it.  Then open the can and enjoy
