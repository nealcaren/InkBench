Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Douglas Esq.
Kansas:

The enclosed
Clipping from the Kansas City Times
of the 23d. I enclose to you for
your verification, or denial, Respects
of your Cleo adumpers, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow.
We have been induced to believe that
both New York, and Indiana
Circuits be fully relied upon to
Cast their electoral vote for our
Greatest Standard bearer, Horace
Greeley.  A reply, either
Confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige

Yours
A. F. Sweeney
