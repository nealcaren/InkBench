OFFICE OF —
C. B. BARNES,
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Cuthbert, Ga. 189

[Handwritten text begins]
Respected Sir — I am in want
of the Cause — I wish you
would send me one of them.
To Mr. Bangs,
I wish you to do me this favor
as I have spent a great deal
of money this year for the
Republican Cause, and am now
greatly in debt for the Same.
And the Republican Executive
Committee have sent a petition
to Mr. Gramamalus Posty. Gen.
asking the Appointment of
Mr. Charles Banton to the
Postoffice at this place
(Cuthbert Ga) This will help
me to Secure my home for
the money spent for the Party
