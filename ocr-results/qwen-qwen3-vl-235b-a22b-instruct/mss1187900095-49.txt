Mrs. Blaine has been sick in New
York for two weeks, and he
does not plan to get well. There
is much interest manifested in
his condition.

I thank you for long and very
kind letter, and regret that I don't
feel well enough to pay to you
now all that I had it in mind
to pay when I began it. But we
get worked out even when we are
very young men ambitious to run
a long race.

With my kindest regards,
Yours truly,
J. M. S. Pottinger
