WORLD'S COLUMBIAN COMMISSION.
OFFICE OF THE
Director-General of the Exposition.

Chicago, Ill. U.S.A. June 12th.

My dear Mr. Douglas:

I leave for the East to-morrow morning & now I have just off to-day my calling on you & Mrs. Douglas that I shall be unable to do so. Do make my excuses to your good wife for me. I remember her with so much pleasure in Paris. I hope to return to Chicago later in the summer & Then I will not fail to see you both. In the meanwhile believe me,

Very truly yours,

Theodore Stanton,
26 West 61st St.,
New York.

DEPARTMENT OF LIBERAL ARTS,
SELIM H. PEABODY, Chief.
BUREAU OF MUSIC.
THEODORE THOMAS, WM. L. TOMLINS,
Musical Director. Choral Director.
GEORGE H. WILSON,
