Friday Mar 8

My dear friend.
I sent to Mr. Agnew from Detmer
because I understood you were to
be there at just that time
& I could not have
met you. I was sick
for Mrs. L. I was very
much disappointed too
but so glad you & Mother
enjoyed yourself so well.
I do take all care of my
self for that future is in
hope. But this weather kills
me if it was one long Summer
