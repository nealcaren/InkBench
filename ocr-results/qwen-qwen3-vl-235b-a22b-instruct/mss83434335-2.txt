Hartford, Octobr 19th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen pounds and seven pence
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1780
left, as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army. In behalf of Peter Cyrus
£.13..0..7 - Chas. Fanning Treas.

Voucher in Elias Lambert's file of this date.
