But first of all what
a night. -

The still lake. - the
pale white trees standing
in varied ranks of the
sides of the mountain
about it. - the dark lines
of peaks rising against
the quiet sky. - the deep
twinkling heaven. the
stars larger & brighter than
we see in the eastern
land, - our great camp
