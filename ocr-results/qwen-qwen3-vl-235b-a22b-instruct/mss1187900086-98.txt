Massachusetts Woman Suffrage Association,
3 PARK STREET.

Boston, July 21 1889.

Dear Frederick Douglass

When we first said your
appointment to Hoyt - and also has
many kind and beautiful things
the newspapers were saying about you
& began to clip and stick them with
the intention of sending them all on
to you - I cut "bits" of them. but alas!
in the huge piles of Editorial scraps
they here all disappeared but three
I enclose.

It was pleasant to see the
appreciation in which you are held.
while you are able to do it.

I am not sure that we are glad
to hear you Ed- We need you
here.

749
