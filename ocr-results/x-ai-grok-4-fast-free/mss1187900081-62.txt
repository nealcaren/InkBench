New York Oct 26 1858

E. Hussey

Mrs A L Byquist

Open your Husbands acct &
send me the design of it
for I want to get one to match
it exactly so as to make
a pair.  I am very anxious
to get them as soon as possible
If you have'nt the design by you
send the pitcher [picture?] & I can
have it done here

Truly your friend
J. D.

[Envelope address side]
Mrs A L Byquist
care of John Murphy
94 Nassau St
New York
