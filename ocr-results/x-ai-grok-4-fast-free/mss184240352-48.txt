State of Pennsylvania

County of Schuylkill

Administrators Notice

Estate of John Claycomb late of Schuylkill County
deceased Letters of Administration
having been granted
to the undersigned by the Register
of Wills in and for said County
all persons indebted to said Estate
are requested to make payment
and those having claims
to present the same without delay
to

John Claycomb

Administrator
with the will annexed

County of Schuylkill

Between the hours of 10 & 5

Octo 12th 1801

Aquila Wills
