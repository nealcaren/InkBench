Carrie Chapman Catt  
November 9, 1904  

Miss Alice McLelland  
60 W. 36th St.  
New York City  

Dear Alice  

In this morning's paper we see that Mrs. Dunn  
died yesterday. I told my husband  
and wrote to his sister both in the hospital  
waiting to undergo an operation,  
and were both very anxious as to the outcome.  
Every time we take up the paper  
or see anything about her we  
are reminded of how she suffered  
and how much we miss her.  
She was a friend, if ever there was  
one, and if there was no one during  
her last illness who had as much  
sympathy as we had just to know  
about her.  

With my sympathy, sorry as I am  
I cannot go to her just when  
I would most wish to express it,  
I send the enclosed loving  
message which perchance may interest  
you.  

Lovingly,  
Carrie Chapman Catt
