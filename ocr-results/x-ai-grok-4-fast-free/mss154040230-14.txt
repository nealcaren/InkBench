Traversal of F's letter Dec. 1913

Dear Sir:
I have referred your last letter to the
Commissioner of Internal Revenue. I have also sent
copies of the telegrams you have received from our
Argentine delegates to the Congress.  The report to
Congress is being completed, and those delegates
who have returned to the United States expect that it
will be ready for distribution some time in
January.  Copies of it will be sent to the delegates.
We hope that you will be able to hear our results
with reference to the possibility of publishing the
report of the Congress.
If you have any other inquiry which you may wish
to make on this subject, I shall be glad
to hear from you at any time.

Very truly yours,

Traversal of F's letter Dec. 1913
