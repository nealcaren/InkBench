Washington D.C.  
April 3rd 1869  

Dear Sutton  

Your reply to my letter was  
received yesterday.  

I was glad what you said in your letter  
discharged my mind of some other  
thought but much was not erased.  

I should have wrote but could not at first  
immediately. The following is it  

Heugelein has taken charge of the Howard University  
and has admitted 24 colored students
