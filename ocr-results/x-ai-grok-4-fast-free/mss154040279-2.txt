November 5, 1930  

My dear Mrs. Dal:  

I congratulate you sincerely on the  
award of Picture Review's prize. We who have received  
and admire your work are particularly pleased that so  
important a group as this has recognized it.  

Very sincerely yours,  

Mary V. Burnham  

Mrs. Grace H. Dal  
New York, N.Y.
