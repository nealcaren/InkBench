Dear Madam

I have just received

your letter & in reply to

your enquiry about the

account with Mr. Roberts

would say that the Department

has never had any

communication with him

on the subject.  The

first information we had

was from his letter to the

General in which he

enclosed a statement of

the account claiming

a balance due him

of $25.00 which statement

I have preserved & when

compared with our books

shows him indebted to

the Library in a much

larger amount than

he has ever acknowledged.

I think it would be

better for you to see

him personally with

the books & vouchers

in hand & endeavor

to adjust the matter

amicably.

Yours truly

H W Compton
