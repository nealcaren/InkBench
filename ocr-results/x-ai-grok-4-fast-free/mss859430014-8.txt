Oct. 6th 1857

My dear Daughter

My dear [?] still getting along
well I am still on the job [Paul?]
being heat despites the summer
fever weather Brother we are having
[school?] were here last night we
were [Daisy?] was not feeling
well during the last days the [doctor?]
her [pulse?] and she says it
[is?] better day by day she was all
that came so [enclosing?] a letter
last week and so from that day on
write whenever you can Direct
to you much love from us
Mother
Vienna.
