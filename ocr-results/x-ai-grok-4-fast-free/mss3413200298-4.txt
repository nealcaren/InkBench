Dear Mrs Vernon

Aug 1st 189?

I came away from Richmond  
I love motherless children, And trust God  
will bless this with His Spirit work in  
spite of my Past failure which I am sure  
This is my Hearts prayer for me  
There are many in the foundling Home  
who are ready. I hope in particular,  
this course to anchor, which I  
Thanks for your friendship, which  
I most keenly feel.  

Sincerely yours  
Mary Jane Dunwell
