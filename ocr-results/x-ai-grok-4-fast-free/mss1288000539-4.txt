Miss Alice N Blackwell
27. 4. 31
3 Monmouth St. Peabody Mass U.S.

My dear Miss Blackwell
much obliged for your
most kind thanks for my act in sending for "La Paloma" - music.
I found the translation so afflicting + beautiful
that music it would plant in my head - and last
I was tempted to make a very simple little song out
of it as one of my verses, like our print an.
If so from the verses; like for it our print in.
office with to you, in case it might be published.
