Marines and soldiers where the American flag flies unfurled
have been faithful and by their wise rules ought to be
called soldiers of liberty. They guard the inalienable rights of man and therefore cannot be bribed, in
defense of liberty. There would be less profits through interference with trade
and commerce and this would not be borne patiently without efforts
and infinite malice which it is the duty of citizens to resist to
the last ditch, and for each such would break our idols.
Carrie Chapman Catt
