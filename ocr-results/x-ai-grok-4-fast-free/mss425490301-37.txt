Published in the State of California by the Women of the Church of God in Christ to Combat the evils that have been created in the United States since 1906, and to serve the best interest of the women of the state both religiously and socially.  They have for the past twelve years had a State Matron who has given her full time to the work, and has built up a strong organization of women to combat the evils that have menaced the homes and good women of the state.  Since her election to the office of State Matron, Mrs. Lula C. Saunders of Los Angeles, has given her full time to the work and has built up a strong organization of women to combat the evils that have menaced the homes and good women of the state.  The women have done considerable work in the field of legislation, and have supported legislation that aims at the betterment of the women and children of the state.

The four women who have served as State Matron since 1918 are:

Mrs. Vachel E. Rhone      Los Angeles
Mrs. A. L. Johnson       Los Angeles
Mrs. Lula C. Saunders     Los Angeles
Mrs. Mary C. Odom        San Fernando

Mrs. Lula C. Saunders, the present State Matron, has given her full time to the work and has built up a strong organization of women to combat the evils that have menaced the homes and good women of the state.
