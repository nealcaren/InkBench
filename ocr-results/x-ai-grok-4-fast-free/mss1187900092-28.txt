St Augustine Fla  
Oct 19th 92  

Dear Grandson- I have learned with  
much pleasure that you are still  
in the city of Washington & intend  
to remain there awhile. Your duty,  
I am very glad  
that you have granted  
me such a pleasure after so long  
an absence from your dear  
Grandma. I hope that you are far  
happier now than you were when  
those matters could not be arranged  
as I would have wished them to be  
But as you remain in this  
iss all in you As remain in this  
92
