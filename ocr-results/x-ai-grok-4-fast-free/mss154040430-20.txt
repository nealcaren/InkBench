In this delicious and balmy spring weather our park is becoming very beautiful - It is a great treat to have it so near us. I can take the girls there almost every afternoon - The little ones like it very much & we can stay there an hour or two without fatigue - 

Our baby is five months old tonight - I think her the most beautiful child I ever saw. Do not mistake me - I mean the most beautiful baby - She is a little fairy - with the fairest skin and loveliest blue eyes - I think the color of her eyes will be a light blue - like papa's. 

Her first smile was at the sight of the moon through the window - and at the wreath of roses on the wall. She is so gentle and good - and so very quiet - I wish thee could see her - 

The other children are well and happy - The baby has had but one little ailment - a slight cold - which was soon cured. She is very fond of the children - and they of her - She knows their voices and smiles at them. 

Judge Benson is very kind to us - He has offered us the use of his garden - and his pony - and carriage - whenever we want them - We go there almost every afternoon - and enjoy it very much. 

We are all very much obliged to thee for thy kind remembrance of us - and for thy beautiful present to the baby - She is very proud of it - and wears it with great satisfaction. 

I hope thee art enjoying the spring in thy beautiful home - and that all thy loved ones are well - 

With much love from us all - I am thy affectionate friend - 

S. M. V.
