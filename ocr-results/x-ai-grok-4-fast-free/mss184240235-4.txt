My dear M. Archbald Esq.

I was grieved to learn of your

loss in the death of your

St. Francis on the twentieth

May – I came from the city the

last Tuesday on the Pennsylvania line.

I am truly sorry for

you that you are left without

this comfort. Your first letters

I shall look for with interest

when you return – that I have

made for you

by the Philadelphia class in

our religion – from your

friends in the city – I have been

following instructions given

before you left – the small

land ? & you delivered ? as

your project – a noble one
