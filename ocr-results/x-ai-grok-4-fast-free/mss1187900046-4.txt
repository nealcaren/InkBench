Jan 23d 1864  
Washington D.C.  

My Dear Father,  

I was glad to receive your  
letter & was glad to  
hear from you.  I am so  
sorry that you feel  
so unwell, I hope it will  
be all right there,  
she will take good care of you  
doctors there not any think of  
our home had a very  
pleasant winter.  

Your affectionate  
Son Geo.
