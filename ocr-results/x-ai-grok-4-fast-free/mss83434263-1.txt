DISCHARGE.

[Image of full-rigged sailing ship]

It is hereby certified No. 663

that William Gordon born in Ireland aged 21 years. 5 feet 8½ inches high light complexion blue eyes light hair having served in this Vessel two years and the Ship being about to be decommissioned from the U.S. Service has this day been honorably discharged from said Ship by reason of expiration of term of enlistment.

Character good.

Richmond Va. June 30 1865
