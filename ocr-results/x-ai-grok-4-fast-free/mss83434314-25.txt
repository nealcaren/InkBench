Indorsements of letters received should be entered in a book kept for that purpose, to be presented to the Committee at a Special meeting during the temporary absence of the Secretary, and the book returned after the interview, after which the letters are to be filed alphabetically by the Secretary.

1. Every letter received should be entered in the letter book on the day of receipt, noting the date of the letter and the page on which it is filed. Questions will be asked in the Committee about letters not so entered.

Indorsements of letters received should be made immediately upon opening of mail. If the Secretary is absent on account of illness or other cause, the Chief Clerk will make the indorsements, referring the number of the letter and the page on which it is filed to the Secretary.
