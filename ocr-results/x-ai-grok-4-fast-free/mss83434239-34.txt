Pope's Wells 15 miles northwest of the junction
of Pope's Creek & Pecos River northeast of Carlsbad.

Beginning in the spring of 1855 some 70 soldiers
under the direction of Lt.  John Pope drilled under
the southern transcontinental railroad route to
supply water for the troops.  The well was carried to a
depth of 1600 ft. but eventually all work was abandoned.
