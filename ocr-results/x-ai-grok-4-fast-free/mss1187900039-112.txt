Decr 24th

Dear Papa

Since our account of the trip was given
to you we have had another of your letters & I hope
that you will not be at all apprehensive about it, having
so kept your appointment with two or three friends.
Your mind about the affair with him, with my
own doing your mind & affair with since you do
not the first white trees and of a kind that I spoke of
in Europe will not thrive well after if you will be home
any length of time. I will write again soon from home
I am coming. Your Affectionate

Sarah
