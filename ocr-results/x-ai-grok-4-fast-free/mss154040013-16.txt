The barber brings his stool and basin, ready to do a large amount of the shaving and hair cutting usual in the open streets. Each of these quick barbers has his own stool, basin, combs, brushes, scissors, razors, and knives. Razors for shaving goats, skins, skeletons of every little beast that is eaten, are sold in the shops; along with birds'-nests, sharks' fins, and all the queer articles of food and medicine used by the Chinese.
