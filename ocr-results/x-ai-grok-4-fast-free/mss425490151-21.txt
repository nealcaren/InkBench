NATIONAL WOMAN'S PARTY  
Washington, D. C.  

October 29, 195  

Dear Mr. Chairman,  

The report of the Credentials Committee, signed by Dr. Harsh, Chair-  
man, has [seated delegates] from all over the country [presented] in their Nominating Com-  
mittee. [Some] were named. Other names were written in by the Nominating  
Committee to serve, and were elected.  

All will be ready on the first meeting of the Nominating Com-  
mittee, which all Chairmen are members of the National  
[Executive Council].  

[We are] thanking the three Committees, and the undersigned, for their  
[efforts].  

Our Chairmen [owe no small debt for their] greatest efforts. We regret [that] Miss Paul will be  
[unable to attend, but she will be] [representing the] National Woman's Party. Miss Paul will give the  
[address].  

[Alice] Allan's widow has all qualifications for the heavy duties she is  
undertaking. Resolutions are [passed] so we may succeed [in our fight] for Rights. At  
least the New Council will [meet] at the call of Miss Paul.  

Gratefully yours,  

Caroline F. Purston Bray
