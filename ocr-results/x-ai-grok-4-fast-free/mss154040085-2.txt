COOK COUNTY HOSPITAL  
Chicago 12, Illinois  

December 8, 1943  

Mrs. Daryl Clemens  
1520 North Avenue  
Chicago, Illinois  

Dear Mrs. Clemens:  

I wish to express my appreciation of the committee's gift of the book "History of the Lemons, Inc."  

From all reports I was glad to receive a copy of Tuesday's proceedings.  

Personally, our club has done much to inspire every young girl who is choosing medicine as a career.  

You can see, therefore, how happy I am that we have your membership in our organization.  

With kindest regards, I am  

Truly yours,  

Millie B. Rakestraw  
Chief Executive Secretary
