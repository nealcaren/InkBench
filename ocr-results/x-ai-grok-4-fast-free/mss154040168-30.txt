LES LIE WOMAN SUFFRAGE COMMISSION, INC.

OFFICERS Carrie Chapman Catt PRESIDENT DIRECTORS

TELEPHONE Bryant 3654 171 Main St. Ames, Iowa.

Miss Clara M. Hyde,

New York, August 5, 192-.

Dear Miss Hyde:

I am in receipt of your letter of recent date in which you told me of a young woman who desires a position as stenographer.  I do not know if she is a competent stenographer.

She has been out of practice for ten years.  I do not believe she is the kind of stenographer we are looking for.

You said you would try to find her better than she is.

This book: Everything about here to be found?

August Sunday. There may be another season.

Thanks for expecting you to pay about the week.

etc.

lovingly yours,

Carrie Chapman Catt

President
