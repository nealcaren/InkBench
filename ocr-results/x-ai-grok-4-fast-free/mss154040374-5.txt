Mrs. Mary P. Beard,
September 28, 1897.

My dear Mrs. Beard,

Now I am only trying to fill some deficiencies
in my library and in yours. There were some books which
appeared only in your list and not in the
fragile as they are those books and my trying,

Then if I have any of those books of my collection
which thou wouldst like to have I will be very
glad to give them to the library. With the exception
of those for which I think there is no charge for wear,
but I think there is charge for rent.

I have consulted you no disposition of my collection until

Sincerely,
O D W.
