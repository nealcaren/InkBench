August 1, 1863 with Peter H. V. Hagner State Dept to J. P. Benjamin with duplicate of letter of June 29 and copy of report of Committee of House of Representatives on the subject of the arrest of C. L. Vallandigham. Also a copy of the opinion of the Attorney General and of the case of John Merryman.

This letter was recieved by Mr. Benjamin and was published in the Richmond Enquirer and Examiner in the summer of 1864.

J. P. Benjamin to Judah P. Benjamin Confederate Secretary of State.
