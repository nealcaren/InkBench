I was truly sorry
to hear of the death of thy dear
Mother - This death has oceurred
just as I was looking for the letter
from thee & Hally assuring us that she was
getting better.  I was at the office today
& saw many new flowers from
the greenhouse.  I think of thee & Hally in this sad
bereavement & pray that the Lord may sustain
& comfort you both.
I send thee some of the new flowers for thy use
as well as to remind thee of my love for
thee.
Truly thy frd
Hannah
