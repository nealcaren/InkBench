I enclose the purse, and I trust it will please
thee and have thy approval as well as
Mr. B.'s. He has been in New York for two weeks, as there
is much uneasiness manifested in
his condition. For thy long & sympathetic
letter I thank thee; it must suggest that Phineas
knew all that I had to say to him.
I began to pay him, but the
grocer sent his bill to me
a large one; sent it to me
Now my husband requests
[it?].

Yours truly
J. M. Valentine
