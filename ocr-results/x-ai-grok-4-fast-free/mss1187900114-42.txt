World’s Columbian Exposition  
Department of Agriculture  
Chicago Ills May 24, 1893  

#70-380  

Honorable Frederick Douglass,  
Commissioner,  
Haitian Pavilion,  
Jackson Park.  

Dear Sir:  

I have the honor to advise you of the completion of plans  
for a series of platform talks, limited to thirty minutes each, on  
subjects of Forestry, Agriculture and Live Stock, connected with the  
lectures on Forestry, but extending to a broader field.  

They begin June 20th and will be given and continued daily through  
the Assembly Hall, opposite the Agricultural Building for this purpose, in connection with the Agricultural  

Department.  

It is our utmost desire for the requested results from these talks  
that you will do the great honors to be present, and to all  

me the names of such members, and assist me greatly, or send men
