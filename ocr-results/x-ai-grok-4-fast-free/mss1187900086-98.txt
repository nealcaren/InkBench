Massachusetts Woman Suffrage Association,

3 Park Street.

Boston, July 21, 1883

Dear Mr. Drew Sr.

We have your letter of

the 19th and also have

many kind and beautiful things

to say for your intention

of sending them. All our

friends are sure that

it is the end of so much

suffrage but there

are 5 of these pleasant[er]

to be affection with child on it.

Will not be so late as on good

for us. Mr. McC. here is

under your G. McC.

yours truly
