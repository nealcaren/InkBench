La Porte, Ind. 9 mo. 18 '62

Dear Hamma:

Yesterday we went on a pleasant trip
to Little Bay. It was an
all day trip. The Lady Ann
left early breakfast, 9 a.m. We
all had lunch in a field came over a
steam launch, that was built
for the 25 of them. It carried
20 of us. The engine driver
sat it in the middle, with
wood (heaped a steam engine
fired by wood) the back
