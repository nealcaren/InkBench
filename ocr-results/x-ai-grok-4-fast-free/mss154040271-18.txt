Dear Mary

I have just finished a book which I thought would interest you – a spring book by the author of So Big – I can't remember her name.  When I read the last chapter, I was so interested in what had happened to the people in the book that I had to get the book to find out.  They live in my garden – the tulips and lilies and all the other flowers are full of little blossoms just like my garden.  It is wonderful how you get a garden out of a book.
