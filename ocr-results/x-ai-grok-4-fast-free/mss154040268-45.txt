To the Tribune Bureau.

Oct. 18/10

Mr. Graves,

Thankful for your kind letter,

I raise my voice in quiet protest against the

misuse of my name. Please don't appropriate any more

of my money through Wilbur or for any cause.

Having vile policies held us in the placidity of

ignorance shows what cruel torments lie. The New York, Oct. 18

is a daily plea. The Jew.

Tho. Sphinx.

Th. Dervy
