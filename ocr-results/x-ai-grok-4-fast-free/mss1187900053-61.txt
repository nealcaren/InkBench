My dear friend

since we have for a very long time written to
you or heard from you
hoping for a reply. We really are
anxious to know how your little
cold affects you with his
dysentery, and if you have used
a hair tonic that you
mentioned over the quality plant was
at all. I hope that you report
well yourself of your family
