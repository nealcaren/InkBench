SOCIETY FOR PROMOTING  
THE RETURN OF WOMEN AS POOR LAW GUARDIANS.  

WOMEN AS POOR LAW GUARDIANS.  

Because the larger number of Paupers are Women and children.  

Because the training up of Pauper girls requires the aid of women. These advantages have been shown in Poor Law Unions where women are elected.  

Because there are many women ratepayers they should be represented on the Board and can give careful attention to business.  

Because it is the women only who understand household management  

For these reasons secure the election of a fair proportion of women ratepayers as  

Poor Law Guardians. The aid of all is earnestly requested by the Society.  

An annual subscription of 5s. is requested by the Society.  

For information, Mrs. O’Brien, 18, South Kensington.  

Published by the Society for Promoting the return of Women as  
Poor Law Guardians.
