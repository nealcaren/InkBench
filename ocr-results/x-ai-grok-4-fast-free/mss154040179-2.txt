Mrs. J. Hayne Irvin  
May 5,1907  

Dear Mrs. Irvin,  

I am sorry your circular letter finds me  
doing nothing, at least for  
the time being, due to difficulty  
to pursue both health. I want  
to say to you that my husband has no desire  
to take a vacation. He is able.  

If he does not feel that the archive is required in June  
he will not trouble himself over the problem of ensuring  
providing the venture very sincerely, in  

G.O.C.  
Gary Chapin
