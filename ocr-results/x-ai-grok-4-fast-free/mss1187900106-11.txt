J. C. K.

STATE OF WASHINGTON

J. B. K.

1610. Olympia, Wash., Washington,

Aug 7, 1892.

Dear Daughter:

I am now West on my first visit up in my life
to see your sister, Belle, the wife of H. D. Scott who
holds his official duties at Olympia in the
Thurston County, & they call it here. I am
about in perfect health in my left arm
until I here again  
Dr. Blaine is the surgical officer two months
ago who extracted the cataract from my
left eye, & then cauterized the cornea for the former
inflammation. Some time ago I let the doctor
to my left eye applied drops with the aid of
forceps. I can see almost as well as ever. Thanks to your
kind discovery and skill for my eyes out of which I may
become naturally sighted.
