Washington March 10th 1857

Dear Father

Your letter received March 10th

Mrs P receives your kind wishes regularly twice

a week - which we keep with confidence that God will

restore her health - He is anxious just now about his.

Georgia delegation called on him as

he has been appointed Col Taylor's

Asst. - A Field officer in the name

of the Wisconsin. The political

aspect is looking badly between Mason & Hunter

Congress has received the report for

the President's Office & is now in

his hands - The President & Mr.

Breckinridge wish confirmation - Mr. Fales they

expected he would be rejected.

1857
