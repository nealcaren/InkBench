Stevens House, 23 Broadway.

My dear Friend,

I have nothing new to communicate since my last, and no instructions of any importance to give thee. Thy words of encouragement are like diamonds to me. Thy many kind offices of a public nature I can never forget. Much of my time at present is taken up with the concerns of the 3d day. It will be with pleasure that I shall comply with thy request as soon as I can. Present my kindest regards to thy husband, and to all friends. In much love to thee, I remain thy sincere friend,

S.D. (or similar signature, partially legible)
