The John Drew Theatre  
A. M. Street Theatre  
Built by Randolph Gardiner & Associates  
New York, N.Y. Oct. 22 1909  

My Dear Mrs Sherman  

As your affairs are an open subject with you I take the  
liberty of enclosing a check for $50 which I trust  
will be of service to you.  

This has a special claim upon you, having been made possible  
through the kindness of Mr. Caleb J. Morse, who, I am certain,  
would be more than pleased to know that it is to be used in  
helping you.  

I trust a receipt for same has been forwarded by this time  
to Mr. Morse.  

You have a signal honor (at least) belonging to you  
which I would like to share, if you would permit me  
to send you a small pamphlet which gives an account  
of it.  

I shall take the liberty of doing so before long.  

Yours very truly,  

Randolph Gardiner
