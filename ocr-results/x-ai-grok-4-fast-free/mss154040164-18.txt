My dear Friend,
I dont think I can possibly convey by this my very great
pleasure & satisfaction with thy graphic
letter ye'd written - ye told it
so clearly that it was a conversation
with thee last Easter & me
The Christian's joy has passed from
me.  Their hearts that trouble
in the future, must my grief summon
as I have not heard from thee a thing
for thirty years & a week or two
& no time at it.  My friend
that old London fashion I did get
unusually good by each in Southampton
