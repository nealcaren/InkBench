Mrs. Howard
Lynn Mass Sept

Dear Madam

Enclosed you will
find $2500 which I send to
be applied to my account for the
year. I have my address below one
this paper through a friend but
shall have one of my
own now. So that I have one of my
own I give yours.

I received your letter Let
me thank you for your courtesy & 
kindness & for your friendly criticism 
of my own. I feel more 
encouraged by your comments.
