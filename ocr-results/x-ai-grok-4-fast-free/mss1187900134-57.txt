Frederick Robeson.N.Y.Sept.10,187.

Dear Madam,

We are in receipt of your favor of the 8ᵗʰ inst. enclosing two sketches for monuments.

The contracts are to be awarded in a few days, and if you wish to submit a proposal please send it to our office at once. A regular form of contract is inclosed, and if selected

you will please fill in on the reverse side your proposal, and return same to us. We will then return one marked "accepted" with two very heavy leaves

of tracing paper, on which you can trace your design, one to be used in cutting the monument and one to be retained by us.

You will please fill this out better than the other so as

to meet the claim for $500. and return

the contract with claim for same, and we

will send you the plan and form start order on you. hands,

and we shall take pride in executing it in the finest way, and

hoping to hear from you Yours respectfully,

J. Weston
