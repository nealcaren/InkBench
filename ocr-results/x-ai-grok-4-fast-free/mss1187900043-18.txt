Department of State
Washington, 6 July, 1871.

Sir:

Your letter to the President of the 6th instant, enclosing the Legislative resolution of the District of Columbia, for his acceptance, as a member of the Board of Trustees of the Columbian University, has been referred to this Department, and the President directs me to say, that he accepts the trust, to take effect six days after the 6th instant.

I am, Sir, Your obedient servant
Hamilton Fish
