You're Welcome

The new Women's Department, which is in the center of the main floor at our Broadway office, has been equipped with every facility for the convenience of women customers. It includes three private consultation rooms and is staffed by a corps of trained personnel familiar with the special problems of women in their financial affairs. Whether it is opening an account, making investments, or securing loans, the Women's Department offers personalized service in a pleasant atmosphere. We invite you to visit at your convenience.
