Both these wonders are in Ceylon and
many more. In Egypt we attended
the ceremony of the Holy Carpet. A
carpet is made annually, and 300
soldiers are assigned to escort it to
Mecca. There it is placed in the Holy of
Holies and remains a year. The escort
bring back the carpet [?] which
has now become holy & it is received
by the Khedive. Thousands of
people stood in great crowds where
it came. I never saw so quiet and
orderly a crowd. I could only account
for it by the story that they were spell
bound with religious awe. The carpet
came on a richly caparisoned
camel mounted by a small rich
temple. The camel is holy too. It was
followed by seven camels which
