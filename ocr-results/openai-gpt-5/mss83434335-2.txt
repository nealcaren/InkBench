Hartford, Octot 19th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen pounds, and seven pence —
it being the Balances due to [me,] Peter Cyrus on the first Day of January 178[?]
as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army... In behalf of Peter Cyrus
Chs. Fanning Suet.

£13..0..7 —

Voucher in Elias Lambert’s Rec’t of this date.
