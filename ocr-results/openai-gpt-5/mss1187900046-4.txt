jan 22 1874.

Washington D C

My Dear father.
I have received your
kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you, but I am so
sorry That you feel.
So. a bout going to
Rochester, i hope it will
be all Right thair.
when you go thair. I
Shill take good care of your
letters and not think of
distroying them in any way,
we have had a few very
Cold days but we have
very pleasant weather.
