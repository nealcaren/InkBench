[ORIGINAL RECEIPT.]

CLAIM NO. 38.

COLUMBIA, S. C., 1864

Received of the State of South Carolina Eighteen hundred dollars,

(1800.)

being the amount admitted and advanced by the General Assembly of the said State, upon the claim of Jessie Briggs, for compensation for his slave, named Charles, lost by reason of the employment of the said slave by the authorities of the Confederate Government upon the military fortifications in this State.
