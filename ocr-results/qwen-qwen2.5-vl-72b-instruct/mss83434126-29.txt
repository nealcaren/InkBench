March 30th 1866 - Issued to Bvt. Major A. R. Buffington Commanding U.S. Arsenal at Baton Rouge La. the following Ordnance Stores as per Inventory and Inspection Report of Bvt. Major Gen. J. F. E. Sherman dated March 20th 1866.

8. Eight Springfield Rifled Muskets Cal. 58.
7. Seven Cartridge Boxes Cal. 58.
7. Seven Bayonet Scabbards
8. Eight Gun Slings
9. Nine Screw Drivers and Cone Wrenches
10. Ten Canteens
3. Three Hammer and Band Spring Punches
5. Five Waist Belts (Pats)

Sent the invoices through the Post Office Dept. and Received my Receipts April 12th bearing date April 4th 1866.

Stephen Newman
1st Lieut. Co. "K" 81st U.S.C. Inf.
Commanding Company K

April 30th 1866. Charged on the Muster and Pay Rolls of Co. "K" 81st U.S. Colored Infantry the following Ordnance Stores.
Bvt. Henry Des Anges. One Complete Set of Adj. Accountant Auglts.
