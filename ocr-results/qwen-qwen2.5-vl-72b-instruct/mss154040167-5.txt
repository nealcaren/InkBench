**Postcard Front:**

Portugal — Vianna do Castelo — Costumes portuguezes

**Postcard Back:**

Correspondence

We are fairly on our way. The ship and the company satisfactory. We left promptly at three and will keep stopping at Cherbourg this evening when this will get mailed. All is well with us and you will get a letter before we are over. It is very cold today—nothing tropical about it—

Address

Miss Clara Hyde  
317 W 45th St
