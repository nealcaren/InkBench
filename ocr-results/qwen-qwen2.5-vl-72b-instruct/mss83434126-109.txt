1859 Jan 28th William Wrench Russell, American historian dies aged, 

1859 Feb 23rd Gen. Miguel Miramon, elected President of Mexico by the revolutionary junta Jan 6th abdication of Zuloaga.

1859 April 11th Entrance of Gen. Miramon into the city of Ayacucho - he assumes the presidency and governs without respect to law, life, or property.

1859 July 13th Juarez, constitutional President of Mexico consumes the ecclesiastical property of his country.

1859 Oct 31st James G Mason United States Minister to France died at Paris.

1859 Oct 17th John Brown with twenty one companions crossed the Potomac River at Harper's Ferry and entered Virginia where he and his followers incited the slaves to rebel, and to take up arms against their masters. After a short time "Brown" was captured and tried and found guilty of Treason and conspiring and advising with slaves and others to rebel, and murder in the first degree - for which he was executed on Friday 2nd day of Dec. at Charlestown.

1859 Oct 23rd Incisive battle between the forces of the Argentine Republic and of Buenos Aires.
