Much will depend on the Steamer's. We tried to get the sailings but most Company's clerks yet know about the January Steamers. Mrs. Bakker van Bosse of Holland wants to come. You met her at the Amsterdam Study Conference. She is a very good speaker and speaks very good English. I spoke to some Belgian women today who might go. The real difficulty will be that they all will want to come over for the days of work in Washington, but they


**Page 5**

cannot take extra time to do sightseeing in New York. However, some may be able to do that. They specially will want to come and see you at any rate. When we go to Stockholm in October we might possibly reach some more women who will want to go. We are also trying to get some younger women, but most of them have jobs and could not afford the expense journey.
