Port of Spain.
Mar 26, 1923.

My dear Mollie:-

I gave you cheap peace for a
cable and none is here. She remains
another day but I suspect that progress
is in the direction of frequency and that you
have not cabled at all.

Mar 27. I was interrupted here and now several
days have passed. Your cable has been received, and it was such
a relief to get it. Imagine you passing through
that discouraging stage when you think you
never will be well again and when life has
lost all its flavor. But keep up your courage
and you'll pull through. I am so glad
you are better. This world would be a
black old place without you dear Mollie.
