**THE EAGLE MOUNTAIN HOUSE**
**WHITE MOUNTAINS - JACKSON, NEW HAMPSHIRE**

Aug 9th/1949

Dear Emma Alice:

When Ethel went to Huntington I went out to spend the day—Now it was about two weeks after she had gone—She had failed steadily—I spoke to Bruce & said that perhaps I had better write you—Then realized that she had cancer & that she could not get well—Bruce said that he had told Howard & that he would

*Cross-written note:* She would not have wanted you to worry

*Cross-written note:* Call Mrs. Price

*Cross-written note:* She refused to go to Dr. Emery
