Well on Slavery we old abolitionists are all right side up with Care. But now the very principles apply in the case of rum, and I have noticed that what few of the old abolitionists are living are mostly prohibiting themselves. God also right is on the side of temperance that is sure. Attend the W.C.T.U. in Phil. Their annual meeting. A grand organization. Miss Frances E. Willard is certainly one of the grandest.
