New Haven, Aug. 15, 1890.

Dear Mrs. Douglass:

Judge Sheldon has just informed me that his family had received recently a letter from you, and that you are in Washington. We fancied that you were in Boston with Mr. Douglass, and so none of us knew how to address a line either to you or to him.
