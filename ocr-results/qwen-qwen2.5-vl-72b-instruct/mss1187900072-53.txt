Charlotte writes that she received an invitation from  
Mrs. Jane Maria Mitchell,  
to attend an Alumnae gathering in Washington, before  
which Stephen Foster's daughter Ada, had been invited to  
read a paper. Charlotte listened to her address with  
absorbing interest for her parents' sake as well as for her own,  
and said it was an exhaustively descriptive of the government  
of all the Colleges that are worthy of the name.  
Forty-eight in twenty-one states. Miss Foster is a teacher in the  
Girls High School in Boston where my Cousin, Quisten of  
the one who died, spoken of above, also teaches.
