I wrote Car—there, instead of 54th if you can. The facts of the two are that 54th went in with over a thousand men in excellent condition as to arms, equip. Went & Beale was exposed to moderate fire—next morning found all but 100 men on duty & 3 officers only, slightly wounded. The 1st North Carolina were double quickened the last mile—came into the hottest fire of the whole action—a few was only from the fresh enemy—poor guns—and only 600 at the most—came out 68 killed 131 = 199—three officers eight killed—seventeen severely wounded.
