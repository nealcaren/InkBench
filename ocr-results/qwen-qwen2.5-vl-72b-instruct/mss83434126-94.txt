1828  
May 13th. Tariff Bill, imposing heavy duties on British goods.

1829  
Feb 27th. Battle of Tarqui in which the Peruvians are defeated by the Colombians.

March 4th. Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun Vice President.

Sept 11th. A Spanish expedition for the recovery of Mexico sails from Havana. July 5th – It surrenders to the Mexican Gen. Santa Anna.

1830  
July 4th. A treaty concluded between the United States and the Ottoman Porte – the ports of the United States are again closed to British commerce.

Dec. 17th. Simon Bolivar, a South American and the liberator of Bolivia from the rule of Spain died aged 47 years.

1831  
April 6th. Abdication of Pedro I, Emperor of Brazil in favor of his infant son Dom Pedro.

Nov. 17th. Venezuela, New Granada, and Colombia So. America again became separate states.
