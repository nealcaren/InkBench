1601 Mt. Vernon Street,
Philadelphia.
Oct 23rd 95

My dear Susie-
I saw of your journey
on my way home from work, though I
heard you were pleased with it. and to
have met some kilpseas[i] with its purpose
last np[t]h. I had it long fealtus– he
was cued leam & bustled with me
in launching it alele. Joo chewed
delire– if throughout the country it will
do just good

Yours truly
