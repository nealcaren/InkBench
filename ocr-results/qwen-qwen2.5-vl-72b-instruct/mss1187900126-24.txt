Rochester Oct 14-94

My Dear Brother Frederick,

This is a fine crisp cold morning, and while the girls are taking their last nap I will pen a line to you. An hour in the morning is about the only real quiet time I have, the rest of the day is so full of work and duties. We are all settled down to our easy city life.
