No. 1.

Quartermaster General's Office,
PROPERTY DIVISION,
Washington, D.C., January 27th, 1866.

To Major General J. G. Foster

Sir:

Your Return of Quartermaster's Stores as stated, for the
month of December, 1863,
has been received, and, when examined, will be sent to the Third
Auditor of the Treasury for settlement, of which you will be duly
notified.

By order of the Quartermaster General:

Very respectfully,
Your obedient servant,
Col. O. O. Howard,
Col. Q.M. Dept. Q.M. Genl. Staff.
