Hd Qrs 8th Regt U. S. C. I.  
Near Chappin's Farm Va  
January 17th 1865  

---

**[Left Panel]**

Bailey Romezo C.  
Capt 8th U. S. C. I.  
Colerd Regt.  

Requests a leave of absence for twenty (20) days for the purpose of visiting Brigg Penn. on business.  

---

**[Middle Panel]**

Respectfully forwarded.  
I certify That the within ap-  
plication will not make thealtonents on leave or furlough, from  
this Reg and keep one retention (26) of its efficient strength: that  
I do not belong to a one year kept  
that I have not returned from a leave of absence since the 1st of  
April 1864; that I have been in the service more than one year.  
That my term of service does not expire before the lst of May 1865; that I have never been  
tried or convicted of any offence by a court Martial: that I am  
a member of a Regt raised in the State of Pennsylvania.  
The number of officers present in my Regt is 20  
Number Absent is 12  
The number of enlisted men  
present in my command is 538  
Number Absent is 256  

Romezo C. Bailey  
Capt. 8th U. S. C. I.  
Colerd Regt.
