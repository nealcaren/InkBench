**Logansport, Ind., Oct 7th 1888**

Miss Anna Dickinson  
Care The National Republican Committee  
New York City W.Y.

My Dear Miss Dickinson:

Your valued note of the 6th inst. was duly received.

I have expressed your thanks to Mr. Brown for the Indiana Journal reference & to Mr. Brown for the more extended notice of your address in our Logansport Journal.

Mother, my brothers are well and Tom says in expressing our high regard & consideration for you and yours.

I was yesterday at Indianapolis where we had a momentous demonstration. We hope some of your friends will have regret Indiana after Nov. 6. I have noted the influence impressed you have made where you have been since leaving here.

and feel confidence that your efforts will not be without fruitage in Indiana as well as Michigan New York.

Very Respectfully Yours  
H.G. Mason
