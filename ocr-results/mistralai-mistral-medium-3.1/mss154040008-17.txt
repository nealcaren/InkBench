2 Immense gatherings, while the shouts & many religions greet each other. The Buddha has forbidden his followers to eat meat, keeping his birthday by eating rice and bananas. The people are very merry, and the whole town is hung with banners and lanterns. The Chinese go to the temples of Fo, where the priests receive them and make them welcome. The bonzes are very numerous, and there are also a curious sect of female bonzes, who live in convents. The Chinese have a great many superstitious customs. When a man is about to found a house, he consults the astrologers, and if they say it is a bad day, he will not begin. The King of Cochin China is a Buddhist, and he has a great many pagodas. The bonzes are a very powerful body, and the King is much influenced by them.
