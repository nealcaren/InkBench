Discharge.

[Center Text:]
This is to certify, That No. 503, Spencer Bair, a Landsman,
has this day been discharged from the U.S. Steamer "Bienville"
and from the naval service.

Dated this 11th day of May 1865.

[Left Bottom Corner:]
Approved:
J.R. Mullany,
Captain, Commanding

To Ge[orge] C. Brinckle,
to John H. Brinckle,
Philadelphia
