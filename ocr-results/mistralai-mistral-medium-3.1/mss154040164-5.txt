Both these wonders are in Cairo and many more. In Egypt I attended the ceremony of the Holy Carpet. A carpet is made annually, and 300 soldiers are assembled to escort it to Mecca. There it is placed in the holy of holies and remains a year. The count bringing back the carpet (the year) before which has now become holy it is received by the Khedive. Thousands of people stand in great crowds to see it come. I never saw so quiet and orderly a crowd. I counted only account for it by the theory that they were spell bound rich religious awe. The carpet came on a richly caparisoned camel mounted by a small rich temple. The camel is holy too.
