It seems like a nice old lady too to enlarge upon now - very much we enjoyed your visit though, and how glad we should be should you welcome you; should you be tempted once more to cross the waste of waters. Here we are all quite well but amounts some of our relations and friends, sickness and death have been present. A dear cousin of mine, Miss Lamb, who remembered your visit to Belfast forty-two years ago, and who was here to welcome you last
