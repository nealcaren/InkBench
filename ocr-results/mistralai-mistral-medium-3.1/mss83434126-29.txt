March 30 1866 Issued to Bvt Major A. R. Buffington
Commanding U.S. Arsenal at Baton Rouge La.
the following Ordnance Stores, as per Inventory and
Inspection Report of Bvt Major Gen'l T.J. Sherman,
dated March 27th 1866.

8. Eight Springfield Rifled Muskets Cal .58
7. Seven Cartridge Boxes. Cal .58
7. Seven Bayonet Scabbards
8. Eight Gun Slings
9. Nine Screw drivers and cone wrenches
10. Ten Wipers
3. Three Tumblers and Main Spring Vices
5. Five Waist belts (Plates)

Sent the Invoices through the Post Office
Dept. and Received my Receipts April 12th bearing date
April 4th 1866.

Stephen D Neumann
1st Lieut Co. K 81st U.S.C. Inf.
Commanding Company K

April 30th 1866. Charged on the Muster and
Pay Rolls of Co "K" 81st U.S. Col'd Infantry
the following Ordnance Stores.

Post Henry De Russy One Complete set of Inf. Accoutrements.
