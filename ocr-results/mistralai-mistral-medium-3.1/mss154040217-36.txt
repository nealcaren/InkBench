Much will depend on the Steamer's. I tried to get the sailings but most Company's don't yet know about the January Steamers. Mrs. Baker or Bosse of Holland wants to come. You met her at the Amsterdam Study Conference. She is a very good speaker and speaks very good English. I spoke to some Belgian women to day who might go. The real difficulty will be that they all will just come over for the days of York in Washington, but they

cannot take time to do sightseeing in New York. However, some may be able to do that. They specially want to come and see you at any rate.

When we go to Stockholm in October we might possibly reach some more women who might want to go. We are also trying to get some younger women, but most of them have jobs and could not afford the expense of journey.
