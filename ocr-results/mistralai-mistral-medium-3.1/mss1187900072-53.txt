Charlotte writes that she received an invitation from
Mrs. Jane (Maria Mitchell's niece) to attend an Alumnae gath-
ering in Washington, before which Stephen Foster's daughter
Alice had been invited to read a paper. Charlotte
listened to her address with absorbing interest for her parents'
sake as well as for her own, and said it was an elegant
description of the government of all the Colleges that
are worthy of the name -
Party eight in twenty-one States.
