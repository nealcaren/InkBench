Newport, R.I. Monday, Aug 29th

My dear Mr. Douglass,

Champney told me the effort
to get me to the Newport Casino
last evening was unsuccessful.
I regret it as I should like to have
your impressions regarding -
Torrey's third Thursday lecture.
