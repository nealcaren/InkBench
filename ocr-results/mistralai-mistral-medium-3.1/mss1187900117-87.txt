This note was written here. He is still strong and vigorous in his eighty fifth year. His four last lectures - two of which he gave recently in the home of Judge Berry in our city - were very interesting.

We have very pleasant memories of our call made upon you, upon our way home from Florida, and hope we may have the pleasure of a visit from you when you come again to Boston.

We have a fine Cabinet Photo of yourself, and wish we had one also of Mrs. Douglas, to whom Mr. [illegible] feels very strongly attracted.

I hope this may find both in excellent health and that we may hear from you.
