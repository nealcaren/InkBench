Her purse is the real thing &
to the active suit, &
mostly ends me.
A month
since I dimmed my eyes
to good for a troublesome old
head.
Miscellaneous looks
delightfully fresh, & clear, &
green, & rolling.
I should like one
at once.
I wrote Uncle Nightingale Mann
often here & both look
to see the horse. I kind
of wish he might.
I was more than glad to
get my mammas sealed written
when she had little Clara at Aurora.
