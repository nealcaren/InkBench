Directions for chicken.

Scrape off the jelly and nice fat from a table-spoonful of drippings, add salt, pepper to taste, stir well and drop chicken in while boiling. Have a skillet hot; melt butter or bacon fat and add quickly fried chicken. Pour the chicken fat over the chicken. Pour the nice brown gravy into the pan, skim off the cream and add a bit of flour, thicken with a little flour.
