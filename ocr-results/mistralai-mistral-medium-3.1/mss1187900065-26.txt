(The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Letterhead)

Headquarters: Fourth Ave. cor. 22d St.

New York, Jan 28, 1885

Hon. Frederick Douglass,
House of Representatives, Washington D.C.

Dear Sir,

Yesterday, deposited with the Senator from Hudson Co. New Jersey, the Will of the late Ottilia Assing, along with a letter from me declaring to serve as executor, copy of which I forward to you with this.

You will please take such action in the matter of supplying a substitute, as you deem proper, and believe me

Yours with great respect,
Henry Bergh
