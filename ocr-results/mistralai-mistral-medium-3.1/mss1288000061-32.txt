Georgieville, P.E. Island
Aug. 20, 1897

Dear Papa & Mama:
   Yesterday we went on a pleasant picnic to
Fitch Bay. It was an all-day trip. We had an
early breakfast, and then the Holbrooks came over,
and we all piled into a little steam-launch, chartered
for the occasion. It carries 25, & there were 25
of us. The engine divides it in the middle, with
wood heaped up on each side (fancy a steam engine
