Kansas City, Fort Scott & Memphis Railroad Co.
Marion Arkansas
September 21st 1888

Hon Frederick Douglass
Washington D.C.

Dear Sir,

I am an Union Soldier and have Endeavored to be a consistent Republican ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the civil war and in my humble sphere in life have been a friend to the colored race.
