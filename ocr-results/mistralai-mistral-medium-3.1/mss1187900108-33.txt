OFFICE OF
C. B. Barnes,
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Cuthbert, Ga., 189[?]

Printed Sir, Your name
is on the list. I wish you
would send me one of them
now Sir, Bangs[?] I wish you
to do me this favor
as I have spent a great deal
of money this year for the
Republican Cause, and is now
greatly in debt for the same.
And the Republican Executive
Committee have sent a petition
to Mr. Granamalus[?], Post M. Gens[?]
asking the appointment of
Mr. Charles Paxton to the
Post Office at this place
(Cuthbert Ga). This will help
me to get my home for
the money spent for the last.
