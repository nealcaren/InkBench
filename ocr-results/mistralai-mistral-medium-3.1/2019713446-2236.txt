Ro Chester Aug 1/59

Aurelius Contlin Esq

Dear Sir

Herewith I
hand you one dollar which I
am told is the amount required
to secure me a Copy Right
on my New Game, the
title of which I inclose.
If right have the goodness
to return me Copy right
for same. They much oblige

Yours very truly,
G. W. Fithian
