June 21, 1۹۵۸,

my dear daughter. Miss Mack came yesterday and brought the box and visited with me awhile. She said she had a nice trip up and working at home and clearing away the like.

We were very glad to talk with you Sunday night. It help Parks, feel well at present. Brother and family are quite often now. Is still working out at Jackson, and help. I hope you are well for I know your work be now that school is out I suppose some of the children will be out to stay. Much love from mother.
