We make sure of a June flitting, meantime whose farewells are now said. Remember for which you dear Frederick dislike departures but - at the say, I imply that I never found any clear fashion to mean any precious of now - amidst the many trials, I heart teeming - His appointments have gone through I can realize that "all things" must win. I am writing to this present - When we reach yonder glorious city we shall be able to look back on all the way the Lord on God has led us to humble us - Know us" May our Lord Heavenly Father bless you forever, may you live faithful and affectionate despite Dec. 13 1873 J. G. Curtis
