Directions for Chicken
Scrape, affix the jelly or juice
Take a table spoon full of flour, add salt, pepper, & taste
Stir well and dip chicken in, moving it all well. Have a
skillet hot, melt butter or
bacon fat in it and quickly
broil the chicken. Remove
the chicken, pour the juice
or jelly into the pan. By all
like add a little cream and
thicken with a little flour.
Brown Bread. Heat boiling
inground water in a pot big
enough to contain the tin
of Brown bread. A half
hour's cooking will not hurt
it. Then open the can and enjoy
