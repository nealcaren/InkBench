Charlotte wrote that she received an invitation from Mrs. Jane (Maria Mitchell) to attend an Alumnae gathering in Washington, before which Stephen Foster's daughter Alle, had been invited to read a paper. Charlotte listened to her address with absorbing interest for her parents' sake as well as for her own and said it was an exhaust[ive] description of the government of all the Colleges that are worthy of the name - Forty-eight in twenty-one States. Miss Foster is a teacher in the Girls High School in Boston where my Cousin, Sister[?] the one who died, spoken of above, also teaches.
