Concord June 14 1885
My dear Friend:
I have sent you various portions of my book in a preliminary form, not having the jackets for them, but I hope to give you what I have done, just yet. I shall also have advance sheets of the whole 650 pages and will send you an early copy when you desire it. As I get back from Barn other books which I have lent them, Dewey sends you more. Today I enclose the end of Chapter XIV, including Brackett's account of his visit to Brown Inn
