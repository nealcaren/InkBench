Mr. W.D. Kelley has my application for a clerkship in the Treasury in his hand, and my chances for a position by the first of next month are good. Judge Kelley has got Lucy Borewell to promise me a clerkship provided I will do some extra writing for the Judge out of office hours. Of course I have consented to do so, and my appointment is only a question of a little time. Senator Enning gave me my flattering recommendation, as also did Senator Pomeroy, Hon. Geo. M. Julian, and Noah Davis, member from our district, who gave me a letter to Mr. Borewell covering two pages. All of these gentlemen desired particularly to be remembered to you.
