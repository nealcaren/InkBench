Dear Steady:

I cannot think I could possibly convey to you the very great pleasure and satisfaction your breezy graphic letter gave me. I wanted to know all that you told me, and you told it so clearly that it was like a conversation with you.

The Christmas card came and was hung up on the foot of my bed for alas disasters. They tell us that travelers in the tropics must beware of diarrhea but I thought myself quite immune as I had not had such a thing for thirty years, but I got one nevertheless and was in bed a week.
