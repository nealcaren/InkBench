$400 On or before the 15th day of January 1866 we promise to pay Jane E. Tearing her heirs or assigns the just and full sum of four hundred dollars in current funds, it being for the hire of a negro slave named Turner which negro is to be clothed in the usual manner of hirelings with the exception of a blanket. (It is understood by the that harteer should the said negro be taken either by the enemy or the Confederate authorities, the amt of the hire cost shall be deducted from the hire.) For the true payment of which sum we bind ourselves jointly several Our joint & several heirs, executors Our hands & Seals this 4th day of Jan 1865 H.H.G.R. Williams Seal
