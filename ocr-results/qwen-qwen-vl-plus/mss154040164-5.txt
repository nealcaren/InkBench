Both these wonders are in Ceylon and
many more. In Egypt we attended
the ceremony of the Holy Carpet. A
carpet is made annually, and 300
soldiers are assigned to escort it to
Mecca. There it is placed in the Holy of
Holies and remains a year. The event
bringing back the carpet which has now
become holy is received by the Khedive.
Thousands of people stood in great crowds
to see it come. I never saw so quiet and
orderly a crowd. I could only account for
it by the theory that they were spell
bound with religious awe. The carpet
came on a richly caparisoned camel
mounted by a small rich
lump. The camel is holy too. It was
followed by seven camels which
