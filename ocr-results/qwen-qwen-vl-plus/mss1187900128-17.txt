I know not where to find justice for them! I appeal to your judgment. I have ever been friendly to the Colonel Laroche, having lived many years at Cape Haitian on the Island of Haiti, where my husband, [deleted text] a prominent commercial house, under the name of Wilson, Laroche. Also, we are well known to the present President of Haiti, as for years he was interested in the purchase of coffee for the house of Wilson, Laroche. Also, the Wilson was also consul for the United States, and the recognition of Haiti was effected by him. You will finish this record in the State Department. Be so kind as to write for us to influence public opinion in the favor of the Colonel people. Begging you to excuse me for addressing you, I remain
Very Respectfully,
Frances L. Wilson
