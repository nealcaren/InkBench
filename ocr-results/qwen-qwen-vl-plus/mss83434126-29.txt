March 30th 1866 - Issued to Bvt. Major A. H. B. Buffington, Commanding U. S. Arsenal at Baton Rouge, La. the following Ordinance Stores, as per Inventory and Inspection Report of Bvt. Major Gen. J. E. B. Sherman, dated March 20th, 1866.
8. Eight. Springfield Rapid Buckets, Cal. 55.
7. Seven. Partridge Boxes, Cal. 58.
7. Seven. Bayonet Scabbards
8. Eight. Gun Slings
9. Nine. Screw devices and cone wrenches
10. Ten. Alspes
3. Three. Tumbler and band spring punches
5. Five. Waist Belts (Pots)

Sent the Services through the Post Office Dept. and Received my Receipts April 12th, bearing date April 4th, 1866.

Stephen D. Newman
1st Sgt. Co. "B", 81st U. S. Inf. Commanding Company K
