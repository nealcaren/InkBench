1828 May 13th Tariff Bill imposing heavy duties on British goods
1829 Feb 29th Battle of Tarqui in which the Peruvians are defeated by the Colombians
1829 March 4th Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun Vice President
1829 Sept 11th A Spanish expedition for the recovery of Mexico sails from Havana, July 5th. It surrenders to the Mexican Gen'l Santa Anna
1830 May 9th A treaty concluded between the United States and the Ottoman Port the ports of the United States are again open to British commerce
Dec. 17th Simon Bolivar, a South American and the Liberator of Bolivia from the rule of Spain died aged 47 years
1831 April 6th Abdication of Pedro I, emperor of Brazil in favor of his infant son Don Pedro
Jan. 17th Venezuela, New Granada, and Colombia de America again became separate states
