Dear Sir
             I have thought of an expedient to bring
    in Caroline which I submit to you. It is this, Genl
    Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mississippi
    Now if you would dispense with Janet & see Genl M
    & let her go & play his negros & get it out that he has bought
    her - it would I am sure bring her in- Let it be out
    that it is an arrangement of mine over which you have
    no control: What do you think of it? Let me know
                                    Yrs truly
                                    John H. Seay
    Mr O'Neile
