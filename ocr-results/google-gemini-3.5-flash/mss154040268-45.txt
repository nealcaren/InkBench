To the Speakers Bureau: [* Oct 18 in office *]
Those roses were sweet, beautiful, temper dispelling, but
Roses sind Verboten.
Keep your money to defend the suit to vindicate my
character. Please dont disobey orders again.
I have a vile temper, growing viler every day which
shows what politics will do for a woman. It
is a daily comfort to witness the placidity of
the Speakers Bureau!
The Terror

New York City, October
1915
