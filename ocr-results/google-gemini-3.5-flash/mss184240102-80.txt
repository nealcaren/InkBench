Friday Mar 8

My dear Friend.
      yours from Ithaca
I sent to M— because I
understood you were to
be there at just that time
but I could not have
met you. I was sick
so was Mrs L. I was very
much "disappointed" too
but so glad you & Mother
enjoyed yourself so well.
I do take all care of my-
self for that future so in
hopes. but this weather kills
me if it was one long summer
