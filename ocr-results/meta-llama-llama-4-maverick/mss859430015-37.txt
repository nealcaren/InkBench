June 21, 1908.

my dear daughter. - miss 
mack came yesterday
and brought the box and
visited with me awhile.
she said she had a nice
trip up and working at
home and clearing away
the weeds in the back and
the like.  we were very glad
to talk with you Sunday-
night.  It helps Pa, feel
better.  Pa and feal
well at present.  Brother &
family are quite
often now. is still work-
ing out at Jackson. and
they are getting on. and
I hope you are well
and wont over work be-
fore you comes home. 
now that school is out I
suppose some of the child-
ren will be over to stay,
much love from mother.
