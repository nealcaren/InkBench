THE AMERICAN SOCIETY
FOR THE
Prevention of Cruelty to Animals,

Headquarters, South Ave., cor. 22d St.

New York Jan 28 1885

Hon: Frederick Douglass
House of Representatives, Washington, D.C.

Dear Sir

Yesterday, deposed with the Surrogate of Hudson Co., New Jersey, the Will of the late Ottilia Assing, along with a letter from me declining to serve as Executor, copy of which I forward to you with this.

You will please take such action in the matter of supplying a substitute, as you deem proper and oblige me

Yours with great respect
Henry Bergh
Bergh
