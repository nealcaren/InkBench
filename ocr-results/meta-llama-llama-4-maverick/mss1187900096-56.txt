Nantucket, July 18, 1891.

My dear friends:-

Mr, Charlotte and I, congratulate you upon your safe arrival back to Nantucket.  Charlotte is sojourning in Nantucket for a few weeks.  Mr. Mann remains at New Haven.  Mrs. Moore comes frequently to pass the day with me and I return the visit.  She suggested my writing to you.  Writing has become quite burdensome.  It is as feeble - seemingly dwindling down!
