DISCHARGE.

This is to certify, That No. 503. Spencer Carr a Landsman on the U.S.S. "Pennville"
has this day been discharged from the U.S.
and from the naval service.

Dated this 14th day of May 1865.

APPROVED:
R. R. Rouman
Captain, Commanding
Wm. Wm. Goddard
Paymaster.

To be returned
to 
John H. French
Paymaster
Hicks
