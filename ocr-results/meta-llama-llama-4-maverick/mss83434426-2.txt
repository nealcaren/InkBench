$400 On or before the 1st day of January
1866 we promise to pay Jane & bearing
her heirs or assigns the principal and full
sum of four hundred dollars in
Current funds- it being for the hire
of a negro slave named (Turner which
negro. is to be clothed in the usual manner
men of his kindlings. with the exception of
a. blanket (It is understood by the
parties should the said negro servant
be Taken within by. The enemy. or. the
Confederate authorities, the amt of the
above shall be deducted from the true
amount of hire we bind ourselves formerly
our bond. & several him, witness
their hand & Seals then this day of Jan
1865. 

H.F.G.Rt. Williams [Seal]
E.A.C [Seal]
