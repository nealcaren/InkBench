Boston Dec: 25-!!

My dear Miss Dickinson

Your favor of. the 19: Containing a draft for $200. Hundred dollars - came safely to hand: and I should have acknowledged its receipt at once: but with our Journal both Louise and myself are delighted to do Even so.  Little...me are more.  Nothing can or will continue at all events my XXXth. Many thanks for your kindness made love to you & your Mother
Emily 
Dickinson
