1861 Aug 10th Battle of Wilsons Creek Mo The federals under the
command Gen Lyon made a flank attack on the rebels
stood from their camp & attacked the federals
for six hours hard fighting and in taking a battery oblige

plans and retreated in an orderly manner to Springfield
leaving captured howitzers and to horses.  Lyon lost 279
killed 700 wounded.  Rebel loss in killed and wounded was
much greater.  The brave Col Lyon the hero of the battle 
gave his honor (his fortune) and his life to his country
that his countrymen,  Springfield Herald afterwards
