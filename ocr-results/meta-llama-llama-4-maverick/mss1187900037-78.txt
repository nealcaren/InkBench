Pineatic Phs/ Aug 12.1866

My dear friend

Your favor came in due time for which all thanks -   Your suggestions were worthy of serious, and its kind meaning most highly.   You will stay where he is at school, with an increase in his Salary of $200. and has also engaged to teach Elocution at the Catholic College, where his rate of compensation will be more liberal than he has had before - and in addition to this, he is determined to try at Public Reading the next winter - and can furnish him with a list of the different Lyceum's where he might be able to make engagements & I came back

1937
