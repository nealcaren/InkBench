New Haven Aug. 15, 1890.

Dear Mrs. Douglass:

Judgy Sheldon
has just informed me that your family has received a letter from you, and that you are in Washington.  We learned that you were in Boston with Mr. Douglass, and to our regret we know how saddness a line written to you or to him.
