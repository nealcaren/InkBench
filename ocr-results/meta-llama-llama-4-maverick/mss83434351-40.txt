No. 1.

Quartermaster General's Office,
PROPERTY DIVISION,
Washington, D. C., January 27, 1866.

To U.S. McKean
Esq.

Sir:

Your Return of Quartermaster's Stores as Stillds U.S.A. for the month of June 1863, has been recieved, and, when examined, will be sent to the Third Auditor of the Treasury for settlement, of which you will be duly notified.

By order of the Quartermaster General:

Very respectfully,
Your obedient servant,
M.M. Bink,
Col. & Asstn. Qr. M. Genl.
