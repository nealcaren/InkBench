No. 01. Monkey Temple.  Two hundred monkeys live in the trees surrounding the temple.  Only one is seen in the picture.  They eat from the hands of strangers and the other monkeys snatch away their babies when they think they have had enough and run up the trees with them.

No. 02. The burning ghat of the Hindus on shore of Ganges.  The body on the frame is lying feet in the holy water of the river before burning.  About six attendants sweep ashes, and unburned fingers, legs or arms into the river.
