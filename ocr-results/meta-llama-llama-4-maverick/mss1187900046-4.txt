Jan 22 1874.
Washington D.C.

My Dear father.

I had received your kind letter and was very glad to hear from you, but I am so sorry that you feel so. a bout going to Rochester, I hope it will be all right when you go thair. when you go thair. I shall take good care of your letters and not think of destroying them in any way, we have had a very cold days here Color days but have very pleasant weather.

240.
