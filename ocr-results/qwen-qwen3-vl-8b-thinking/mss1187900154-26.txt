Chicago, Ill. U.S.A. June 1st.  

My dear Mr. Douglass:  

I hear for the East coast tomorrow morning & how I have just offco to my calling up fri & hisn. Douglass that I shall be unable to do so. Do make my excuses to your wife for me. I remember her with so much pleasure in Paris. I hope to return to Chicago later in the summer. Then I will not fail to see you both. In the meantime I write you,  

Vers Chant Tenny  

Theodore Stanton,  
26 west 61st St.,  
New York.
