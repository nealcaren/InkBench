September 21st 1885  

Hon Frederick Douglass  
Washington D.C.,  
Dear sir  

I am an Ex Union soldier and have endeavored to be a constant Republican Ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the Civil War and in any humble sphere in life have been a friend to the Colored race.
