Concord June 14 1885

My dear Friend:

I have sent you a copy of the first part of my book in a supplementary form, not having time to give you what I have done with the whole 650 pages and will send you an early copy of those remaining. As I get back from Boston other chapters I have done for them, and send more. Yesterday I read chapters XIV, making a copy of Chapter XIV, & will await your advice on it.
