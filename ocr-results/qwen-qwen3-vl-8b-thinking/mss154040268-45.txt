To the Speakers Bureau;  

Three roses were sweet, beautiful, tuerpe dishelling, but  
Roses sind Verboten.  

Rush your money to dignify the suit to vindicate my  
Character. Please don't disobey orders again.  

I advise a vile leech, grooming vile every day which  
show what politics will do as far as a woman is a daily  
complaint to the president. the Speakers Bureau  
The Terror  

New York City, October 1915  

Oct 18 in office
