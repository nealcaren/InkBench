Dictated

My dear Miss. Catt:

I'm in the General Hospital, Osteopathic Unit #180 - at Los Angeles, at the present with a fractured leg. I have been here 8 weeks today. I hope soon to be released from this terrible house of torture, a cast, so that I can return to my little apartment at 609 Fairview Ave, San Pasadia Calif. Many friends wonderful to me in all ways. Address my close friend Mrs. Lois Gaukilde, 1918 Oak St., San Pasadia - who has all of me in charge. Cordially

Henrietta G Moore

11-20-31
