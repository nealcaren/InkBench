1828 May 13th. Terry's Relief, impounding many captives on British galleys  
1829 Feb 27th. Battle of Tucapí in which the Peruvians are defeated by the Colombians  
1829 March 4th. Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun Vice President  
1829 Sept 11th. A Spanish expedition for the recovery of Mexico (side from Havana, July 5th - A surrender to the Mexican at Santa Ana)  
1830 May 1st. A treaty concluded between the United States and the Colombian Republic - the parts of the United States are a few of the British possessions  
1830 Dec 12th. Simon Bolivar, a South American and the liberator of Bolivia from the rule of Spain died aged 47 years,  
1831 April 6th. Abdication of Pedro II, emperor of Brazil in favor of his infant son Don Pedro,  
1831 Nov 19th. Venezuela, New Granada, and Colombia became separate states.
