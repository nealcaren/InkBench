Life and escape from bondage  
For me to sell, saying, if I sold them I should have the volume to keep for my reward, I think. I sold ten volumes. They were published by Joseph Parker of Cudo Yorkshire. I kept my book until 1857 and some one stole it and never have I seen one like it since.  

The (remembrance of all the atare notes) prompts me to write you and your wife to a trial visit. I have been searching for days to find out your abiding home and failed correctly to get it—until yesterday. And now a line from you will give me pleasure to say when, where, and at what time you can receive a call from me on myself and wife (Whom I have not met) our home are not far apart—Salem Ohio. Do you remember Benjamin Bonn? He had an old Aunt Haver who had a daughter who married John Dennis. Mr. Dennis's daughter is married to her. Mrs. Dennis's father is a man who had an Ebbitt all Salem Ohio. The Dennis's have an Ebbitt in Manufacturers Hall. The mainland has been in Manure. My glass on Sedication Day in your through your coal, answer with best October. I wished Truly yours, Henrietta Harris Ramsay.
