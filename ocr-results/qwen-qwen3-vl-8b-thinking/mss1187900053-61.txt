Conegar Villa,  
Bridport.  
Dorset  
May 16th 1879  

Dear Mr Douglas.  

It is a very long time since we have written to you or heard from you, and Christie and I are hoping a reply. We really are very much in the dark about you, I do not know whether you still hold office under the Government, as we have not heard to the contrary I conclude that you do - I hope for that you have not been discharging holding meetings over the Country. His last writ is as old. Hope that your health is good - & that you will report well of yourself & of your family.
