Jan 22 1874.  

Washington D.L.  

my Dear father.  

I have received your kind letter and was very glad to hear from you, but I am so sorry that you feel so a bent going to Rochester, I hope it will be all Right there, when you go thair. I shall take good care of your letters and not think of disagreeing in any way, we have had a few very cold days but we have very pleasant weather. 40
