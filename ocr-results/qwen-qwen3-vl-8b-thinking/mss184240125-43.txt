I came To Boston an Nov. to make a short visit before winter, I am still here, now waiting for winter To be gone. I have lived very quietly, only been to the opera a few times. I have seen some of the celebrities, they were too costly. My heart is not broken, does not break easily. I thought I should see you here this spring, because the Peepers said so. It has been a trying winter, but spring & violists are nearer the cure. Cigie writes me often, it is happy if her bit of house-work will do it itself. But if she is shut in, she so hates in-door duties. That she frets, the hames Chapes. She has a governess practical man, to keep her from doing anything of things. Saint's day. Your blessed are always. Lovingly, Mama Mary.
