I know not where to find patron for them; I appeal to your judgment; I have ever been friendly to the Colonial Race, having served many years as Cape Haytian in the Island of Hayti, where my husband, now a prominent commercial man, under the name of Wilson, Larocke, also, we are well known to the present President of Hayti, as for years he was interested in the purchase of Coffee, for the house of Wilson, Larocke. Also, The Wilson was also known for the United States, and the recognition of Hayti was effected by him. For such a good record, I am so trivial as to write for as to influence public opinion in the favor of the Coloured people. Begging you to excuse me for addressing you. I remain Your Respectfully, Francis L. Wilson
