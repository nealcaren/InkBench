ALBERT H. WALKER,
COUNSELOR AT LAW.
(Including Patent Causes.)

Hartford, Connecticut,

4th 5th the colored leaders tak
to announcing the Dabney law; the
well taken up the sympathies
of two best friends with the Party.
The abolition of slavery; the
prohibition of law-criminating
state laws; the extension of
the ballot to the colored man;
these are secure. So the colored
leaders announce any folly for
not also furnishing by laws, the
assent to the colored people from
particular private property; and
as hotels & theaters will look rather childish. It
will tend to show that the colored
people care more for the show
than for the substance of citizenship.
