Hartford, Octot 14 1782.  
RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order  
on the Treasurer, of this State, to fecure the Payment  
of Thistare Jounds and sterer juns  
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1782  
1ft, as flat by the Committees of the State and of the  
Army. In behalfl of Lister Cyprus  
Chas. Fanning Trust.

Voucher in Elias Lamberts Milt of this date.
