Dinner we all read at the parlor & a talk and chatter until ten o'clock when B came in and we had something to eat. After which we all went to bed.

We made a long journey. I was afraid you would have been disappointed with me, so I've been making you a letter. I wish that the truth might have been sent, but I cannot be sure. I shall write my other letters soon.

I hope you have found the letter about the "N" letter (which I mentioned before) & I've been trying to send it. I think you'll appreciate it. I'm so sorry I couldn't be with you, but my health is not good. I hope you'll find it well and be happy.

I am so happy that you have found the letter about the "N" letter (which I mentioned before) & I've been trying to send it. I think you'll appreciate it. I'm so sorry I couldn't be with you, but my health is not good. I hope you'll find it well and be happy.

From your affectionate

In Ghalia R. Douglass
