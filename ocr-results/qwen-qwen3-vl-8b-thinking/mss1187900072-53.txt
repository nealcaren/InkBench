Charlotte writes that the received an invitation from Mrs. Jane (Maria Mitchell) to attend an Alumnae gathering in Washington, before which Stephen Foster's daughter Alice, had been invited to read a fragment. Charlotte listened to her address with absorbing interest for her parents' sake as well as for her own, and said it was an orphant description of all the Collegeth that are worthy of the name. Forty eight in twenty one States. Miss Foster is a teacher in The Girls High School in Boston, where my Cousin, the one who died, above, also teaches.
