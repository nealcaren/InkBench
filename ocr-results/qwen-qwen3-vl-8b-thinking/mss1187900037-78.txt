Mccarte/ths/ Aug 2. 1866  

My dear Friend  

Your kind favor came in due time for which all thanks –  
Its suggestions were valuable of service,  
and its kind sayings most grateful  
to both of us –  
You will stay when he is at school, with an increase  
in his salary of $200, and has also engaged  
to teach Eloquention at the Methodist college,  
where his rate of compensation will be more liberal  
than he has had before –  
and in addition to this, he is determined  
to try at Public Reading the next week –  
and can now furnish himself with a book of the different hymns,  
where he might be able to make engagements?  
I can but
