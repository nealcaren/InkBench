1 Ort of Spain. May 26/1923.

My dear Mollie:-

I gave you this piece for a. Cable and now I'm in the hospital, the remain another day that I suppose that progress is in the direction of recovery and that you have not called at all.

May 27. I was interrupted here and never got at anything any different and time has passed, your cable has been received, and it was such a relief to get it. I imagine you passing through that discouraging stage when you think you never will be well again and when life has lost all its fervor. Don't keep up your courage and you'll pull through.
