THE EAGLE MOUNTAIN HOUSE  
WHITE MOUNTAINS – JACKSON, NEW HAMPSHIRE  

Aug 9/1947  

Dear Cisain Alice:  

When Ethel went to Huntington I went out to spend the day – No it was about two weeks after she had gone – She had failed steadily – I spoke to Trace & said that perhaps I had better write you for I then realized that she had Cancer & That she could not get well – Ittruce said That he had told Howard & That he would
