OFFICE OF—  
C. B. BARNES,  
SECRETARY OF THE  
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,  
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.  

Cuthbert, Ga., 189  

Painter Gideon Gurr & Dr. The Cause, I wish you would send me one of them. I am very busy. I wish you to do me this favor as I have spent a great deal of money this year for the Republican Cause, and is now greatly in debt for the same. And the republican committee have sent a petition to Mr. Grannamahus, Postmaster, asking the Appointment Of Mr. John L. Taverton, to be the Postmaster at this place (Cuthbert Ga). This will help me to secure my home for the money spent for the Party.
