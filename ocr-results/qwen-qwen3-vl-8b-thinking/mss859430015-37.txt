June 21, 1958.

my dear daughters.- miss mack came yesterday and brought the box and visited with me awhile, she said she had a nice trip up and working at home and clearing away weeds in the back and the like.

we were very glad to talk with you Sunday- better, we are doing very well at present. Brother and family are quite well. he doesn't come so often now, is still work- ing out at Jackson, and he's getting welfare and hope you are well and wont over work, be- fore you come home.

now that School is out I suppose some of the Children will bid over to stay, much love from mother
