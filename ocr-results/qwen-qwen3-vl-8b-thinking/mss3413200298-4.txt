MRS. MARY WARE DENNETT  
51 SINGER ST.  
ASTORIA, LONG ISLAND  
NEW YORK CITY  

May 21 1935  

Dear Mrs. Brown:  

I Can't let your note about Mrs. Blackwell go unanswered, even if the checks I wrote like to send must be omitted.  

I have always admired Mrs. Blackwell, and, in late years, have loved her especially. She always beautiful Christian and Parlett messages, and her widomtable spiritual peace are such lonely additions to the great background of her life works.  

It wounds my heart to know that she, too, is facing a financial vacuum. There are so many who are ! I am one of them myself, or of course I would share in making the funds which you are raising. Being a starving artist is a most painful handicap, in every way, but it is particularly hard to endure, when appeals like this come.  

Thank you for including me as one of her friends, which I most heartily am.  

Sincerely,  
Mary Ware Dennett
