Elizabeth is making preparations to lead her lecture next Wednesday night and have as on Tuesday or Wednesday of next week, I believe she has more made better her appointments yet - but I think it will take longer to decide and will not a great deal of noise and confusion with the schoolroom and the few who have brought their difficulties as possible. I hope you will have as comfortable a time as possible and that you will not return home sick. (Old Blown will have to keep out of sight for a little while the Governor of Mississippi has a reward of $1,000 offered for his capture and Ida will have given himself up. Mother wishes me to give her love and enclosed you will find a note from Elizabeth and perhaps Annie will send a few lines. Your aff. daughter Rosetta Dougla
