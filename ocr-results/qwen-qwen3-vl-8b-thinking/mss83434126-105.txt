1855 July 8th. American expedition under Commodore Perry arrived at Japan,
1855 July 14th. Exhibition of the Society of Arts held at New York City,
1855 Sept 4th. Remaining portion of "Tall Chief" at the fall of Virginia, broken off,
1855 Nov 9th. Suspension of the Washington Agreement,
1855 Dec 16th. Such Santa Anna elected dictator of Mexico for Life, He had formerly been President from 1833 to 1845,
1856 March 8th. A Treaty of Commerce concluded between the United States and Japan,
1856 April 20th. Miss Digby's bill ameliorating the condition of the indigent insane - voted,
1856 April 25th. The President issued a proclamation, announcing the neutrality of the United States in the Eastern question,
1856 June 10th. A reciprocity treaty between the United States and Great Britain was concluded at Washington, respecting Newfoundland fishing, international trade,
