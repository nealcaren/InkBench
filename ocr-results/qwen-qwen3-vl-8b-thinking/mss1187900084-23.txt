Those are thoughtful people, or rather the good of the officers, some at heart will get a good bit in the dory, I think, from the subjects. I'll have been taken at the address in parenthesis form. If so, I shall order some for Circulation. This is not a new or thoughtful- in in that I would have changed in any subject. I again think to thank thee for laying before two American people to take and time an address. I wish Jane would send a copy to our Communion Journal, from the legals of the Ohio.
