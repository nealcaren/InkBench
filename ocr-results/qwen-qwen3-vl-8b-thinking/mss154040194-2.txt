Buc., 4 Sept. 1922

Dear Mrs. Chapman, Gatt, it is not in our programme of this year, to read lectures on "Tainous, Women" (Mrs. Reus Janulescu and I will do it, she in the L.D. D.F. and I in popular evening course. But as there are no complete libraries in Roumania and not a single feminist library, it is impossible for us even to know the title of the book, necessary for preparation. We address therefore to you as to the other sections of I. N. S. W. to ask your help. Feeling so much the lips of a fine library we will try at one time with our studies, to found one at B., and perhaps that will be able to assemble around it the Women in a club, we are dreaming of since a long time. Would your association, be able for the generous act of your help with our sisters and to send us one of the old standard works on women, movement in your country and specially books, where we could find biographies of your famous women?
