Carrie Chapman Catt  
404 Riverside Drive  
New York  

May 24 1920  

Dear Mary,  

I received a book yesterday the 24th gift this spring! If this book tells how to attain success with a boat when the author (would be) has neither sense nor pep and no vocabulary to wrap the public thought in, it will be most welcome. Until I have read it or to keep it with thanks or whether to return it or to keep it without thanks is a dim idea I have not the newestst idea. I had these three of them are, but I am still finding out what any of them are, but I have been given you the garden for all your garden fuel plants from the green house and my garden. They are the green house and the garden. They are the green house and the garden. They are the green house and the garden.
