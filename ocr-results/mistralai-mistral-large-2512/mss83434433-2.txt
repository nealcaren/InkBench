Dear Sir,

I have thoughts of an expedition to bring
into Louisiana which I submit to you. It is the gene-
ral policy now mustering his force to leave for Mississippi
River to join our boats disperse with orders to see them all
out of our way & destroy, his negroes get out that he has bought
for it would I am sure bring him & all others to our
terms. It is an arrangement of mine over which you have
no control. What do you think of it? I do not know
how truly,        Your friend,
                    Sam Heile

Gen. Meade
Col. McNeil
