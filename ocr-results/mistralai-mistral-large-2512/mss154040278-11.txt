Directions
for chicken
Scraps of the little mince
taken at table (persons who
allow scraps to accumulate
will find enough for chicken
stuffing) well wet with a
spoonful of milk, butter, or
broth.  For two mince
pies, one-fourth teaspoon
salt, and a little flour,
the minced meat with a
teaspoon of cream and
brown bread, if at hand.
Spread between a soft hot
corn cake or corn bread.  A half
cup of browned cracker crumbs
may be added to the mince
meat.  Mix well and cook
in a double boiler.  The chicken
for croquettes must not have
too much breading when the canned or any
meat is used.
