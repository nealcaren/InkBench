- OFFICE OF -
**C. B. BARNES,**
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Ashboro, N.C., 189

Printer Says: Your order
for the Courier I wrote you
would send me two of them,
and you, Bumgarner,
wrote me to send a great deal
of money, this year for the
Republican cause, and is now
grudging in debt for the same.
And the Republican Chairman
committee here sent a petition
to Mr. Graham asking for the
appointment of
Jno. L. Barnes to the place
of Post Officer at this place
(Coleridge, N.C.) This will help
me to secure my pay for
the money spent for the party.
