State of Pennsylvania,

Know all men by these presents, that I,
he in Norristown A. W. Hendricks,
Superintendent of the State Hospital
for the Insane, at Danville, Pa., Samuel Kenney,
Junior, and as such, by the laws said
State, Custodian of the books and
records of said Institution, do
hereby certify, that the within, to
forgoing is a true copy of an original
writing now in my possession and has been
compared with such original,
and is a correct transcript thereof,
and of the whole of such original.
Witness my hand and seal this
14th day of April 1893.

[Signed] A. W. Hendricks Supt.
State of Pennsylvania, Dept.
County of Northampton.

Subscribed and sworn to
before me this 14th day of April 1893.
