Vineyard, July 18 - 1891.

My dear friends: -

We, (Charlotte and I),
congratulate you upon your safe
arrival back to Washington.
Charlotte is sojourning in Nantucket
for a few weeks. She, Mamma, &
Mrs. A. B.
comes frequently to pass the day
with me and I return the
visit. She suggested my writing
to you. Nothing has become
quite burdensome to her since she
is so feeble - seemingly like Middle-
town! But that she has improved
since her arrival on the Island
is clearly evident - she did not
think of watching out at all
when I was in Newtown, now,
she does more - watching than
