I think we are the superior people. But is
there any reason why we should commence
raids and murders and disregard
treaties for the South of Mexico
merely for the benefit of a few land and oil
men? That does not appeal to such reforms
again. We resented it when Germany
tried to force us to recognize Huerta
against the wishes of the Mexican people.
But now we ourselves
seem to be taking the same stand. We
superior to the Mexicans, but we are
meddling in a way that the South of the Republic
profits by. If we persist in this, Mexico that
should be our friend will turn against us
and the whole Southwest corner of our
will be in flames. But now we are in
Munroe Doctrine, not war, and we must
on this continent be able to settle our
own quarrels without going to war for oil
or new fields. I do not understand
politics down in the valley, but I understand
selfish aims may cause you much trouble
and transform it into the nightmare
tolls.
