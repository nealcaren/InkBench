Both these wonders are in Ceylon and were more in Egypt me attended the ceremony of the Holy Carpet. A carpet is made annually, and 300 soldiers are assigned to it in the Holy of Mecca. There it is spread in the Holy of Holies and remains a year. The event brings back the carpet. Though it is now received holy it is received by the Khedive. Thousands of people stand on either side in great crowds to see it. I never saw so great and orderly a crowd. I could only account for it by the thought that they were spell-bound by religious awe. The carpet came on a richly caparisoned camel mounted by a small rich turban. The camel is holy too.
