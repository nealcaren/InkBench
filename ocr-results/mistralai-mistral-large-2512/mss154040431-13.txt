**Man Unbelieving**

**ILLEGITIMATE BIRTHS IN PRUSSIA NOW 10%**

**"Evidence of Moral Heedlessness of German Race," Berlin Councillor Declares.**

*Vienna — Dec. 4 (1917 Associated Press) — Ten per cent. of the wartime births in Prussia are illegitimate, according to statements made in the Prussian House of Representatives by Dr. Engel, the Berlin Town Councillor. Dr. Engel, in a statement that this large proportion of illegitimate births is a warning of the moral heedlessness of the German race, called for the withdrawal of all distinctions between legitimate and illegitimate children. Paradoxical though it may seem, this great increase in the measure of the morality of our people, the idea of the restriction of family emphasis bids us combat, has not yet penetrated the mind.*

*The terrible fact, however, is that only one child in every ten children born in their nineteenth year, as compared with 312 in the eighth, or 10 per cent. of illegitimates is a convincing plea for the unmarried mothers and their children. The unmarried mothers and their children, and women who were engaged to men who have fallen in the war, are in a pitiable state. Frau Fraenkel, speaking to the card system, is a torture for the unmarried mothers. The number of unmarried mothers in Vienna alone has reached a total of nearly 200,000 yearly.*
