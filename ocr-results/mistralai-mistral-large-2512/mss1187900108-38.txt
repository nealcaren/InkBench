you will not run any risks by
travelling or lecturing till summer
comes again.  When grandma
wrote you were in some anxiety
about one of your sons, who was ill.
Hope that he has long since recover-
ed that all your family are well.
I hoped that the book to
us Carpenters' letters & a few
other writings, which may with-
hold, would have been out by
this time, but it will be two
or three weeks before it appears; it is
entitled "Personal & Social Chris-
tianity" & is prefaced by a short
memoir by Mrs. T.L. Cumbie.
