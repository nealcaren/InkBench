MRS. MARY WARE DENNETT
51 SINGER ST.
ASTORIA, LONG ISLAND
NEW YORK CITY

May 21
1935

Dear Mrs. Dennett:

I can't let your note go unanswered, even
Miss Blackwell (or whatever she said)
if the checks I enclose like 25 cents
must be omitted.

I have always admired Miss Blackwell,
and, in late years, have loved her
especially. Her always beautiful Christian
manners and Parker messages, and her
wonderful spiritual grace are such
lovely additions to the great task-
foundations of her life work.

25 wraps my heart to know that
she, too, is facing a financial vacuum.
There are so many who are! I am one
of them myself, or otherwise I would
share in making the fund which you
are raising. Being a stamp artist
and most painfully handicapped
in every way, but in a particularly
hard-to-endure, when appears like
this comes.

Thank you for including me
as one of her friends, which
I most heartily am.

Sincerely,

Mary Ware Dennett
