Leonard June 14 1885

My dear Friend,
I have just
seen Bosson's letter to
you, sorry not having one
direct from Ross. Suspect it
gave you what I nursed
like, just yet, a shock.
Now have a dozen or so
of the whole 687 pages
and will send you in
a day or two, a few
selected. As I get
back to Ross, Bern or other
warmer climes I shall
send more. Yesterday I am
clear into the 3rd
chapter XIV, in Ledru
Rollin's account 707
