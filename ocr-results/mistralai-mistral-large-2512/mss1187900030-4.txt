I lecture next Tuesday night but have

Perhaps she has near-sighted eyes or her cap-

go as soon as I could get a substitute. There is
so much confusion and so many things
in the way of those who have to
do night-time duties are so often delayed.
I hope you will have discovered
a suitable nurse as possible and then you
will not return to check. And Solomon
will have to keep an eye on a little while
the Government of Missouri has a somewhat
shaky foundation for its capture and I am
not sure whether it will hold up.
And enclosed you will find a letter
from Elizabeth and perhaps these
will be of use to you.

Your affect. daughter,
Josetta Gamble
