Conewago Villa,
Bridport.
May 16th 1879

Dear Mr Boughey
It is a very long
time since we have written to
you or heard from you, and
I write now in hopes of pro-
voking a reply. We really are
merrymaking in the dark about
you, & do not even know whether
you are held office under the
Government, as we have not heard
of the country. I conclude that you
do. I suppose that you boarded-
out the family whilst you went
as of old. I hope that your health
is good & that you can report
well of yourself & your family.
