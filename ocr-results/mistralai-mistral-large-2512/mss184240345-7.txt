Wednesday 17th
S. 11. 91

Dear Mr. Turner, A thousand
thanks for buying me a
study 8x6 photo. — That
is what I desire — when
I fully affirm it here
(and a carte in whole
& 6 carte de visite)
I'll write to Mr. Nich-
olls at once, but
in case already taken, I've
taken liberty to order
dates from you for the
sittings.  I shall be
glad to pay for them.
May I also be so bold as to
ask you to kindly bring
the proofs to me here?
