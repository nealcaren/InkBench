1853 July 8th. American expedition under Commander Ringgold to Japan.

1854 July 14th. Conclusion of the treaty of all nations opened at New York 4th.

1853 Sept. 8th. Landing near mouth of Gadsden, and the valley of Tehuantepec bought.

1853 Dec. 9th. Organization of the Washington Monument.

1853 Dec. 16th. Gen. Santa Anna, elected dictator of Mexico for life.

He had journeyed from Perote prison from 1845 to 1846.

1856 March 6th. A Treaty of Commerce concluded between the United States and Japan.

1854 April 29th. Irish Repeal agitation, the association of the incident ensign adopted.

1854 April 28th. The President issued a proclamation announcing the neutrality of the United States in the Eastern question.

1859 June 7th. Anniversary treaty between the United States and Great Britain declared at Washington regarding the carrying trade.
