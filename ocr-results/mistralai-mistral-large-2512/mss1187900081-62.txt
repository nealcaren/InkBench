Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Mowbray Esq.
Attorney

Opens from the Kansas City-Times
of the 23rd. I enclose to you for
your verification or denial, hundreds
of your Colo. adherents, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much perplexity and sorrow.
We have been induced to believe that
both New York and Indiana
would be fully released up to
cast their electoral vote for our
Galveston standards bearers. Kansas
and Missouri, at reply, either
confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige

Yours &c
A. S. Sevewing [?]
