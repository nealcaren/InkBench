Orta, Spain.   March 26, 1923.

My dear Mollie:—
I gave you this paper for a
cabinet and now is here. I remain
anxious day that I suspect that progress
is in the direction recovery and that you
now must be able to sit up.
Much 27. I was underwhelmed here and now since
all things are different and true has passed
your cabrer has been renewed, and was such
a relief to get it. Imagining you passing through
that discouraging stage when you think you
had discovered that when life has
never met the self again and when hope has
lost and its power. But keep up your courage
and you'll pull through. I am so glad
you are better. This would indeed be a
black old place without you dear Mollie.
I expected to meet you at a note here
because I thought your address was a good
cabrer post. It should be a good mail
station, but it seems many Lucian's
slaves.
