WIFE DIVORCES
MORSE IN RENO;
HE LOSES PLEA

Reno, Nev., Dec. 9—(Special)—
David Kendall Morse of the Chicago
and New York bar, and former actor of New York,
today got a divorce from his wife,
former Ruth Chandler of Winnet-
ka. The decree was awarded Mrs. Morse
upon her deposition alleging that he
had deserted her.

Mrs. Morse testified that she
and her husband were married at Reh-
both, Mass., on June 14, 1910.
They were married at Reh-
both, Mass., and lived together until
1928, when he left her.

Among the makers of a testimo-
nial dinner to Charles G. Dawes at
the Congress hotel tonight are list-
ed Charles S. Whitman, former
governor of New York; John J.
Raskob, Democratic national chair-
man; Silas H. Strawn.
