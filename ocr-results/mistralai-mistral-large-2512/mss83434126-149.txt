His battle was the first of several thousand Troadians, under

1862 March 17th Gen. Wm. Nelson assumes command of the Army of the
Potomac in the field. — Gen. McClellan assumes command of the
Mountain Department — Gen. Halleck assumes command of the
Department of the Mississippi. Tennessee occupied by the Federals.

1862 March 19th Commodore DuPont captures and takes possession
of Jacksonville, Fla. — The Federals attacked and drove the
rebels compelling them to abandon their encampment at Paris,
Tenn.
