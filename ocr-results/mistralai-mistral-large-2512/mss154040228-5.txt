Mrs. Marian Ures. Catt.
The General Hospital, Osteopathic
Unit #180 - at Los Angeles, after 23
days present with a fractured leg.
Have been here 8 weeks today.
Hope soon to be released from
this terrible home of torture, a
cast, so that I can return
to my little apartment at
609 Fairview Ave., So. Pasadena
Calif. Many friends wonderful
to me in my distress.
Address my close
friend Mrs. Lois Fairbanks, 1918
Oak St., So. Pasadena - who has
all - of me in charge.
Cordially,
Henrietta C. Moore
11-20-'38
