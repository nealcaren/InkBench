Mr. Balcony, 50 Cents.

Massachusetts Woman Suffrage Association,
3 Park Street,

Boston, Sept 21, 1889.

Dear Frederick Douglass,

When we first saw your
appointment to Hayti, and also how
many kind and beautiful things
the newspapers were saying about
you, pagan to clip and stick them all in
the album of sending them all to
you—) but "lots" of them, but alas!
in the huge piles of editorial scraps
they here all disposed, but three.
I enclose
it, lest you want to see the
opinion in which you are held,
while I am not sure that we are glad
to have you go. We need you
here.
