Hartford, Oct. 19th 1788

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of his orders and warrants -
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1789
as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army. In behalf of Sd. Cyrenius
Chester's farming acct.
£13.0.0

Your most obdt. Servant, Sec. of this State.
