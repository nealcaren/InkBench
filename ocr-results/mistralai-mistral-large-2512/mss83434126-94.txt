1828. May 28th. Treaty infringing being ratified on British soils.

1829. Feb 7th. Battle of Tarqui, in which the Peruvians are defeated by the Colombians.

1829. Grass forth Andrew Jackson inaugurated President and John C. Calhoun Vice President.

1829. Sept 17th. A scientific expedition for the survey of Magnetic declination from Savannah, under Bache, set sail to the Pacific from New London.

1830. May 28th. Velocity act extended between the United States and Ottoman Porte. In the ports of the United States are recognized the British possessions.

Dec 17th. Simon Bolivar, in South America and the idea of a federation of Bolivia from the rest of Spanish did agree 97 years.

1831. April 6th. Abolition of British Empire of Brazil in favor of the infant son Dom Pedro.

Nov 17th. Ecuador, New Granada, and Venezuela as Americas again became separate states.
