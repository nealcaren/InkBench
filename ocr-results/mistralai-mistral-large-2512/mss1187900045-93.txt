But - let me say, my own
dear, that I never knew how
clearly dear a room could be, 'til
I've been away.  And now
all the rooms, all the furniture,
the walls, the rugs, all seem
to reach out to me with dear
arms to be enveloped to work
back on the way they
used to be.  I love us in
the house again.  Have lots
to say to you, and hope to
find you well.  I've had a
few days of perfect rest, and
now feel fit to come
expecting to get to work.
Dec.13.1893
