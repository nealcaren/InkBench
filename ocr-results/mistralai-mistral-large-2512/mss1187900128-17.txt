I know not where to find pastures
for them; I appeal to your judgment.
I have ever been friendly to the
Colored race, having served many
years at Cape May Light on the
Island of Five Mile, where my husband
had a department; commercial houses
under the name of Wm. Laroche
& Co., the one known to the present
President of Hayti, as for years he
was interested in the purchase of
coffee for the house of Ormond, Laroche
& Co., and Ormond was also consul
for the United States, and the
recognition of Hayti was effected
by him. From what I just now received
in the State Department,
he is kind as to write for an interview
with the influence of public opinion on the
favor of the colored people ...
By giving you to know me for
addressing you, I remain
Very Respectfully,
Francis L. Wragg
