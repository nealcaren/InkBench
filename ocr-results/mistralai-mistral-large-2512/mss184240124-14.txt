Dear Miss Dickinson:-

I am sorry it is impossible for me to answer intel-
ligently the questions you ask, for the reason that I am not strict-
ly speaking a theatrical man, simply a business Manager. I have, how-
ever, asked Mr. Lindsay Morrison, who is Manager of the Boston Theatre,
and a gentleman who is thoroughly posted in matters of this kind to
reply to your favor.

Thanking you for your kind wishes, I remain,
Very truly yours,

Henry D. Duzce
