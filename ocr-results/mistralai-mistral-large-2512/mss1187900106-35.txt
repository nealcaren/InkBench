[*Everett House, Union Square*]
[*Ocean House, Newport, R.I.*]

J.G. Weaver Jr. & Co.
John G. Weaver Jr.
OCEAN HOUSE, BELLEVUE AV.
Newport, R.I. Aug. 27, 1889

My dear friend Root,
I am clearing out of the Everett
House today for the boat
and go to Newport this
evening for a week or ten days at the
[*Ocean House*]. I will write you
from there in a day or
two. I have just had a
postal from my brother at
Bar Harbor who says he
expects to be in Boston on
Saturday next.
