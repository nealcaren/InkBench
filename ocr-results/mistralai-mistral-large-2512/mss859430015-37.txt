June 21, 1958.

My dear daughters, - Miss
Mack came yesterday
and brought the box and
visited with me awhile.
She said she had a nice
trip up and was working at
home and clearing away
the weeds in the back and
we were very glad
to talk with you Sunday night. It helps Parkes, I feel
well at present. Brother's
family are quite
well. He doesn't come so
often now, is still work-
ing out at Jackson, and
helps. I hope you are well
and work over your work be-
fore you come home.
Now that school is out I
suppose some of the child-
ren will be over to stay,
much love from Mother.
