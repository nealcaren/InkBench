I came to Boston as Nov. to
make a short visit before winter, &
I am still here, now waiting for winter
to be gone. I have lived very quietly, only
been to the opera a few times. I have
seen some of the celebrities, they were
too costly. My heart is not broken, does
not break easily. I thought I should
see somewhere this spring, becomes the papers
say so, it has been a trying winter,
but spring will come soon now.
Maggie writes me often, it is cheering if
her bit of house-work while she rests.
But if she is shut in, she so hates
"in-door studies" that she frets, the harsh
changes. She has a good position now, to
keep her from doing unwise or things. I wish
easy, for she is dear - always.

Yours aff. - mamma Lucy. -
