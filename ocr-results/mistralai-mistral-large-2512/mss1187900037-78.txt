Precinct 16, May 22, 1868

My dear friend

Your kind form came
in due time for which I felt thankful.
The suggestions were sensible & seasoned
and its kind sayings much gladly
to both of us.  I will stay where
I am a school, with an increase
in my salary of $200, and has also
engaged to teach Elocution at the
Methodist College, where this same
compensation will be more decided
than he has had before - and in
addition to this, he is determined
to try at Public Reading, the next
winter - and can you, with such hours
with a list of the different Lyceum
Lectures he ought to be able to
make engagements.  I can but

Nov 1937
