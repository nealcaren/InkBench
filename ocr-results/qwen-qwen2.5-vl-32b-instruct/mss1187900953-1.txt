**Howard University,**  
**Washington, D. C.**

**Dear Sir:**

The Semi-annual meeting of the Board  
of Trustees of Howard University will be  
held in the President's Room on Tuesday,  
January 16, 1894, at 3 o'clock P. M.

Very respectfully,  
**J. B. Johnson,**
