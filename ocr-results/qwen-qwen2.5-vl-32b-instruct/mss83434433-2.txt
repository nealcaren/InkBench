```
Dear Sir,

I have thought of an expedient to bring
in Caroline which I submit to you. It is the time
Molety is mustering his force to leave for Mappopippi.
Now if you could dispose with Janers & see him
let her go slay his negro & get it out that the buyer
is dead, I am sure bring her in & let it be our
thee, it is an arrangement of mine over which you have
no control. What do you think of it? Let me know.

John & Lea.
```
