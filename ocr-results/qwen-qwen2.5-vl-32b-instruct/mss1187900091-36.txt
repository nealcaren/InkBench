New Haven, Aug. 15, 1890.

Dear Mrs. Douglass:

Judge Sheldon has just informed me that his family had received recently a letter from you, and that you are in Washington. We feared that your were in Boston with Mr. Douglas, and so none of us know how to address a line either to you or to him.
