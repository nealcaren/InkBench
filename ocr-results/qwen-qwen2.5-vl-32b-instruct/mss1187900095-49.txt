Mrs. Blaine has been sick in New York for two weeks, and he does not seem to get worse. There is much concern manifested in his condition.

I thank you for long and very kind letter, and regret that I don't feel well enough to pay to your now that I had it in mind to pay when I began it. But we get worked out even when we are very young men ambitious to run a long way.
