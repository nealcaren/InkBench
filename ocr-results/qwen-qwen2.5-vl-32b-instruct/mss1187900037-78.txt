Worcestersks / Augt 2 1816

My dear [Illegible]

Your kind letter came in due time for which I all thanks. Its suggestions were sensible & serviceable, and its kind sayings most grateful to both of us. Fred will stay where he is a school with an increase in his salary of $200, and has also engaged to teach Election at the Catholic college, when his late of compensation will be more liberal than he has had before – and in addition to this, he is determin’d to try at Public Reading the next winter, and cannot furnish us with a list of the different Lociums [=Locales?] where he might be able to make engagements?
