**THE AMERICAN SOCIETY**  
**FOR THE**  
**Prevention of Cruelty to Animals,**  
Headquarters, Fourth Ave., cor. 22d St.

**New York January 28, 1885**

Hon: Frederick Douglas  
House of Representatives, Washington D.C.  

Dear Sir,  

Yesterday, deposited with the Surrogate of Hudson Co., New York, the Will of the late Olivia Assing.  

along with a letter from me declining to serve as Executor,  
Copies of which I forward to you with this.  

You will please take such action in the matter of appointing a substitute as you deem proper.  

and believe me,  

Yours with great respect  

**Alonzo Beech**  
Deacy
