**Kansas City, Fort Scott & Memphis Railroad Co.**

**Marion, Arkansas**  
**September 21st, 1888**

---

**Hon. Frederick Douglass  
Washington, D.C.**

**Dear Sir:**  

I am an Ex-Union Soldier, and have endeavored to be a consistent Republican ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the Civil War and in my humble sphere of life have been a friend to the Colored race.
