Secret Service has at last been able to show that the war of our "Rebellion" was instigated by this ["I" ?] that again they had so slandered us in the great Nations of Europe that on one pretext—and another the doors of the "old world" were nearly closed to us as a market. Hence our long years of stringency—a strike, which our victimized Christian Capitalists dare not explain to the people. England & Germany, the two great Protestant Nations had been made to believe in a great American "dying—mote" plot against themselves. I cannot name it. It is too terrible in view of the blood of every nation in our veins—the spirit of bitterness and strife in our own midst—with this ["I" ?] damnable issue in ambush with their 5,000,000 [illegible] element, waiting, waiting to occupy again, a million dead men's [illegible], in our land, as they did, both North & South, after they had brought on a family war—so to speak—in which brother met brother in mortal combat to the extent of almost total nations' death. Suffice it the truth had been reached when about 3 years ago—Sir Edwin Arnold.
