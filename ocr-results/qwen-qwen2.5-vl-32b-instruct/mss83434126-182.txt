A decisive victory seemed about to crown the efforts of  
the rebels, at this critical moment - a deliverer suddenly appeared,  
Genl. McClellan signaled to the Federal gunboats  
to open fire on the enemy, soon their ponderous shells  
bunsh among them, whole ranks went battered to the  
earth, confusion quickly spread through their columns,  
and they who a few moments before were confident of  
victory, were themselves compelled to give way,  
Genl. Heintzelman determined to recover the fortunes of  
the day, by making a desperate charge, The gunboats  
new order came flying.  This veteran galloped  
from column to column and cheered his men to the attack,  
the enemy met them with firmness, but had  
gradually to give way, their lines soon broke, and  
eventually fled from the field in confusion.  The  
rebels previously captured a large number of cannon, but  
portions of the battalions of Ramdam, Iroquois, Aquia.  The  
goal of the Campaign was reached in safety, and  
the soldiers of the Union, saved the honor of the  
nation with the blood of its patriots.
