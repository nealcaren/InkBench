**Cedar Hill**  
**Anacostia, D.C.**  
**March 24, 1896**  

**Mr. C. J. Thomas**  
**No. 72 Island St.,**  
**Grand Rapids, Mich.**

Dear Sir:  

I am glad to be advised through your letter this morning received, that the sentiments expressed by me met with such hearty approval from your club, and that the Souvenir has an honorable place in your room. The price of the Souvenir is thirty (30) cents.
