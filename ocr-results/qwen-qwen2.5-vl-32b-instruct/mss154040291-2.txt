November 1 - 1938  
Dear Mrs. Catt:—  
Hearty congratulations on the 
Presidential Renew 
award. A bit tardy but 
well deserved! I hope 
you realize how many 
women have longed 
for you to have it!  
Faithfully,  
Anna Édou Richarden  

**Note:** The signature appears to read "Anna Édou Richarden," though it could also be "Anna Edw Richarden" or similarly interpreted due to the handwriting style.
