If you will but keep  
the eye of your own  
spirit turned within,  
for it is from within  
not without that  
the precious jewel of  
truth and insight is to  
be found—and found—  
And clearly you  
will find it, and seek  
it, and love it—and  
all men and women will  
call you blessed.
