June 21, 1958.

My dear daughter.- Miss
mack came yesterday
and brought the box and
visited with me awhile,
she said she had a nice
trip, up and working at
weeds, clearing away
the like, in the back and

we were very glad
to talk with you Sunday-
night. At Kelley Parks, felt
well. we are doing very
and at present. Brothers
well. family are quite
often now, he doesn't come so
ing out at Jackson, and
they are getting welfare
help. I hope you are well
and won't over work be-
fore you comes home. now
that School is out I
suppose some of the child-
ren will be over to stay
much love from mother.
