4-6-58

Parker, my dear husband;

How are you? I hope you are well and that mother is all right. Does she still take her medicine? I hope she will have a doctor to check her, if she is not well.

I am sending the income tax form for you to sign and mail to the Michigan Revenue office. Brother or some one else can address it for you. I don't have the list of places with me. We have a friend so you may sign the check, mail it to me and I will cash it and send your part if you want it.
