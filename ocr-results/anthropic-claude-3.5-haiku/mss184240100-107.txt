Boston Dec. 25th

My dear Miss Dickinson

Yours favor of
the 19th containing a draft
for four Hundred dollars
came. Safely to hand. I should now-
acknowledgeit to accept
at once. Such is out-
found both desire
and myself. As idle do
do even so. Little me
are now. Rather aunt
here to continue do is
left all count. me should
try. XXX. Many thanks
for your favor -

Leanna sends much
love to you & your Mother
Yours truly
J W Quackenbos
