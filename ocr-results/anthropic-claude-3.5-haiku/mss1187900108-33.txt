[Letterhead]
OFFICE OF
C. B. BARNES,
SECRETARY OF THE
REPUBLICAN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE,
OF RANDOLPH COUNTY.

Cuthbert, Ga., 18[9]

Printer Given your notice of the Clara - I wish you would send me one of them. Your law, being lack, I wish you to do me this favor as I have spent a great deal of money this year for the Republican Cause, and is now greatly in debt for the same. And the Republican Executive Committee have sent a petition to Mr. Manassas Postmaster, asking the appointment of Mr. Charles Taunton to the Post Office at this place (Cuthbert Ga) This will help me to secure my home for the money spent for the party
