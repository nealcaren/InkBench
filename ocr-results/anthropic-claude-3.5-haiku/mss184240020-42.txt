1710 Locust St. Philadelphia
March 21. 1871

My dear Sister,

I write, first of all, about Edmund, - because after a long talk we held about himself last night, that takes the first place. I read the little note to him from Findlay in which, while they hope to find him better, they say I fear me. - How has been nothing startingly new with him, only thus seems to be less of him from week to week, and his energy and ability to go or do anything grow less continually.
