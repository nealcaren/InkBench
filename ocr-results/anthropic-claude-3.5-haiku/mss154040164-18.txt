My dear Sturdy:

I do not think I could possibly convey to you the very great pleasure and satisfaction your breezy graphic letter gave me. I wanted to know all that you could tell me clearly that it was like a conversation with you.

The Christmas event came and was being up on the pot I may bed for alas was thus. They told us that travelers in the tropics must beware of diarrhoea but I thought myself of quite immune as I had not had such a thing for thirty years, but I got one nevertheless and was in bed a week.
