Worcester, Mass, May 22, 1856

My dear Emma

Your kind favor came
in due time for which all thanks -
The suggestions were sweet to of service
and the kind sayings most grateful
to both of us - My son will stay a year
& is at his school, with an increase
in his salary of $200, and has also
engaged to teach Elocution at the
Oatholic Cooley, where his taste of
employment will be more valuable
than he has had before - and in
addition to this, he is determined
to try at Public Reading the next
winter - and can you furnish him
with a list of the different Lyceum
& - where he might make able to
