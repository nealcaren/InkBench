Columbia, Tenn.
Nov. 5th 1889.

We respectfully call your attention to our letter head and also take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful progress this move is making.

As far as practicable the leading men of cities of our race have been conversant, and up to date, out of the many responses received, not one has withheld endorsement and solid cooperation.

We plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in Fla. that being near the center of the "Chautauquan Belt."

At first the Chautauqua is to be intimately unto place of meeting. The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the famous New York Chautauqua.
