$400     On or before the 1st day of January
1866 we promise to pay Jane E Deane
her heirs or assigns the just and full
sum of four hundred dollars to
current funds, it being for the hire
of a negro slave named Turner which
negro is to be clothed in the usual man-
ner of hiring with the exception of
a blanket it is understood by the
parties that should the said negro during
be taken in his by the enemy or the
Confederate authorities, the amt of the
time cost shall be deducted from the
hire; for the time payment is withheld.  Jointly and severally
our joint & several heirs, jointly, & severally
over hands & seals this 4th day of Jany
1865     A.H.R. Williams
