Hartford, Oct. 16th 1782

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment of Three Pounds and ten pence - Exeter Cyrus
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1780
[partially illegible text], as stated by the Committees of the State and of the Army.
