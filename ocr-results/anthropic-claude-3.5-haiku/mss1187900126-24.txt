Rochester Oct 14 - 9 4

My Dear Brother Frederick:

This is a fine crisp
cold morning, and while
the girls are taking their
last nap I will pen a line
to you! One hour in the
morning is about the only
real quiet time I have,
the rest of the day is so
full of work and duties.
We are all settled down to
our easy day life. Each day
has its own yoke know,
and what with getting
ready to move and keeping
things going here I find
1741
