Jan. 22 1874.
Washington D.C.

My Dear Father.
I have received your kind letter and was every glad to hear from you. but I am so sorry that you feel so a hard going to Rochester. I hope it will be all right there.
When you go there. I shall take good care of your letters and not think of disturbing them in any way. we have had a few very cold days but we have many pleasant winter.
