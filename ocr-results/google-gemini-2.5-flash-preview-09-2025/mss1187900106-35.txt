Newport R. I. Monday Aug 22, 1892
My Dear Don José-
I have just found
in the Daily Tatler the report
of last year's Hampton Day
Glancing over it and mistaking
Aug. 1 to be the date of the
impressions [[]] will
notice I wrote this before
hand in a great hurry.
