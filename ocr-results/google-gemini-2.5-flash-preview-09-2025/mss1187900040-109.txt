But it realy seems that
I am never - never to have
what I wish for. -
Every thing seems against
me. I wonder if some
of us are realy destined
to suffer. - Perhaps it is
the only means of
softening the heart, - and
of teaching us true
sympathy. - O! to - Ah!
it is better to be afflicted
for it is terribeel to be
indifferent to the sorrow
of our fellow creatures
I know by sad experience
what it is - to be surrounded
by those - upon whom
fortune ever smiles -
Dand hard hearted they
are. - Their coldness is worse
to bear - than the sorrow which
breaks the heart. -
