Department of State
Washington 17\textsuperscript{th} June, 1871.

Frederick Douglass, Esq.
Washington, D.C.

Sir:
Your letter to the President of
the 6\textsuperscript{th} instant, resigning the position
of Member of the Legislative Council
of the District of Columbia, for the
first District, has been received
and accepted, to take effect ten
days after the 6\textsuperscript{th} instant.
I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Hamilton Fish
