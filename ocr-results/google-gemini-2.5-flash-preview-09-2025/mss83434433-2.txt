Dear Sir
I have thought of an expedient to bring
in Caroline which I submit to you. It is that Gene
Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mississippi.
Now if you could dispense with Janet & see Gene all
& let her go & stay her negro & get it out that he has bought
her. it would [?] sure bring her in & let it be out
that it is an arrangement of mine over which you have
no control: What do you think of it? Let me know
Yours truly
Mr O'Neile
John St. Seny
