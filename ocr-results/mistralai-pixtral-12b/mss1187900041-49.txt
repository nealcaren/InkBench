Washington D.C.
April 13, 1869

Dear Brother,

Your reply to my letter was received yesterday.

I see what you said in your letter to Lewis Steward that you suffered me to be charged for neglect of duty or some other such cause, but such was not the case. General Howard did not know at first that I was among the number, and he intimated to me afterwards that he had been a member of the law class that had been retained. The following is the truth.

Mr. Huntington has taken charge of the law department of Howard University and has about twenty colored students -
