Rochester Oct 14 - 94

My Dear Brother Frederick -

This is a fine crisp
cold morning, and while
the girls are taking their
last nap I will send you a line
to you. An hour after the
morning is about the only
real quiet time I have; the
rest of the day is so
full of work and duties. We are all settled down to
our every day life. Each day
has its own work known,
and what with getting to
ready to move and keeping
things going here I find

1741
