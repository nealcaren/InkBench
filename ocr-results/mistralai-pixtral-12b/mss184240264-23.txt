# U. S. Signal Office.

## Summit of Pike's Peak, Colorado Territory.

### 14,216 Feet Above Sea Level.

**Mrs. Anna E. Dickinson**

**Philadelphia.**

The undersigned members of the U.S. Signal Corps have the honor to respectfully
by request the pleasure of your company at
the opening of the Summit Station,

**Saturday, October 11, 1873.**

Geo. Bommer, Observer in charge.
L. E. Seebee, Assistant.
J. H. Smith.
L. A. Lemman.
E. W. Boutelle.
