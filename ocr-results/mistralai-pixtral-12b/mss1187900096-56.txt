My dear friends: -

We (Charlotte and I) congratulate you upon your safe arrival back to Washington. Charlotte is enjoying herself in North Carolina for a few weeks. Her Uncle remains at New Gotham. Miss Mo. comes frequently to pass the day with me and I return the visit. She suggested my writing to you. Writing has become quite a burden to her.
