THE AMERICAN SOCIETY
FOR THE PREVENTION OF CRUELTY TO ANIMALS,
Headquarters: South Street, cor. 23d St.

New York, June 28, 1885

Hon. Frederick Douglas
House of Representatives, Washington, D.C.

Dear Sir:

I beg to enclose a letter from the Superintendent of Hudson Asylum, the Hon. the late Otto Loring, along with a letter from me enclosing to you as Enclosure, copy of which I forward to you with this.

You will please take such action in the matter of supplying a substitute, as you deem proper, and believe me -

Yours with great respect

Henry Bergh
