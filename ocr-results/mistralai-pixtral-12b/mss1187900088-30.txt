The respectfully call your attention to our letter head and ask you to take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful progress this move is making, as far as is practicable. The leading men of cities of our race have been conferenced with, and with to act, out of the many responses received, not one has withheld endorsement and active co-operation.

The plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in July. That being near the center of the "Baptismian Belt."

At first the Chautauqua is to be the "initial" and the place of meeting. The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the famous New York Chautauqua.
