### BOUND VOLUMES OF "OUR YOUNG FOLKS"

Volumes One and Two of "Our Young Folks," comprising the Numbers for 1866 and 1867, have been bound uniformity in cloth, with gilt edges, in quarter-mohagony, with gilt edges, in yellow cloth, and in red cloth. These binders have also made a uniform binding for the three volumes of "Your Own Story," that will be published next Autumn. handsomely bound in cloth with gilt edges, will be ready for the Libraries of the United States except Nevada and the Territories, in a short time.
