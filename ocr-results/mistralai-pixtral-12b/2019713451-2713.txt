# Scott's Report of American Fashions,
### Spring and Summer, 1864.

#### 156 Broadway,
#### New York.

**Contents of the Present Report of Fashions.**

This Magazine, is explanatory in English, French and Spanish languages.  It contains Plates designed to represent the dress of General officers in the three Armies, and giving all necessary directions for Cutting and Making Garments.

The work is complete, and includes the Uniform and Dress of the Navy and Army.
