Hd.Qrs. 8th Regt. U.S.C.T.
Chas. Hopkins Farm, Va.
January 17th 1865

Balley, Romanzo C.
Capt. 8th U.S.C.T.
Comdg. Regt.

Requests a leave of Absence
for Twenty (20) days for the
purpose of visiting Vigo
Penn. on business

Hd. Qrs 8th Regt. U.S.C.T.
Chas. Hopkins Farm Va.
January 17th 1865

Respectfully forwarded.
I certify that the within application, will not make Twenty 
(20) days; from the dates of this Request, if he receives it,
(20) of his present strength, that
I do not belong to one years Regt.
that I have not returned from
a leave of absence since the 1st of
April 1864; that I have been in
the service more than one year
that my term of service does
not expire before the 1st of May
1865; that I have never been
tried or convicted of any offense
by a court Martial; and I am
a member of a Regt. raised
in the State of Pennsylvania.
The number of Officers present
in my Regt. is - 20
Number Absent is 12
The number of enlisted men
present in my command is - 538
Number Absent is - 258

R.C. Ballay
Capt. 8th U.S.C.T.
Comdg. Regt.
