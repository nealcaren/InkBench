1859 July 12th Juarez, constitutional President of Mexico, confiscates the ecclesiastical property of his country.

1859 Oct 3rd James G. Mason United States Minister to France died at Paris,

1859 Oct 17th John Brown with twenty one companions crossed the Potomac River at Harpers Ferry and entered Virginia, where he and his followers incited the slaves to rebel, and to take up arms against their masters. After a short time Brown was captured and tried and found guilty of treason, and conspiring and advising with slaves and to rebel, and murder in the first degree - for which he was executed on Friday 2nd day of Dec, at Charlestown, Virginia.
