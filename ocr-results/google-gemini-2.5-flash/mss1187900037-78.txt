Worcester,
Mass., Aug. 2, 1866

My dear Friend

Your kind favor came
in due time for which all thanks.
Its suggestions were sensible (of service,)
and its kind sayings most grateful
to both of us. You will stay where
he is at school, with an increase
in his salary of $200. and has also
engaged to teach elocution at the Catholic college, where his rate of
compensation will be more liberal
than he has had before - and in
addition to this, he is determined
to try at public reading the next
winter - and can furnish those
with a list of the different lyceums
- where he might be able to
make engagements? I can but
