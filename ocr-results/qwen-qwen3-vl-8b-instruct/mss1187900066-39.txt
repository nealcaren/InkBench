Concord June 14 1885

My dear Friend:

I have sent
you down perhaps
my book in a fragmentary
form, not having our
whole proofs enough to
give you what I have
done, just yet.  I shall
soon have advance sheets
of the whole 650 pages
and will send you an
early copy showed you
define it.  As I get
back parts from other
places I have sent
them, I'll send you
more.  Doubtless I'll
close the end of
Chapters XIV, including
Brickett's account
