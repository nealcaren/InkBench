A decisive victory seemed about to crown the efforts of
the rebels, at this critical moment—a delivery suddenly ap-
peared, Gen. McCallum, rejoined to the Federal gunboats
to open fire on the enemy, from their ponderous shells
burst amongst them, whole ranks were battered to the
earth. confusion quickly spread through their columns,
and they who a few moments before, were confident of
victory, were themselves compelled to give way,
Gen. Heintzelman determined to recover the fortunes of
the day, by making a desperate charge, The gunboats
now ordered to cease firing. This veteran collapsed
from column to column and cheered his men to the
attack, the enemy met them with firmness, but had
gradually to give way, their lines soon broke, and
eventually fled from the field in confusion, The
rebels previously captured a large number of Cannon, big
portions of the batteries of Paudae, Molt, Sopup. The
goal of the Campaign was reached in safety, and
the soldiers of the Union, saved the honor of the
