Hartford, Octob. 19th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of thirteen pounds and sever pence
Exeter, Cyrus
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1784
left, as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army.  In behalf of Exeter Cyrus
£. 13. 0 7/-
Cha. Fanning Esqr.

Voucher in Elias Lamberts Book of this date.
