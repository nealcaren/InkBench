I know not where to find justice for them; I appeal to your judgment. I have ever been friendly to the Colored Race, having served many years at Cape Haytian in the Island of Hayti, where my husband, was a permanent Commercial Agent, under the name of Wilson, Laroches & Co. We are well known to the present President of Hayti, as for years he was interested in the purchase of Coffee, for the House of Wilson, Laroches & Co. The Wilson was also Consul for the United States, and the Recognition of Hayti was effected by him. For such just record, in the State Department. Be so kind as to write for us to influence public opinion in the favor of the Colored People.

Begging you to excuse me for addressing you. I remain

Very Respectfully,

Frances L. Whiss
