Dear Sir,

I have thoughts of an expedient to bring in Caroline which I submit to you.  It is the Gen. Mosely is mustering his force to leave for Mopopeppi.  Now if you could dispense with Janet & see Gene old & let her go & stay his negro & get it out that he has bought her, & it would I am sure bring her in.  Let it be out that it is an arrangement of mine over which you have no control: What do you think of it?  Let me know.

Yr. truly,
John O’Neile
John St. Lewis
