ALBERT H. WALKER,
COUNSELOR AT LAW,
(Including Patent Causes.)

Hartford, Connecticut,

4st by the colored leaders, take
to announcing the Dummies law; they will alienate the sympathies
of two best friends within North.
The adoption of slavery; the prohibition of discriminating
State laws; the extension of
the ballot to the colored man:
These are secure.  So the colored
leaders announce anybody for
not also furnishing by laws, the
authenticated and a sound exclusion
of colored people from
particular private property
such as hotels & theaters it
will look rather childish.  It
will tend to show that the colored
people care more for the show
than for the substance of citizenship.
