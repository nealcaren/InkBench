To the Speakers Bureau:

Three roses more sweet, beautiful, temper disdelling, but
Poes sind Verboten.

Tuck your money bodyguard the suit to vindicate my
character.  Please don't disobey orders again.
Shade a vile temper, growing vile every day which
shows what policies well as for a woman.  It
shows what policies well as for a woman.  It
is a daily comfort to witness the pleasantry of
the Speakers Bureau!

New York City, October
1915-

The Terror
