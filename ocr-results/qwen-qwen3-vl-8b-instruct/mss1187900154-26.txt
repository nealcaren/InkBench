WORLD'S COLUMBIAN COMMISSION.
OFFICE OF THE
Director-General of the Exposition.

Chicago, Ill., U.S.A. June 1st.

My dear Mr. Douglass:

I plan for the East coast
to-morrow morning & now I have put off
long my calling on you & Mrs. Douglass that
I shall be unable to do so.  Do make my
excuses to your poor wife for me.  I remember
her with so much pleasure in Paris.  I hope
to return to Chicago later in the summer.
Then I will not fail to see you both.  &
The meanwhile believe me,

Very cordially,

Theodore Staater,
26 West 61st St.,
New York.
