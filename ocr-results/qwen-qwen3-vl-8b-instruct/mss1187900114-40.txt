The Remembrance. All the
above notes prompt me to
subject you and your wife to
a trial visit. I have been
searching for days to find out
your abiding home and failed
correctly to get it until yesterday.
And now a line from you will
give me pleasure to say when,
where, and at what time you can
receive a call from me on yourself
and wife (whom I have not met)
our homes are not far apart - Salem, Ohio.
Do you remember Benjamin Bown? Wife
old Anti-Slavery Stal Wives. They had an under
ground daughter. She married John DeWitt.
Mr. Denning's daughter is married to her. Mr.
Denning has an exhibit
at Salem Ohio. The Denning's have an exhibit
in Manufacturer Hall. The husband has been
ill and have not yet got to the Fair. Saw
you through my glass on Dedication Day in
October. Wished Truly yours, Henrietta Harris
Ramader.
