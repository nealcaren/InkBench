Directions
for Chicken,

Scrape off the gills or juice.
Take a Table Spoon full of
flour, add Salt, pepper to taste.
Stir well and dip Chicken in
this mix and let it rest.
Have a non-stick or well-greased
skillet hot; melt butter or
bacon fat in it and quickly
brown the Chicken. Remove
from the Chicken, pour the juice
in the pan.  Sprinkle with a little
flour, stir and let it thicken.
Then add a little cream and
thicken with a little flour.

Brown Bread: Heat with
a pot big enough to contain the
ten or twelve loaves of bread.
