Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Douglas Eng.
Dear Sir:

The enclosed
Clipping from the Kansas City Times
of the 23-rd. I enclose to you for
your verification, or denial, Hundreds
of your Cola admirers, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow.
We have been induced to believe that
both New York, and an Indiana
Crew be fully relied upon to
Cast their Electoral Vote for our
Gallant Standard bearers, Harrison
and Morton. A reply, either
Confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly Obliged

Yours very truly
A. F. Stevens
