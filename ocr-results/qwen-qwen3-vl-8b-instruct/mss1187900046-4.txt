jan, 22 1874.
Washington D.C.

my Dear father,

I have received your kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you, but I am so
sorry that you feel
so a bent going to
Rochester. I hope it will
be all right there,
when you go thair. I
Shill take good care of your
Letters and not think of
displaying them in any way,
we have had a few very
Cold days but we have
very pleasant weather.
