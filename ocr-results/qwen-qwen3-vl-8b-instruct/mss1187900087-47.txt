"of things will continue yet for months.
"If the Governments of Cleveland and Harris
"now had acted in good faith in their rela-
"tions with Hayti, this war would not have
"lasted. -- I ask myself if it would not
"be well for me to go to Port-au-Prince with
"Mr. Douglass to see if the two parties content-
"ing cannot arrive at an honorable understanding".
Now, my dear Douglass, this letter is already
long that I must cut it short right here.
Very sincerely and very cordially yours,
Mr. Bassett and Lotty
