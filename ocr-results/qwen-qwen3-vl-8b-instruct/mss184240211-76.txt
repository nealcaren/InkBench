Thursday—

My dearest—I had his sooner
than that messenger did—both
the letter & you.  Then “Dixie
Dix” herself knocked at the
door.

Of course the trols are a lot
of news.  — I told her both
to go near Libra, if she let
call & go to Boston. — The
looked so small & frail, I
could hardly realize that she
had the spirit of Copper Hill.
