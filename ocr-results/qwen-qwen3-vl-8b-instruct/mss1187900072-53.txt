Charlotte wrote that she received an invitation from
Mrs. Dame (Maria Mitchell's niece),
to attend an Alumnae gathering in Washington, before
which Stephen Foster's daughter
Allie, had been invited to read a paper. Charlotte listened to his address with
absorbing interest for his parents'
sake as well as for her own,
and said it was an orphaned
description of the government
of all the Colleges that
are worthy of the name.
Forty eight in twenty one States.
Miss Foster is a teacher in the
Girls' High School in Boston
(where my Cousin, Cecilia, the one who died, spoken of
above, also teaches).
