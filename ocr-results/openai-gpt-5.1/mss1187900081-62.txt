Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888  

A. Douglass Esq  

Dear Sir  

The inclosed  
clipping from the Kansas City Times  
of the 25th, I inclose to you for  
your verification, or denial, Thousands  
of your [?] adherents, and friends  
in this state, have read the inclosed  
with much surprise and sorrow.  
We have been induced to believe that  
both New York, and Indiana  
could be fully relied upon to  
cast their electoral vote for our  
Gallant standard bearers, Harrison  
and Morton. A reply, either  
confirming or denying the inclosed,  
will greatly oblige  

Yours &c  
H. L. Steinmeyer[?]
