And now let me add that on Easter eve [?] I

proof that Philanthropic money as he called it could  
be found in the North (in fact had been offered)  
at low interest for such work in words as I had invited  
to which he added the suggestions mentioned  
and attracted my attention to Part 1 Page 1 of the  
Daily Inter-Ocean of March [?] giving an account  
of a successful coloured colonization at [?] place  
Alabama. to which I refer you as one to interest  
you. It is a number of 64 pages printed at  
commemoration, I infer, its 21st anniversary which  
contains very interesting items about the Columbian  
Fair! When upon this I heard of your works  
I thought I could not but write you if only to  
ask your opinion & counsel how best to proceed.  

I feel sure of your sympathy for the cause &  
perhaps for auld lang syne? in common, hopefully  
& with very best wishes of the season for your
