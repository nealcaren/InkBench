1853 July 8th. American expedition under Commodore Perry arrives at Japan.

1853 July 18th. Exhibition of the Industry of all Nations opened in New York.

1853 Nov 9th. Inauguration of the Washington Aqueduct.

1853 Dec 16th. Genl Santa Anna elected dictator of Mexico for life. He had formerly been President from 1833 to 1844.

1854 March 25th. A treaty of Commerce concluded between the United States and Japan.

1854 April 29th. The President issues a proclamation announcing the neutrality of the United States in the Eastern question.

1854 June 7th. A reciprocity treaty between the United States and Great Britain, negotiated at Washington respecting transportation, fishing, international trade, &c.
