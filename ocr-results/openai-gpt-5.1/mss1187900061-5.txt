Washington D.C.  
July 15 - 1883.

Dear Grandpa,

We received  
Your affectionate letter on the  
12.th and we were very glad  
to hear from you, and to know  
that you are getting along  
nicely!  
Every thing is passing along  
smoothly here, excepting the  
weather Which is at times al-  
most intolerable. It is now  
about nine oclock and the  
thermometer stands about - 84 1/2.  
Charlie has not left yet-  
for the Eastern Shore, and  
I doubt whether we will or  
not; I think the latter as  
I don't hear him say any.
