That may do for Summer–  
Just this moment comes the  
letter bag with your letter  
thank you Mary dear I  
sympathize with the pang  
you feel at Mr  
Bransons decline. But all  
things work together for good  
to so good souls as J and S, so  
Mary dear never mind.  
There must be something good  
for Jn somewhere, because  
I assure it I am glad  
the children are as good as  
nice pie, I hope they will  
keep so, Tel Mary I want  
to have brine on the butter–  
Please look and see if there  
is any– Love to Mother &  
all– But Most to you.  

A. H. G.
