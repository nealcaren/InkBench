Jackson acting with great alacrity also to crown the efforts of  
the rebels at this critical moment ordered [?] by a flank  
passage, Genl McClellan, apprized of it, ordered the Federal gun boats  
to open fire on the enemy, and their ponderous shells  
burst amongst them, whole ranks were battered to the  
earth, confusion was spread through their columns  
and they who a few moments before were confident of  
victory, were themselves compelled to give way.  
Genl Heintzelman determined to secure the fortunes of  
the day, by making a desperate charge. Fragments of  
Regiments were ordered to cease firing. This Veteran galloped  
from column to column and formed his men for the  
attack, the enemy met them with firmness, but had  
gradually to give way, their lines soon broke, and  
eventually fled [?] from the field in confusion. The  
rebels previously captured a large number of Cannon, being  
portions of the batteries of [?] & [?]. The  
goal of the Campaign was reached in Safety, and  
the soldiers of the Union saved the honor of the  
nation with the blood of its patriots.
