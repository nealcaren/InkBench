To the Speakers Bureau:  
Those roses were sweet, beautiful, temper dispelling, but  
Roses sind Verboten.  
Tuck your money toward the suit to vindicate my  
Character. Please dont disobey orders again.  
I have a vile lumbago, groaning ribs every day which  
show what jollities will not do for a woman  
is a daily comfort [?] the placidity of  
the Speakers Bureau!  
New York City, October  
1915 -  
The Terror  

[*Oct 6 in office*]
