REMARKS.

1. All officers and soldiers are to be taken up on the rolls, as soon as assigned to the  
company, whether present or absent.  

2. The names are to be arranged in the following order: Captain, First Lieutenant, Second  
Lieutenant, First Sergeant, Sergeants, Corporals, Musicians, Artificers, and Privates.  

3. The remarks opposite each name, in the column of remarks, will show distinctly the  
condition of the officer or soldier – whether present or absent; and if absent, the  
cause of and since when, &c.  

4. The column of “Joined for service and enrolled” is intended to show when, where, and  
by whom the officer or soldier was enrolled; that of “Mustered into service,” when,  
where, and by whom mustered – whether by a commissioned officer or United States  
mustering officer.  

5. In all cases of death, desertion, or discharge from the service, the cause, place,  
and date are to be stated in the column of remarks.
