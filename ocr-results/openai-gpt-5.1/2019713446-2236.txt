Rochester Aug 1/59  

Aurelius Conklin Esq  

Delhi  

Herewith I  
hand you one dollar which I  
am told is the Amt required  
to secure me a Copy Right  
on my New Game, the  
title of which I inclose  
If you have the goodness  
to return me Copy right  
for same Very much oblige  

Yours very Truly  

G. W. Fisher
