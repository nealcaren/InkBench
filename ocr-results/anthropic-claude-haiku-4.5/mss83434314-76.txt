1. All officers and enlisted men on to be taken up on the rolls, as soon as assigned to the company, or company's command during, as they have been paid on and left before, and may be taken on the list whenever they are paid on the list.

2. Under the head of ENLISTED, the date when any appointment before office, the Sn. date when commissioned; under Rank, the grade or rank; and under Joined for Service and Promoted, the date when promoted; under When, the date when promoted; under By whom, the name of the officer by whom promoted; under Mustered into Service, the date when mustered into service.

3. As the Pay Roll must agree with the Muster Roll, every name appearing on the Pay Roll must also appear on the Muster Roll. The dates of the commencement and ending of the payment, which must correspond with the dates on the Muster Roll, must be inserted opposite each name. When a soldier is promoted, a new entry must be made on the Pay Roll, and a line should be drawn through the name on the previous entry. All officers and men who have been paid on account of a previous entry must be struck off the roll before they leave for home or otherwise disposed of according to Army Regulations.
