Washington, D.C., NOV 5     191

Journal Friends

There is to be a Suffrage national here Nov 14 and 15th and we all have a womans Journal both. Can you send us any broadside and a large card to have some Newsie Page- and I have about 50 subscription Blanks.
I've never done anything of this sort and have little idea how to go about it.
But Dr. McMaughton who is putting up the festival telephone this morning that Alice Paul had sent her word she would not pay the cost of creating a booth for the suffrage and if she Dr. [?] bad to pay the cost of erecting the booth she would let it be used for the Journal the paper she preferred to help. I promised to see to it.
