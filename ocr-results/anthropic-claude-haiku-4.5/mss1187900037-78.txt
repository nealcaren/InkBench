Worcester, Mass. Aug 2. 1840

My dear Friend

Three kind, love came
in due time for which all thanks -
It suggested well double of service
and his kind sayings much gladdened
to both of us - Your will stay where
he is, a school, with an increase
in his Salary of B200. and he also
engaged to teach Elocution at the
Wincluele College, when his fate of
compensation will be made liberal
than he has had before - and in
addition to this, he is determined
to try at Public Reading, the next
winter - and can for afrewick there
with a sick of the different Cannog
& - when he musquit be able to
make engagements - I can both
