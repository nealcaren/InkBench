1862 June 17th Evacuation of Cumberland Gap, Tenny. The federals
under Genl Morgan advanced towards his place for the purpose
of spelling the rebel Genl Shimmun & Smith who occupied
the gap with 13,000 men and well fortified the same. The
gap is a defile in the Cumberland mountains, and is situated
narrow that there is through the gap for only a single
winding. It was expected that the rebels would defend
its possession until the utmost tenacity. It was never
followed. When the rebels new informed of the approach
of the federals, they precipitately evacuated the place.
