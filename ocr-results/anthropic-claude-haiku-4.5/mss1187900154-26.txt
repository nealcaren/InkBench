Chicago, W. 11th St.  June 16.

My dear Mr. Douglass:

I lean for the last sag
Towards meeting & how I hav put off so
long my calling on you & Mrs. Douglass that
I shall be much to sorry. Do make my
excuses to your just wife for me. I remember
her with so much pleasure in Paris & hope
to see her return to Chicago later in the Summer.
Then I will not fail to see you both. & I
the meanwhile I have my,

Vy [cordially?] yours,

Theadore Stanton,
26 West 61st St.,
New York
