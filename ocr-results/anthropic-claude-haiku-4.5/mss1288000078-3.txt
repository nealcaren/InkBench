I came home from the city one afternoon, & was greeted by Maggie with the pleasing intelligence that Mr. Klaude's Johnny had pulled out the plug of one of the stationary tubs, in which new soap had been put to harden - the soak had gone down into the pipes & hardened there, stopping the escape of the waste water, & promising us a abundant trouble & a long plumber's bill, before things could be set right. Mr. K's oldest boy is a quiet well behaved lad who never
