Hartford, Octot 1st 1782

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treafurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen Pounds and seven pence —
it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January 1782
as flated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army. In behalf of State Cyrus —
£.13.07 —
Che Treasurer kept
L.B. O.7 —

Vouchers in Chas Lambertin file of the date
