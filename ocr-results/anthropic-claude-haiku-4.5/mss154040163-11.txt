My dear Brady;

I thank't thank you for this letter and the notes and clippings concerning Mrs Bliss. I thank you especially for the clippings for as you suspect, I am interested in her outgoing last year, in Buffalo, at the National Convention. During Mrs Sweet brought a letter she had received from Effie Vance, and she had described very vividly how Mrs Bliss had been able to rise above her suffering thro Christian Science. Deuchss hears from Ed as she read it, and she was no great admirer of Mrs B. I believe she bid me that Ed Vance was also getting interested in Ch Sci Mrs B was one of the most generous and forgiving natures I ever met, and altho she also had her mahogues of Christian, as well as her rest of us, she was a woman to be admired for very many admirable qualities.
