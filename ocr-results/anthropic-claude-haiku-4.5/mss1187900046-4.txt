June 22
1874.

Washington D.C

My Dear Father.

I have received your kind letter and was very glad to hear from you. I had I am so sorry that you feel so. a heat going to Rochester. I hope it will be all right there. When you go there, I shall take good care of your letters and not think of destroying them in any way. we have had a few very cold days here we have very pleasant weather.
