Concord June 14 1885
dearest Friend,
I have deubt
your second letter & my book in a Box made
by Form not leaving our
objects for you - I & I
gave you all of turned
take just yet. I shall
have a strong shelf
& the whole 650 pages
and well send you in
Eoply offy Shavespear
delight. It. As I get
back from Bern other
foldern I have Sent
them, surely Send you
more. Forbey d Sm
eclose the ad & 
Chaffee XIV. enderly
Boucledti as Count his
his visit & Berm in
