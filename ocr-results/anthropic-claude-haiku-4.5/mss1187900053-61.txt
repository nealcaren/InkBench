Poneggar Villa,
Bridport.
Dorset
May 16th 1899

Dear Mr Douglass
It is a very long time since we have written to you or heard from you, and Lissie is now in hopes of proposing a reply. We really are so perplexed in the dark about you, & do not even know whether you still hold office under the Government, as we have not been to the Country. I conclude that you do - I trust that you have not been shelving & hobbing meetings over the country like it would as of old. Lissie had your health is good. & that you can report well of yourself & your family
