ILLEGITIMATE BIRTHS
IN PRUSSIA NOW 10%

"Evidence of Moral Healthiness
of German Race," Berlin
Councillor Declares.

[* Addenda to Dec. 14/1917 *]

AMSTERDAM, Dec. 12.—(Associated
Press)—The number of illegitimate children
born in Prussia in wartime has
shown a remarkable increase of from
3 to 10 per cent, according to statements
made by Dr. Heimann, a Berlin Councillor,
in which he declares that this large
increase is evidence of the moral healthiness
of the German race. He criticises
the proposal to register all illegitimate
children at an appeal for the withdrawal
of all legal distinctions between legitimate
and illegitimate children. It may seem,
he says, that registration of illegitimate
children is important, in so far as it
specialty aim after all, the idea of the
family would be destroyed. He urges that
this is opposed to the very essence of
German feeling and one yet permissible to
German sense.

The terrible fact, however, is that
war has come upon so many of these
children reach their adolescent year, as
compared with the 2 or 3 per cent. of
legitimate children. This condition, says
the German councillor, shows some of
improvement of the condition of an
underland, which is responsible for the
terrible death-rate. He urges a good
policy to be followed regarding the rearing
of these children should be started.

A further owing to the card system,
which has been adopted in the matter,
the children could be reached early
in their life by these cases of truth, scientific
training, and thereby be removed from
the terrible environment. The system
has been in operation for years and has
already reached a total of nearly 200,000 yearly.
