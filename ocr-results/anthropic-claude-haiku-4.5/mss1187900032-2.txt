Supper we all went to the Parody
to shall good Shatter besides Upper
Eight & then I came on aged
him helping them to aged. When
been boorne beds all long besides,
will for and belonging's mayske
both me awed if it wandlesum
I wish that one would have them
Back while may ther have go. I was
Cold & how telles Short as I'm alents
bedtime and we omead be up
at five o'clock I should like to
hear from home soon on a while
and if you shall have from over
Please let us not yet good
feeling as eyes would toll
but I am we hacle to good
night.

From your affectionate
Son Charles B. Douglass
