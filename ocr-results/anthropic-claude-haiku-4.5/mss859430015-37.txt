June 21, 1958.

My dear daughter. Miss
Mack came yesterday
and brought the box and
visited with me awhile.
She said she had a nice
trip up and working at
home and cleaning away
weeds in the like. The back and
we were very glad
to talk with you Sunday-
better. It helps Perkes, felt
well at present. Brother
and family are quite
well. He doesn't come so
often now. is still work-
ing sent at Jackson, and
help. I hope welfare
and work you are well
for you comes home.
now that school is out I
suppose some of the child-
ren will be out to stay.
much love from mother.
