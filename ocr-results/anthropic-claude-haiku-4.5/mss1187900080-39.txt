Kansas City, Fort Scott & Memphis Railroad Co.
Marion Arkansas
September 2nd 1888

Hon. Frederick Douglass
Washington D.C.
Dear Sir

I am an Ex Union Soldier and have endeavored to be a consistent Republican ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the civil War and in any humble sphere in life have been a friend to the Colored race. It certainly seems to me that the cure of absolute slavery & still rests upon the Country and bears heavily upon the colored race. I reside at present in S[?]
