THE REPUBLICAN LEAGUE,
NEW HAVEN, CONN.

September 28, 1890.

My dear Douglas:
     On my passage thro'
New York yesterday from Phila. I stopped on Friday evening. I called at Kenn-
hardt & Cod office for news about the sailing
of our steamer. And this is what the clerks
in the office assured me of:
     The steamer is the "Oranje Nassau"
She will sail from Bineapart Stores (along
side of the Wall street ferry) Brooklyn,
on Friday next, 4th instant, at noon, for
Port au Prince.
     I presume that we can count on this
information as a tually reliable.
     I have always understood that the
"Oranje Nassau" is the poorest steamer of the
line. But she is steamd endosin at &
guess we can stand it for bis bays on board.
