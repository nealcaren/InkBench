1853. July 8th. American expedition under Commodore Perry arrives at Japan.

1853. July 14th. Exhibition of the Industry of all Nations, opened at New York City.

1853. Sept. 8th. Remaining portion of Falls Park, at the Falls of Niagara brought forth.

1853. Dec. 9th. Inauguration of the Washington Aqueduct.

1853. Dec 16th. Genl, Santa Anna, elected dictator of Mexico for Life.
He had formerly been President from 1833-53 1845;

1854. March 5th. A Treaty of Commerce concluded between the United 
States and Japan.

1854. April 20th. Miss Dix'sagitate bill ameliorating the condition of the insane 
asylum, vetoed,

1854. April 28th. The President issues a proclamation, announcing 
the neutrality of the United States in the Eastern question,

1854. June 5th. A reciprocity treaty between the United States and
Great Britain concluded at Washington, respecting Newfoundland 
Fishery, international trade, &c.
