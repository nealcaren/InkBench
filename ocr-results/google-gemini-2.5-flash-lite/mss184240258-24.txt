Logansport, Ind., Oct 12 1888.
Miss Anna Dickinson
Care The National Republican Committee
New York City, N.Y.
My Dear Miss Dickinson:
Your Valued rote of the 6th
last was duly received.
I have expressed your thank
to Mr Brown for the Indian
reference & to Mr. Bowen for the more
extended notice of your address in our
Logansport Journal.
Mates, thy brothers are well
And you seene expressing our high
regard & Consideration for you and
yours.
I was yesterday at Indiana-
polis where we had a marvelous demon-
stration - We hope some of your friends
will have to regret Indiana after Nov. 6.
I have noted the influence
& imporessim you have made where
you have been since coming here
