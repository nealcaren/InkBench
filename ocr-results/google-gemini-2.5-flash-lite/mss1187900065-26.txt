THE AMERICAN SOCIETY
FOR THE
Prevention of Cruelty to Animals,
Headquarters, Fourth Ave., cor. 22d St.

New York Jan 28 1885

Hon: Frederick Douglas
House o Representatives, Washington, D.C.
Idea Sir:
I yesterday, deposited with the Surrogate of Hudson
Co. N. Jersey, the Will of the late Otilea Assing, along
with a letter from me declining to serve as Executor,
Copies, of which I forward to you with this.
You will please take such action in
the matter of supplying a substitute, as you deem proper,
and believe me -
Yours with great respect
Henry Bergh
Deigh
