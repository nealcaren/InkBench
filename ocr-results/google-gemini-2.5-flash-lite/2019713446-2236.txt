Rochester Aug 1 / 59

Aurelius Conklin Esq.

Deir Sir

Herewith I
have for you one dollar which I
am told is the amt required
to seccure me a Copy Right
on Mr. new Game, the
title of which I enclose
If right have the goodness
to return me Copy Right
for same. They Much oblige

Yours very Truly
G. W. Fisher
