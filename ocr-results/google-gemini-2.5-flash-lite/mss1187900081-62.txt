Ellis, Kansas, Oct 26 1888

A. Douglas Esq.
Dear Sir

Rival

The enclosed
Clippings from the Kansas City Times
of the 23rd I enclose to you for
your verification, or denial, Hundreds
of you Cola admirers, and friends
in this state, have read the enclosed
with much surprise and sorrow,
We have been induced to believe that
both New York, and a Indiana
Cola be fully relied upon to
Cast their electoral vote for our
Galleant Standard bearer. Harrison
And Mesign. A reply, either
Confirming or denying the enclosed,
will greatly oblige

Yours ye
G. S. Seissening
