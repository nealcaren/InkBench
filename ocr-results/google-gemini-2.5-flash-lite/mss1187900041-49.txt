Washington D.C.
April 13th 1869

Dear Father,

Your reply to my letter was
received yesterday.
From what you said in your letter to
Lewis I feared that you supposed I was
discharged for neglect of duty, or some other
such cause, but such was not the case.
Genl. Howard did not know at first that
I was among the number and he informed
me afterwards that had I have been a member of the Law Class I might 
have been retained. The following is the
truth:

Mr. Langston has taken charge of the
Law Department of the Howard University 
and has about twenty colored students-
