jan. 22, 1874.
Washington D.C.

My Dear Father.
I have received your
kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you, but I am so
sorry that you feel.
so. a hunt going to
Rochester, I hope it will
be all Right thair.
when you go thair. I
shall take good care of your
letters and not think of
distraying them in any way,
we have had a few very
Cold days but we have
very pleasant weather.
