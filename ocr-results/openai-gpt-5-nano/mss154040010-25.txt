No. 81. Monkey Temple. Two hundred monkeys live in the trees surrounding the temple. Only one cot in the picture. They eat from the hands of strangers and the mother monkeys snatch away their babies when they think they have had enough, and run up the trees with them.

No. 82. The burning ghats of the Hindoo on shore of Ganges. The body on the frame is lying feet in the holy water of the river before burning. About six may be burning at once. When completed, the attendants sweep ashes, and unburned fingers, legs or arms into the river.
