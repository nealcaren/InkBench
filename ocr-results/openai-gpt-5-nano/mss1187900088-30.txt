FIRST ANNUAL ASSEMBLY, LAKE JACKSON, LEON COUNTY, FLORIDA.

Columbia, Tenn.
Nov. 5th 1879.

Hon. Frederick Douglass,
My honored Sir:—

We respectfully call your attention to our letter head and also take great pleasure in informing you of the hopeful progress this move is making.
As for as practicable the leading men of letters of our race have been consulted with, and up to date, out of the many responses received, not one has withheld endorsement and assistance cooperation.
The plan is to have the Chautauqua hold its first meeting in Fla that being near the center of the “Carthaginian Belt.”
At first the Chautauqua is to be itinerant and to place of meeting. The aim is to gradually work up to the limits of the famed New-York Chautauqua.
