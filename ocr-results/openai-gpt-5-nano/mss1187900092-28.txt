Dear Grandpa:-

I have learned with much surprise, that you are still in the city of [illegible] enjoying an extended leave of absence from your post of duty, I am very glad that such a leave has been granted you, and I know that you are far happier no [illegible] Hill than any place you can go and I only wish that mother could be arranged as to allow you to remain in this place if she were.
