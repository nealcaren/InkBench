Winchester, Aug 12, 1845

My dear Friend

Your kind favors came in due time for which all thanks and suggestions were sensible of service and its kind sayings most grateful to both of us. You will stay where he is at school, with an increase in his salary of $200, and has also engaged to teach 6 lessons at the Catholic College, where his rate of compensation will be more liberal than he has had before - and in addition to this, he is determined to try at Public Reading, the next week - and can you furnish him with a list of the different Lyceums where he might be able to make engagements?
