Hartford, Octobr 18th 1782

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment of Thirteen pounds and ten shillings — it being the Balances due to me, on the first Day of January [1782], as stated by the Committees of the State and of the Army. [In the margin:] In the hands of Letters Cogn… [illegible] [signature line]. 

L.13.0? [amount written in longhand]

[Signature:] Chas. Hanning, Treasr.

Vouchers in Elias Lambeths, Me[et] of this date.
