FREEDMEN'S BUREAU                                                   11

"VI. Brigadier General R. Saxton is hereby appointed inspector of settlements and plan-tations, and will at once enter on the performance of his duties. No change is intended or desired in the settlement now on Beaufort island, nor will any rights to property heretofore acquired be affected thereby.

"By order of Major General W. T. Sherman:

"L. M. DAYTON, Assistant Adjutant General."

In Virginia quite an amount of land was libelled and about to be sold by the marshal just previous to the establishment of the bureau, when the sales were suspended by the Secretary of War, in order that these lands might be turned over to the bureau for the benefit of the freedmen.
