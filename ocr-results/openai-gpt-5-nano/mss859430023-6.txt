H-6-58

Parks, my dear husband;  
How are you? I hope you are well and that mother is all right. Does she still take her medicines? I hope she is keeping it up, or that she will have a doctor to check her, (if she isn’t well), I am sending the income tax form for you to sign and mail to the Michigan Revenue Office. Brother or some one else can address it for you. I don’t have the list of places with me. We have a refund so you may sign the check, mail it to me and I will cash it and send your part if you want it.
