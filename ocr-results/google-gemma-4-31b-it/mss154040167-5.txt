[* Love and thoughts from Rola *]

POST CARD
BRITISH MADE

Correspondence
We are fairly on our way
the ship and the room are
satisfactory. We left promptly
in time and will be stopping
at Cherbourg this eve-
ning when the mail will get
mailed. All is well with us
and you will get a letter before
we are over. It is very cold today-
nothing to write about it.

Miss Clara Hyde
317 W 45 St
New York

[* 15 2 31 *]
[* CHERBOURG *]
