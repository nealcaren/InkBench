**To Chester Aug 1 / 59**  
**Andrias Condlin**  
Ery

**Dear  
Herewith I  
hand you one dollar  
which I  
am told is the Army  
repine  
to return me a Copy  
Right  
on my new farm ,  
the  
title of Which  
linclaire  
if right have the products  
to return me Copy right  
for farm they much oblige**

**Your very truly**  
G. W. Fisher
