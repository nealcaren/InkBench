**Additional Notations**

1. **Illegible Text:** Portions of handwritten notes in margins are faded or smudged.   
   - Example: "[Illegible text]"

2. **Symbols/Abbreviations:** 
   - "&" for "and," "st" for "street," "np-m-m" for coins or other abbreviations.
   - Fractions like "27 1/2" and measurements like "40 miles" preserved.
   - [Cp] used for copied material across pages.

3. **Dates:** Transcribed in original archaic spelling (e.g., "Monthy" instead of "Month").   

4. **Notes on Physical Document:** 
   - Page divisions marked by the typist as "Page" headers blended with content.
   - Handwritten annotations copied verbatim, including editorial markings like crossing out text.

5. **Marginalia:** Handwritten notes preserved, even if inconsistent with main text direction (e.g., reversed or cropped lines).   

6. **Repetitions:** Closing signatures written multiple times ("George Washington") retained as is.
