

Title Page for Henry Whittaker's Book "The Codes"
This is a title page for Henry Whittaker's book "The Codes," with the date listed as 1852 at the bottom of the page. The book was entered into the register of copyrighted books in 1852 by H. Whittaker and published by Edward Jenkins, printer at 114 Nassau Street in New York. This edition was printed at the Treadwell and Perkins Printing House in New York before being sold and bound by Kallahan. There is a "C" noted as the book's state at the bottom of the page. An ink note dated "June 29, 1855" is also present at the top of this page.
