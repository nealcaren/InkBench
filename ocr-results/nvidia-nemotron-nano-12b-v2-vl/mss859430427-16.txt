

**General Transcription Guide**  
**Core Principles**  
- Transcribe as the text appears on the image.  
- Do not correct spelling, grammar, or word choice. Correct numbers only if required by Core Principles.  
- Preserve original formatting (bold, italics, quotation marks, etc.).  

**Text Handling**  
- For blends of letters, use [?] for empty spaces.  
- Do **not** transcribe images; use [[shorthand]] for indecipherable text.  
- Transcribe notes in margins and footers follow the content they relate to.  

**When Not to Transcribe**  
- If the image contains blank pages, select “[Nothing to Transcribe]” and move on to the next document.  

**Special Situations**  
- **Handwriting**: Traditional cursive **should not** be transcribed.  
  - Transcribe each word in the order it appears on the page in outline form as follows:  
    *Kelly 8*  
    *Shann*  
    *TheoryParticularly*.  
  - Notate paragraph digressions with * (asterisk).  
- Nest corrections in **[correction]** as per narrator.  
- **Mixed Handwriting**: If a document contains a blend of lowercase and uppercase lettering, preserve the original formatting.  

**Document-Specific Guidelines**  
- **Tables or Graphs**: Transcribe in paragraph form, focusing on data types and message.
