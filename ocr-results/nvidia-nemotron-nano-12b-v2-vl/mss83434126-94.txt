

### Round-the-World Study Project" "World Crusade Against Communism  

**Where Students Will Go:** The round-the-world journeys of RWTSP students will be conducted by air. Participants will visit the following locations: Washington, Baltimore, London, Paris, Rome, Deferventas, Antwerp, and London. Attendees will complete courses in International Affairs, Cultural Relations, Art Appreciation, Geography, Anthropology, and Field Work. The credit allocated will be between 9 and 12 hours.  

**How the Project is Funded:** Funding for the project will come from cooperative financing. This includes sources such as a Strong’s Cross-writing Committee Support, a Special RTWSP Modification Scholarship Fund, stakeholder tuition fees, and charitable contributions from parents and guardians.
