**Nat Fred Douglas**  
**Dear Sir Francis M Adlington of Weymouth Mass 86 Years of age Kondra the following Offering for the Lincoln Monument Insignification. "Father Abraham Lincoln's Monument:" This Monument would seem to Say that Lincoln's dead but turnd not Do. In Nam the apassione arising in day: In Nam his blood War Made to flow: In Nam the write attacks lift to Kill: In all out heart his living peace: And White in a crop of pathology blood Small flow in any human frame. Guill thrill at Memory of the good; the great and high Lincoln Name their Monument we raise to rash Great Chief whose Mandado Made us free: Our grateful hearts ascend for thee, our true & Countant friend. Also the following: "A Endef to american Personified" the world is gazing on thee. Dispose thy robes with Care And with thy precious jewels Cloth thy Golden hair. "Over."

**2709**
