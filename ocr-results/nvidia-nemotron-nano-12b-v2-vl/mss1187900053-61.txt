

Dear Mr. Douglas

It is a very long time since we have written to
you or heard from you, and
I think you must be hoping of pro-
voking a reply.   We really are
very much in the dark about
you, & don't conveniantly know
your title bold office under the
Government, as you have not heard
from the Cantabury - I include that you
do - & therefore I have not-
been returning your house hold
ever the family that last wrote
as you know.  Hope that your health
is good & That you can report
well yourself & your family
