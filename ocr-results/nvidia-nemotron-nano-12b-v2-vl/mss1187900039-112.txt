Dear Julia

Youree of the 4 "srist. is just received.

I saw an account of one of your lecture in New Jersey published in the N.york Tribune, and I thought
that you might have other appointments, no hearing from you.
All is well except the baby, he has a severe cold. Dr. Beugesta is attending him, we are afraid he will have the cough; but hope not. I dont wish to trouble
your mind about the affair between Nathan, Fred, and mys-self I know you mind, & time is better occu- pied. You
may know the cause of the difficulty; but know you dont
Know the whole truth, and it is hard to find it out under
the circumstances. Dam afraid that New York has gone
against us, place for another man. Oh you will be as home
any length of time I will write again soon, as am
very busy just now. Libbie wouldreu love, if she knew
I was rolling, could be to mother and me.

Yours aff would
The 2013
