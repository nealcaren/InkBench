******Round-the-World Study Project  
World Crusade Against Communism****  
**Where RTWSP Students Will Go:**  
The round-the-world journey of RTWSP (Round-the-World Student Project) will be conducted via air travel, visiting over 25 countries. Key stops include:  
- **U.S. Cities**: Washington, Baltimore, Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Detroit, Chicago, St. Louis, Los Angeles, San Francisco.  
- **Global Destinations**: Hawaii, Wake Island, Tokyo, Formosa (Taiwan), Philippines, Indonesia, Singapore, Cambodia, Siam (Thailand), Burma (Myanmar), India, Pakistan, Arabia, Ethiopia, Sudan, Egypt, Palestine.  
- **Asia/Africa/Europe**: India, Pakistan, Arabia, Ethiopia, Sudan, Egypt, Palestine, British Somaliland (Somalia), Aden, Abyssinia (Ethiopia), Congo, Kasai, Congo Free State, Badlands, Sudan, Egypt.  
- **Europe**: Athens, Rome, Paris, Antwerp, London.  
- **Middle East**: Lebanon, Syria, Palestine.  
- **Asia**: Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, New Guinea, New Zealand.  
The journey concludes in Richmond, Virginia, via Ireland, Canada, and New York.  

**Who Goes:**  
- **Participants**: 50 students from Virginia Union University.  
- **Selection Criteria**: Based on academic ability, recommendation, and demonstrated attitude.  

**College Credit:**  
- **Hours**: 9–12 semester hours.  
- **Credit Applicable To**: International Affairs, Cultural Relations, Art Appreciation, Geography, Anthropology, Fieldwork, Sociology.  
- **Program Design**: Two university professors will lead the project. Activities include lectures, workshops, and collaborative assignments.  

**Source of Funding:**  
- **CO-Operative Financing**: Funds sourced from:  
  - Student contributions and their networks (friends, family, organizations).  
  - A dedicated RTWSP Assistance Scholarship Fund.  
- **Scholarships**: Cover living expenses abroad; administered via foreign governments.  

**Support Structure:**  
- **Scholarship Fund**: Provides need-based aid. Scholarships awarded via a university-appointed committee.
