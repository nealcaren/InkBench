**Main Text**  
•: *On her knees, Mary*,  
(Though my thought  
was but falling light,  
the world was full of noise.)  

•: *Oh, Carousel of Dusk*,  
when stars fade to  
whispers, the wind hums  
**gentle, reminder** that time,  
though soft, does not pause  
(Even when the stars are gone.)  

•: *Carnet of Pages*,  
written with trembling hands,  
each word a *stone* in the river,  
drifted where no eyes may  
watch—yet the river flows,  
and the notes endure.  

] *Asleep in the desert*,  
the moon smiled—  
not upon the dunes,  
but upon the dust  
where dreams, like seeds,  
lay buried  
(Alfred’s first stanza)
