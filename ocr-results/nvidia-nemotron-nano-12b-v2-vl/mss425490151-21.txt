

**Transcript of the Letter from the National Woman’s Party (October 29, 1945):**

**TO: Voters in the Convention by Mail**  
**FROM: Memorandum of the National Woman’s Party**  
**DATE: October 29, 1945**  

The report of the Elections Committee, signed by Dr. Margaret Sebree, Chairwoman, has been handed to me. A copy is enclosed herewith.  

**Election Outcomes:**  
Delegates from all over the country sent in their ballots. Each delegate represented twenty-five members. All nominees proposed by the Nominating Committee were elected. A number of other names were written in, but in many cases, these were members who had expressed their inability to serve, with their names not proposed by the Nominating Committee.  

**National Council Membership:**  
In addition to the newly elected Council members, all former National Chairmen and all State Chairmen are members of the National Council ex officio.  

**Registration Fee:**  
May 1, as Convention Chairman, thank all of you for the splendid cooperation during this our first Convention by mail. The registration fee, which many of you sent, has been most helpful.  

**Committee Acknowledgments:**  
We are indebted to the members of the Nominating Committee and the Elections Committee, and to the Resolutions Committee, for their many hours of laborious work during the hot weather. It is largely due to their efforts that the undertaking of a Convention by Mail has been brought to a successful conclusion.  

**Transition of Leadership:**  
There are no words adequate to express our regret over losing Alice Paul as our National Chairman. Her great leadership has made it possible for the Amendment to reach its present position of strength in Congress. Miss Paul will still be helping us, as she is now planning to devote her time to the World Woman’s Party. Since the National Woman’s Party is the United States branch of that organization, Miss Paul will be working closely with us.  

**Welcome to New Officers:**  
On behalf of all of us, I want to give a very warm welcome to our new Chairwoman, Anita Pollitzer, and to all of our new officers and Council members. Miss Pollitzer’s devoted service and years of accomplishment for our cause are well known to you, as are her exceptional qualifications for the heavy duties she is about to undertake. We are all grateful to her. Let us get strongly behind her and the other officers and Council members so that we may achieve success for Equal Rights at an early date.  

**Publication of Resolutions:**  
The Resolutions, as adopted by the Convention, will be printed in the next issue of *Equal Rights*.  

**Future Council Meeting:**  
The new Council will meet at the call of Miss Pollitzer.  

**Closed:**  
Cordially yours,  
[signed]  
**Cecil Norton Bray**  
Convention Chairman, National Woman’s Party
