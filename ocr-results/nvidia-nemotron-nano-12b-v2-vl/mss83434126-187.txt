Cambridgeport-Bena  
July 30th, 1871  

To the Officers of B.m.e.  
Messrs: No 907  

Gentlemen: In objecting to your resolution of July 15th, 1871, the undersigned (Tim. Conf.) repealed July eats the following Word: Eliz.:— Treasurer Accl.:— Expel the harmonies for the next months ending June 30th, 1871. Expenses to date: Jan 15th, $15.10. Col. in hands of - cook, Ry. 20.4-$15.3.75. Received, in Jan $17.45. Feb: $ 5.00. March 10, $11.75. April 7, $3.62. May 6, $6.15. June 7, $8.14. Total Net $13.13. Total Expenses $3.75. Balance on hand to be carried to next $13.69.
