Travel by Heine.
Charles G. Leland, Esq., the talented
translator of the Pictures of Travel is
already known to our literary public by his varied
contributions to literature. It is indeed
only possible for one who is himself
inspired with poetry, to apprehend the spirit
of a poet, and to render it worthily in another
tongue.
Heine, as the founder of a new school of
poetry, and as the most brilliant star in the
firmament of living German writers,
deserves the first place in this series.
Should his Pictures of Travel prove acceptable
to the American public, both writer and
publisher will gratefully
contribute their efforts by translating all
the remaining works of this most remarkable
newest species of poetry.
A single number of the Pictures of Travel
