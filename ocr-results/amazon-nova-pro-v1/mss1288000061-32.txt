Georgetown, D. C.
3rd: Aug 2 al/857

Dear Papav Mamma:

Yesterday we went on a pleasant pique to F.tes Bar. It was an all-day trip. We had an early breakfast, & then the Holbrooks came over, & we all piled into a little steam lunch, chartered for the occasion. It carried 25 + there were 25 of us. The engine divides it in the middle, with wood heaped up on each side (fancy a stream engine run by wood) + the back
