Dictated
Nov. 20.31

My dear Mrs. Catt:
Am in
the General Hospital, Osteopathic
Unit #780 -at Los Angeles, at present
with a fractured leg.
Have been here 8 weeks today.
Hope soon to be released from
this terrible house of torture, a
cast, so that I can return
to my little apartment at
60g Fairview Ave., SW Pasadena
Calif. Many friends are wonderful
to my in ways
of comfort.  Close friend Mrs. Lois Jenthills 1718
OPP St., SW Pasadena -who has
all of me in charge.
Cordially
[Harrietta g Moure
Sff]
