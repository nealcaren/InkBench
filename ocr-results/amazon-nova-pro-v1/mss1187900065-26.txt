# The American Society
# For the
## Prevention of Cruelty to Animals
Hendquarters: Fourth Ave.. col. 22d St.

New York, Jun 28, 1885

Hon: Frederick Douglas
House of Representatives, Mashington D.C.

Dear Sir,

I yesterday deposited with the Secrogatte of Hudson Co- Newfusey, the will of the late Otella as Sing, along with a letter from me desiring to serve as Executor, Copies of which I forward to you with this.

You will please take such action in the matter of Supplying a Substitute, as you deem proper, and believe me.

Yours with great respect

Henry Bergh
