1862 June 17th - Occupation of Cumberland Gap, Tenn. The federals
under Gen'l Morgan, advanced towards this place for the purpose
of expelling the rebels Gen'l Stevenson - who occupy the
gap with 3,000 men and will no doubt defend it same, This
gap is a cleft in the Cumberland mountains, and is so deep
and narrow that there is through the gap for only a single
railway, It was expected that the rebels would defend
its possession with the utmost tenacity. No such result
followed, When the rebels were informed of the approach
of the federals, they precipitately evacuated the place.
