and timely an address. I wish
you could send a copy to
our common friend Cyrus

of Ohio, of whom a few
are left and please put some
mark on it by which he will know
from whom he receives it. Do you
never come west in these days?
Should you come to this City
remember you have a Carte Blanche,
which if you follow it,
