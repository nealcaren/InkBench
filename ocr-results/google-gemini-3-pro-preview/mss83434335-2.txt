Hartford, Octob. 10th 1782.

RECEIVED, of Pay-Table-Committee, their Order
on the Treasurer, of this State, to secure the Payment
of Thirteen pounds and Seven pence -
it being the Balances due to [me] Exeter Cyrus, on the first Day of January 1780 
last, as stated by the Committees of the State and of the
Army... In behalf of Exeter Cyrus
£.13..0..7 -      Chas Fanning Lieut

Vouchers in Elias Lamberts Rect of this date
