Wisconsin looks
delightfully fresh, &
clean, & green, & rolling.
& I begin to feel better
at once.
I will write my
dear little neglected Mannie
often now & - with love to
all the house, I send
thee my heart.
Anna

I was more than glad to
get my Mannie's scratch, written
by her poor blessed little claw, at Aurora.
