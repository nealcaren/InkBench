Newport, R.I. Sunday Aug 21 1881

My Dear Mr Douglass
I have just found
on the reading table the Report
of last years Hampton Work.
Glancing over it an impulse
urges me to speak of the
impressions received -
To voice them on this Sunday
has become a habit
