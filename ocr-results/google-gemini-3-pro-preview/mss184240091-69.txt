The Metcalf's are in Texas enjoying roses and

is perfect and they are perfectly well -
The Beecher abomination occupies the public
here as in New York. Will it ever come
to an end. The jury will not agree of course

comes on the witness stand he will hold
the key to the position. What an outrage
for Beecher's friends to try to blacken
Mrs Moulton's character - I am glad her husband
is so loyal and true. Write me sweet
