Jan, 22 1874.
Washington D C
My Dear father.
I have received your
kind letter and was
very glad to hear
from you. but I am so
sorry that you feal.
so. a baut going to
Rochester . I hope it will
be all Right thair .
when you go thair . I
shill take good care of your
letters and not think of
dis troying them in anny way,
me have had a few very
Cold days but we have
[?] very pleasant weather.
