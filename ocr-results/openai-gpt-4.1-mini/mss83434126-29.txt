March 30th 1866 - Issued to Bvt. Major. A.H. Buffington Commanding U.S. Arsenal at Baton Rouge, La. the following Ordnance Stores, as per Inventory and Inspection Report of Bvt. Major Gen. J.T.E. Sherman dated March 21st 1866.

8 Eight Springfield Rifled Muskets Cal 58.  
7 Seven Cartridge Boxes Cal. 58.  
7 Seven Bayonet Scabbards  
8 Eight Gun Slings  
9 Nine Screw drivers and some wrenches  
10 Ten Uspens  
3 Three Tumblers and land spring punches  
5 Five Waist Belts (Pots)  

                                 Memorable  

Sent the Invoice through the Post Office Dept. and Received my receipts April 12th bearing date April 4th 1866.  

Stephen S. Newman  
1st Lieut. Co. "B" 81st. U.S.C. Inf.  
Commanding Company K
