No. 1.

Quartermaster General's Office,
PROPERTY DIVISION,
Washington, D. C., June 27, 1866.

To Lt. G. P. Harper
      D. P. Macomb
          La

Sir:

Your Return of Quartermaster's Stores as Lt. and Mst. Coats, for the month of Dec 1865, has been received, and, when examined, will be sent to the Third Auditor of the Treasury for settlement, of which you will be duly notified.

By order of the Quartermaster General:
Very respectfully,
Your obedient servant,

Orry C. Black,
Col. Q.M. Dept. Col. Brig. Genl.
