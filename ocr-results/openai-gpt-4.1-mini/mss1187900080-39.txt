Marion Arkansas  
September 21st 1888  

Hon Fredrick Douglass  
Washington S.C.  

Dear Sir  
I am an Ex union soldier and have endeavored to be a consistent Republican ever since the organization of that party. I have resided in the South a greater portion of the time since the civil war and in my humble sphere in life have been a friend to the colored race.
