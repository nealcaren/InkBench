Concord June 24 1885
My dear Friend:

I have sent you various portions of my book in a form not having duplicates proofs enough to give you what I would like, just yet. I shall soon have advance sheets of the whole 650 pages and will send you an early copy whenever you desire it. As I get back proof from others to whom I have sent them, I may send you more.
