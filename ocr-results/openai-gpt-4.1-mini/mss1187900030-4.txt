It has been beautiful weather ever since you left and I do feel think we will have pleasant small but it will be keen and sharp. Elizabeth is making preparations to read her lecture next Monday night and leave on tuesday or wednesday of next week.  I believe she has not made her apointments yet - but I think to day or tomorrow Levie will attend to it.  Safe writing in the school-room and the few who have brought their dinner's are as full of glee as possible & I hope you will have as comfortable a time as possible and that you will not return home sick.  Old Brown the Governor of Missouri has a reward of $3,000 offered for his capture and Hamilton has given himself up.
Mother wishes me to give her love and enclosed you will find a note from Elizabeth and perhaps Annie will send a few lines.

Your Affec. Daughter
Rosetta Douglas
