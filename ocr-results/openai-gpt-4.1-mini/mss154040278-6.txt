Jean fed us. Olds attended the League. W. Volers and I dodged around among them and along worn out night bought a blue dress and received a caller.

I'm hoping the war will end in the South East and that the Greeks in their white pretty music chase the Nazis home where at least one will commit suicide.

When we meet again I am sure he to clear as you are. Like Mary Woolstonecraft you will be "A shining woman."

Looking Forward
Carrie C. East

Tuesday 1941 April 8,
