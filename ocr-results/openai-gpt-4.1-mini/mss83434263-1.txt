DISCHARGE.

This is to certify, That No. 503. Spencer Parr a Landsman
has this day been discharged from the U.S. Steamer "Birmville"
and from the naval service.

Dated this 14th May 1865.

APPROVED: J.R. Conmullany [or Connullany]
