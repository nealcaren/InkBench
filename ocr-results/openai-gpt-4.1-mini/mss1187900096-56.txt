Nantucket, July 18, 1891

My dear friends: -

We, (Charlotte and I,) congratulate you upon your safe arrival back to Washington.
Charlotte is sojourning in Nantucket for a few weeks. Mr. Mann remains at New Haven. Mrs. Mc comes frequently to pass the day with me and I return the visit. She suggested my writing to you. Writing has become quite burdensome to her she is so feeble - seemingly like hustle down. But that she has improved since her arrival on the Island is clearly evident.
