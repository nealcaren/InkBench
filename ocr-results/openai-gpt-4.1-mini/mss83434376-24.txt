Hd 2d 8th Regt U.S.C.T.
Near Chaplin Farm Va
January 17th 1865

Bailey, Romanzo C.
Capt 8th U.S.C.T.
Corpl. [Corps?] of Regt.

Requests a leave of absence for twenty (26) days for the purpose of visiting Litigo Tenn. on business


Hd 2d 8th Regt U.S.C.T.
Near Chaplin Farm Va
January 17th 1865

Respectfully forwarded.
I certify that the within application will not make the absences on leave or furlough from this Regt in exec of one twentith (20) of its effective strength; that I do not belong to one years Regt. that I have not returned from a leave of absence since the 1st April 1864; that I have been in the service more than one year; that my term of service does not expire before the 1st of May 1865; that I have never been tried & convicted of any offen by a court martial; that I am a member of a Regt raised in the State of Pennsylvania.
The number of officers present in my Regt is 20
Number Absent is 12
The number of enlisted men present in my command is 538
Number absent is  254

R.C. Bailey
Capt. 8th U.S.C.T.
Corpl. of Regt.
