Honorable
James G Blaine

Sir: I have the honor to ask the favor of a leave of absence from Post au Prince, beginning on the 20th of June, excluding the time required to reach Washington and the same to again return to Port au Prince, if the latter shall be deemed my duty.

I am required to state in this request for leave of absence, the the number of days I was absent from my post, during the preceding year. I respectfully comply with this instruction or rule, by referring you to my dispatch No. 95 dated Dec 18th 1890 in which it is stated: "I left Port au Prince on the 19th July and on the 30th of the same month, arrived at my home in the District of Columbia.
