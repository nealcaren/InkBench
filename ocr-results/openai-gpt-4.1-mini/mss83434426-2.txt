$400. On or before the 15th day of January 1866 we promise to pay Jane E. Stearing her heirs or assigns the just and full sum of four hundred dollars in current funds. It being for the hire of a negro slave named Turner which negro is to be clothed in the usual manner of hirings with the exception of a blanket (It is understood by the parties that should the said negro's services be taken either by the enemy or the Confederate authorities, the amt of the time lost shall be deducted from the hire.) For the true payment of which sum we bind ourselves jointly & severally our joint & several heirs, witness our hands & seals this 4th day of January 1865.  H.L.R. Williams  Seal
