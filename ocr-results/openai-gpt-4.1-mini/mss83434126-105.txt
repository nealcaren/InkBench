1853 July 8th American expedition under Comr Perry arrive at Japan.

1853 July 14th Exhibition of the Industry of all Nations, opened at New York City.

1853 Sept 6th Remaining portion of Table Rock at the Falls of Niagara breaks off.

1853 Nov 9th Inauguration of the Washington Aqueduct.

1853 Dec 16th Genl Santa Anna elected dictator of Mexico for life. He had formerly been President from 1833 to 1845.

1854 March 5th A Treaty of Commerce concluded between the United States and Japan.

1854 April 20th Miss Dize's bill ameliorating the condition of the indigent insane - vetoed.

1854 April 28th The President issued a proclamation, announcing the neutrality of the United States in the Eastern question.

1854 June 7th A reciprocity treaty between the United States and Great Britain was concluded at Washington respecting Reciprocal tariffing international trade &c.
