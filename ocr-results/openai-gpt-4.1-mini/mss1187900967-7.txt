You would find me in very pleasant surrounding[s] not quite such as I have on Cedar Hill - yet very well adapted to this climate and country.  I note what you say of young Cooke.  I am sorry to learn that he has made so little progress.  feel ever more sorry still to know that he served Annie such a mean trick - she did a good deal for him.  That is perhaps the trouble.  Had she done lest [less] she would have received more thanks.  Gratitude is not a very strong feature of the Colored race.
